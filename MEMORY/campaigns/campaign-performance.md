**Dates:** 2026-06-01 → 2026-06-12 (planned through Jun 30; closed early and superseded)

| Metric | Target | Actual | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| DM "RESTART" initiations | 15+ | NOT TRACKED (qualitative: DMs arriving, but from free-trial posts + member referrals — not the campaign's cold-content funnel) | |

| Enrollments | 2–3 | 0 via this funnel | |
| Follower growth | +100 | NOT TRACKED | |
| Week 1 content shipped | 7 posts + 21 stories | Week 1 batch produced and launched; Weeks 2–4 execution not confirmed | |

### What worked
Week 1 production system (batching, 3+1 skeleton) proved operationally feasible. DMs are arriving — just through referral/free-trial-style entry points, not through the cold→call pipeline this campaign assumed.

### What didn't
The core funnel assumption: cold content → DM "RESTART" → discovery call → 90-Day. Zero call requests. The discovery call as a public-facing ask is too high-friction for an audience at this trust stage. The campaign also pushed the 90-Day as primary offer to an audience whose proven entry behavior is low-friction (trials, referrals).

### Decision
Close early. Supersede with **2026-06 DM-First Pipeline** (folder: 2026-06-dm-first-pipeline-restart-ph): keep the 3+1 daily system, demote the discovery call to a step *inside* the DM conversation, set OMTM = DM volume, attach a referral-vs-problem-led content experiment (EXP-2026-06-1). Gate 3 learnings routed to strategy-state.md (segment + channel cards updated 2026-06-12).
