- Price tier: FIT45 / On-Ramp → URBN FIT → HYBRID/PFT, PT, small-group

- Promise: "Stop guessing. Follow a system." — The Restart Fitness System

- Scored 2026-06-12: ACCURATE. Role (transformation/results) and promise confirmed by cohort data. One caveat — entry behavior is referral-warmed and low-friction, not high-intent cold pain; campaigns should treat "transformation brand" as the destination, not the door.

## Segment cards

Status is per card. Restart cards validated/retired 2026-06-12 against real cohort + campaign data; URBN cards still DRAFT/partial — validate against real trial data before locking.

### Restart — "The Referred Busy Professional" *(replaces "The Restarter" — updated from real 6FIX cohort data)*
- Who they actually are: professionals with constrained schedules (shift work, lab hours, irregular days) who came in through a trusted referral, not cold traffic
- Pain: no system that fits their actual life — time is real, not an excuse
- Buying context: a colleague or friend already enrolled; low friction because trust is pre-built
- Message: a defined program that fits around your schedule — not another thing that requires restructuring your life
- Offer: 6FIX entry → Phase 2 / 90-Day upgrade
- Objections: schedule fit · "will I keep up with the group"
- Channel: referral-first; IG as secondary warm-up
- Expected LTV: high (upgrade ladder — Phase 2 already proven)
- Evidence: Phase 1 cohort (Katrina/Arianna/Jen/Fame) — all med techs, all referred via one connection, all completed
- Status: VALIDATED (first cohort)

### Restart — "The Event Deadline" *(HYPOTHETICAL — retire until after 6FIX public launch)*
- Pain: visible-results pressure with a hard date (wedding, reunion, trip)
- Buying context: 8–16 weeks out, urgency spike
- Message: a defined 90-day system fits your timeline — guessing doesn't
- Offer: 90-Day direct
- Objections: "is 90 days enough" · "can I do it online"
- Channel: IG, discovery call CTA
- Expected LTV: mid (event-bound, retention play needed after)
- Status: RETIRED FOR NOW — no DMs, no marketing effort, no public launch yet. 6FIX Phase 1 has never been publicly launched. Discovery call funnel not active. Revisit this segment after the 6FIX public launch builds a base and discovery calls are running. Do not build campaigns for this segment yet.

### URBN — "The Hyrox-Influenced Gym Returnee"
- Who they actually are: people with some gym experience who've lapsed or plateaued; drawn to functional fitness (Hyrox culture is the market signal — structured, performance-oriented, not just aesthetics)
- Pain: training alone or on old programs isn't giving them what they want; looking for coached structure with a performance edge
- Buying context: referred by a current member; saw URBN Hybrid content on IG; Hyrox as the category influencer making functional fitness desirable
- Message: Built to Move Better — coached functional training, not just workouts
- Offer: Free trial → URBN HYBRID / URBN FIT
- Objections: "is this too intense" · "how is this different from my old gym"
- Channel: IG + member referrals (primary); Hyrox-adjacent content as warm-up
- Expected LTV: high (membership + upgrade to HYBRID/PFT)
- Evidence: free trial bookers tried Hybrid; have gym backgrounds; came through referrals
- Status: PARTIALLY VALIDATED (profile and entry path confirmed; trial→membership conversion rate still pending)

### URBN — "The Desk-Broken Professional"
- Pain: body feels stiff, weak, older than it should; sits all day
- Buying context: pain signals, energy crashes, "I used to be athletic"
- Message: Built to Move Better — train function, feel the difference in daily life
- Offer: URBN FIT / HYBRID
- Objections: time, "is this for athletes only"
- Channel: FB local, word of mouth
- Expected LTV: high
- Status: DRAFT — zero validated leads match this profile yet (all validated trial bookers are gym-experienced/Hyrox-influenced). Do not build campaigns on this card until trial data shows desk-professional/beginner bookings.

## Job stories

⚠️ DRAFTS except 6FIX (evidence-fed from first cohort, 2026-06-12) — replace remaining stories with real client phrasing from discovery calls and check-ins as it accumulates.

- **6FIX:** When my schedule is tight and every program I've tried hasn't fit my actual life, help me follow a six-week system with structure and check-ins, so I can actually be consistent without rearranging everything.
  Forces — push: schedule constraints, failed solo attempts · pull: short duration, group accountability, trusted referral · anxiety: "will I keep up" · habit: skipping when no one notices.
  *(Updated from first cohort — med tech professionals, shift schedules, referral entry. Shame/failure narrative replaced with schedule-fit + accountability narrative.)*
- **90-Day Coaching System:** When I have a real reason to change and I'm done experimenting, help me execute a complete system with coaching and accountability, so I can get visible results without guessing.
  Forces — push: deadline/pain · pull: done-for-you structure · anxiety: cost, online format · habit: program-hopping.
- **URBN membership:** When training alone hasn't worked and home workouts feel fake, help me show up to a coached session where the thinking is done for me, so I can be consistent without willpower.
  Forces — push: failed solo attempts · pull: community + coach presence · anxiety: gym intimidation · habit: skipping when no one notices.
