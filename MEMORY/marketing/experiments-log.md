## Active experiments

### EXP-2026-06-1 — Proof/referral posts vs. problem-led posts (DM driver test)
- **Brand:** Restart Fitness PH
- **Campaign:** 2026-06 DM-First Pipeline
- **Layer:** Message (STP/JTBD — which angle activates the validated referred-professional segment)
- **Hypothesis:** Proof/referral-style posts (completer results, "tag the friend who needs this") will drive ≥2× more DM "RESTART" initiations than problem-led posts, because the proven channel behavior is referral/social-proof entry, not pain-recognition entry.
- **Metric:** DM "RESTART" initiations, tagged by source post angle (logged daily in DM flow)
- **Baseline:** None — Weeks 1–2 (Jun 16–29) establish it
- **Decision rule (pre-committed):** At Jul 13 close: if one angle drove ≥2× the DMs of the other (min. 6 total DMs), adopt it as the primary post mix (≈70/30). If total DMs < 6 across both angles, the angle is NOT the bottleneck — distribution/reach is; next experiment targets reach (format/timing), not message.
- **Duration:** Jun 16 – Jul 13 (4 weeks)
- **Status:** RUNNING
