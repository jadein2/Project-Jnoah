## Caption

```
Most people quit at week 4. Not because they're lazy — because the plan ran out.

Here's what actually happens:

Weeks 1–2: Everything is new. Motivation is high. The soreness feels productive.

Week 3: The novelty fades. Results slow down. Life starts getting in the way.

Week 4: The plan stops making sense. No one is checking in. No adjustments. So they stop.

Then they call themselves undisciplined.

But here is the thing — most programs are not built for week 4. They are built for week 1. No accountability loop. No structure for the hard weeks. No coach tracking whether the plan still fits your life.

The Restart Fitness System is built for the long weeks. Not just the motivated ones.

If you have been through this cycle — starting strong, fading out, starting again — the problem is not your effort. It is the structure around it.

DM "RESTART" and let's walk through what a real coaching system looks like for where you are now.
```

**Character count:** ~880 characters (within 800–1500 target)
**Hook (first 125 chars):** "Most people quit at week 4. Not because they're lazy — because the plan ran out." ✅
