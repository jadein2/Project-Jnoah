## Caption

Six months of hard training and the mirror looks the same. Sound familiar?

Here's the part most people get wrong: when progress stalls, the answer usually isn't *harder*. It's *structured*. Effort with no plan behind it just makes you tired — it doesn't make you better.

This breaks down why it happens and the two steps that turn effort into actual progress.

Save it for the next time you're tempted to just add another training day.

DM "RESTART" if you want a coached system instead of another guess.

## Design notes (per slide)

**Global:** Background `#1A1A18` charcoal. Headlines Cormorant Garamond Italic, off-white `#F5F5F0`. Body DM Sans 300. One orange `#E06D22` emphasis word max per slide where noted. No-Line Rule — separate with spacing, not dividers. 64px safe margin. No gradients.

- **Slide 1 (Hook):** Largest type in the set. "nowhere" in orange. Heavy negative space above and below. One focal point — text only, or text over a moody gym shot at low opacity.
- **Slide 2:** Two lines, generous gap between them. Charcoal, off-white text. Optional cinematic photo (person resting between sets, unsmiling).
- **Slide 3:** "plan" is the key word — can bold in DM Sans 500 or set in Cormorant italic for emphasis. No orange yet.
- **Slide 4:** Three short stacked lines. Build the frustration visually — tight line spacing.
- **Slide 5:** The turn. More breathing room than slide 4 on purpose — the shift should feel calmer. "direction" emphasised.
- **Slide 6:** Practical. DM Sans 400 for the steps. Keep it scannable.
- **Slide 7:** Same visual system as slide 6 for continuity (they're a pair).
- **Slide 8:** Authority slide — this is the coach speaking. Consider gold `#C9A84C` accent here only if framing as proof/authority; otherwise stay off-white. Do NOT combine gold + orange.
- **Slide 9:** Two-line contrast. The arrow/flow is conceptual — express with line breaks and spacing, not a literal arrow graphic.
- **Slide 10 (CTA):** "Stop guessing. Follow a system." in Cormorant italic. DM "RESTART" line in DM Sans 500, orange on the word RESTART or on a small CTA element. Brand mark top center. One CTA only.
