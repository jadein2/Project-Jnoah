WEEK 1 BRIEFING
Phase 1 — Foundation and Movement Assessment

Week 1 is not about intensity. It is about establishing movement quality, setting the baseline, and building the habit of showing up. Loads stay light. RPE stays conversational. The goal is to leave every session feeling capable, not wrecked.

Session 1 — Lower Body Push + Pull / Short Conditioning
Session 2 — Upper Body Push + Pull / Aerobic Base
Session 3 — Full Body / Longer Conditioning Piece

------------------------------------------------------------

SESSION 1 — LOWER BODY FOUNDATION
Week 1 | Squat + Hinge Pattern | Full Gym

Stimulus: Strength Primer + Short Aerobic Conditioning
Intensity: Low–Moderate
CNS Load: Low–Medium
RPE Target: 6–7
Recovery Demand: Light–Moderate
Duration: ~55 minutes
Session Summary: Establish squat and hinge baselines at controlled load. Short conditioning to close. No redline.

------------------------------------------------------------

WARM-UP — 10 minutes

- Bike or treadmill easy — 3 min
- Leg swings forward and back — 10 each leg
- Hip circles — 10 each direction
- Bodyweight squat — 10 reps slow
- Romanian deadlift with empty bar — 10 reps
- Glute bridge — 10 reps hold 2 sec at top

------------------------------------------------------------

STRENGTH BLOCK A — Squat + Hinge | 4 Rounds | 18 min
Superset format. Perform A1 then immediately A2. Rest 90 sec after A2 before returning to A1.

A1 — Goblet Squat 10 x 4 sets @RPE 6
Feet shoulder-width. Chest tall. Knee tracks over second toe. Full depth — box if needed.

A2 — Romanian Deadlift 10 x 4 sets @RPE 6
Soft knee. Hinge at hip. Bar or dumbbells stay close to leg. Feel the hamstring load at the bottom.

------------------------------------------------------------

STRENGTH BLOCK B — Single Leg + Posterior Chain | 3 Rounds | 12 min
Superset format. Perform B1 then immediately B2. Rest 75 sec after B2.

B1 — Reverse Lunge 8 ea x 3 sets @RPE 6
Bodyweight or light dumbbells. Back knee touches or approaches the floor. Front shin stays vertical.

B2 — Glute Bridge (barbell or bodyweight) 12 x 3 sets @RPE 5–6
Drive through heel. Full hip extension at top. Hold 1 sec. Do not hyperextend the lower back.

------------------------------------------------------------

CONDITIONING — ~10 minutes | Short Aerobic
For Time — 10 min cap

400m Run or 500m Row
15 Goblet Squat (light — same weight as Block A or lighter)
10 Cal Bike
15 Goblet Squat

Target: Finish under 9 min. Steady pace — not a sprint.
Scaling: Walk 200m + Row 250m + reduce goblet squat to 10 reps if needed.

------------------------------------------------------------

COOL-DOWN — 5 minutes

- Couch stretch — 60 sec each side
- Pigeon pose or figure-four stretch — 60 sec each side
- Child's pose — 60 sec
- Nasal breathing — 2 min

SESSION DEBRIEF
Log your RPE for both strength blocks and the conditioning piece separately. Note any movement that felt off — squat depth, hinge, lunge balance. Next session, we build on what felt solid here. If RPE on A1/A2 felt below 5, we add load next week.
