# JOZEN — CYCLE 4 / WEEK 1
Hotel Gym | Strength + Hypertrophy + Conditioning | 3 Sessions
Format: Warm Up → Strength → Core / Accessory → Metcon → Mobility | 60 min per session

Programming rules: RPE 7–8 all strength work · No Olympic lifts · DU default (scale: Singles) · Rotate squat / hinge / push / pull / single-leg / core across sessions

---

## Week 1

---

### Session 1 — Lower Body (Squat + Hinge)

Warm up
3 Sets
10 Air Squats
10 Good Mornings @light DB or bands
30 Double-Unders (scale: 60 Singles)

STRENGTH
4 Sets @7–8 RPE
8 Smith Machine Back Squat
6 Hex Bar Deadlift

CORE / ACCESSORY
3 Sets
12 Hip Thrust @2xDB (bench-supported)
10 DB Step-Up @moderate (each leg, bench height)
15 Sit-Ups

METCON
AMRAP 12:00

200m Treadmill Run
12 KB Swings @moderate
10 DB Goblet Squat @same KB

*Consistent pace each round. KB Swings are hip-driven — not a squat. Log rounds + reps.

MOBILITY
2 Sets
45s Quad/Hip Flexor Stretch (each side)
45s Hamstring Stretch
45s Calf/Ankle Stretch (each side)

---

### Session 2 — Upper Body (Push + Pull)

Warm up
3 Sets
10 Inchworms
10 Band Pull-Aparts
30 Double-Unders (scale: 60 Singles)

STRENGTH
4 Sets @7–8 RPE
8 DB Bench Press
10 Single-Arm Cable Row (each arm)

CORE / ACCESSORY
3 Sets
10 Seated DB Press @7 RPE
12 Cable Face Pull
12 EZ Bar Curl
15 V-Ups

METCON
For Time (10:00 cap)

50 Double-Unders (scale: 100 Singles)
15 DB Push Press @moderate
10 Burpees
50 Double-Unders (scale: 100 Singles)

*Move through the DU in sets if needed. Push Press — legs drive the bar. Log your time.

MOBILITY
2 Sets
45s Chest Stretch (doorframe or cable post)
45s Lat Stretch (overhead, each side)
45s Thoracic Extension over bench

---

### Session 3 — Full Body (Single-Leg + Core)

Warm up
3 Sets
10 Air Squats
10 Push-Ups
30 Double-Unders (scale: 60 Singles)

STRENGTH
4 Sets @7–8 RPE
8 Bulgarian Split Squat @2xDB (each leg)
10 Incline DB Bench Press

CORE / ACCESSORY
3 Sets
12 Cable Row
12 Leg Extension @7 RPE
10 Hanging Knee Raise (sub: Dead Bug x 10 each side if no hang option)
15 Russian Twist @light

METCON
EMOM 12:00

Min 1: 15 DB Thrusters @light-moderate
Min 2: 12 V-Ups
Min 3: 30 Double-Unders (scale: 60 Singles)

*Thrusters — full squat drives the bar, arms just finish. Keep V-Ups controlled — no swinging. If DU takes the full minute, reduce to 20.

MOBILITY
2 Sets
45s Hip Flexor Stretch (each side)
45s Hamstring Stretch
45s Glute Stretch — Figure 4 (each side)
