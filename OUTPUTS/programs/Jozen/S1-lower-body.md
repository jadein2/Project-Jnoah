*Feet slightly forward of the bar. Sit back and down. Drive knees out.

A2 — Romanian Deadlift (Barbell) 10 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Hinge from the hips. Feel the hamstring stretch. Bar stays close to the legs.

B1 — DB Walking Lunge 12 x 3 sets (each leg) @7 RPE
B2 — Barbell Glute Bridge 15 x 3 sets @8 RPE
B3 — Lat Pulldown 12 x 3 sets @7 RPE
