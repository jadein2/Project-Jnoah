STRENGTH BLOCK A — POWER + SQUAT | 5 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 2 min after A2.

A1 — Hang Power Clean 4 x 5 sets @7–8 RPE
*Explosive hip extension. Pull tall, drop under. Receive with soft knees — this is your landing position for basketball. Record load used.

A2 — Box Jump 5 x 5 sets @max effort (24–30in)
*Full hip extension at the top. Step down — never jump down. Reset fully between reps.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — SQUAT + POSTERIOR CHAIN | 4 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Back Squat 6 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Establish working load this session — record it. Full depth. Drive knees out on the way up.

B2 — Romanian Deadlift 8 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*3-count eccentric. Full hamstring stretch at the bottom.

STRENGTH BLOCK C — ACCESSORY | 3 Rounds
Perform C1 → C2. Rest 60 sec after C2.

C1 — Bulgarian Split Squat 8 x 3 sets (each leg) @2xDB @7 RPE
*Single-leg strength is critical for court performance.

C2 — Hip Abductor Machine 15 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
