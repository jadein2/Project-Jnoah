STRENGTH BLOCK A — SQUAT + LATERAL POWER | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Front Squat 5 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Elbows high throughout. Full depth. Drive knees out. Different squat pattern from C1/PO2 — feel the anterior chain work.

A2 — Lateral Box Jump 4 x 4 sets @max effort (24in — jump sideways onto box)
*Full hip extension at landing. Step down. This is your court cut direction — land controlled.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — HINGE + SINGLE LEG | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Sumo Deadlift 6 x 3 sets @8 RPE
*Wide stance, toes out. Sit into the hips before pulling. Bar close to shins. Record load.

B2 — Single-Leg Romanian Deadlift 8 x 3 sets (each leg) @1xDB @7 RPE
*Hinge at the hip. Control the balance — this is court-stability work. Touch and go or dead stop, your choice.

STRENGTH BLOCK C — HIP + ACCESSORY | 3 Rounds
Perform C1 → C2. Rest 60 sec after C2.

C1 — Banded Side Steps 20 x 3 sets (each direction)
*Tension on the band throughout. Controlled tempo. Do not let knees cave.

C2 — GHD Back Extension 10 x 3 sets @bodyweight
*Controlled descent. Full extension at the top without hyperextending. Progress to holding a plate when bodyweight is easy.
