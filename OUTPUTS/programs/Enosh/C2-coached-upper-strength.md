STRENGTH BLOCK A — HORIZONTAL PRESS + PULL | 4 Rounds

*Hinge at 45. Drive elbows back. Bar to lower chest. No hip rise.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — VERTICAL PULL + PUSH | 4 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Weighted Pull-Up 5 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Full dead hang to chin above bar. Add load when bodyweight feels easy. Upper body pulling strength = basketball contact strength.

B2 — Push Press 8 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Drive with the legs. Full lockout overhead. Control the descent.
