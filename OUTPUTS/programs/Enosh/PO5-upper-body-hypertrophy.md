STRENGTH BLOCK A — INCLINE PRESS + CABLE ROW | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Incline DB Press 10 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Elbows at 45 degrees — not flared wide. Full stretch at the bottom. Press to the top without locking out fully. Different angle from C2/PO3 flat pressing — feel the upper chest.

A2 — Seated Cable Row 10 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Chest to the pad. Drive elbows back past the body. Pause at the end position. Controlled return — don't let the stack yank you forward.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — VERTICAL PULL + SHOULDER | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Lat Pulldown 8 x 3 sets @8 RPE
*Wide overhand grip. Pull bar to upper chest. Lean back slightly — not a full recline. Full extension at the top.

B2 — Cable Face Pull 15 x 3 sets
*Rope attachment at face height. Pull to forehead. External rotation at the end — thumbs back. Slow tempo. This is shoulder health work, not a grind.

STRENGTH BLOCK C — SHOULDER + ARMS | 3 Rounds
Perform C1 → C2 → C3. Rest 60 sec after C3.

C1 — DB Lateral Raise 12 x 3 sets (each arm)
*Lead with the elbow. Stop at shoulder height. No body English.

C2 — Hammer Curl 12 x 3 sets (each arm)
*Neutral grip. Controlled tempo both ways. Full extension at the bottom.

C3 — Ab Wheel Rollout 10 x 3 sets
*Knees on floor if needed. Hollow body position throughout. Don't let the low back arch.
