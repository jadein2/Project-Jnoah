### Day 1 - Program Only | Lower Power + Running Conditioning

Warm up
5:00 Easy Jog

3 Sets
10 Kang Squats
10 Greatest Stretch (each side)
10 Hip Bridges
5 Broad Jumps @50% effort

Specific Warm-Up
1 Set
5 Goblet Squat @light
5 Romanian Deadlift @light

STRENGTH BLOCK A - POWER + SQUAT | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 - Box Jump Step-Down 5 x 4 sets @moderate height
*Reset every rep. Land soft. Quality over speed.

A2 - Goblet Squat 10 x 4 sets @7-8 RPE
*Full depth. Drive through the full foot.

STRENGTH BLOCK B - HINGE + HIP STABILITY | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 60 sec after B2.

B1 - DB Romanian Deadlift 10 x 3 sets @7-8 RPE
*Slow 2-count descent. Hamstring stretch at the bottom.

B2 - Banded Side Steps 15 x 3 sets (each direction)
*Stay low and keep band tension.

CONDITIONING
Running Intervals

6 Rounds
200m Run @hard but repeatable
Walk 1:00 between rounds

*All six runs should stay within the same pace range. If round 4 falls apart, reduce pace and finish clean.

### Day 2 - Program Only | Upper Strength + Physique

Warm up
5:00 Row or Easy Bike

3 Sets
10 Banded Pull-Aparts
10 Scapular Push-Ups
10 T-Raises @light
5 Inch Worms

Specific Warm-Up
1 Set
10 DB Bench Press @light
10 Lat Pulldown @light

STRENGTH BLOCK A - PUSH + PULL | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 - DB Bench Press 8 x 4 sets @7-8 RPE
*Control the descent. Press strong without bouncing.

A2 - Lat Pulldown 10 x 4 sets @7-8 RPE
*Pull to upper chest. Control the return.

STRENGTH BLOCK B - UPPER BACK + SHOULDERS | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2 then B3. Rest 60 sec after B3.

B1 - Chest-Supported DB Row 10 x 3 sets (each arm) @7-8 RPE
*Lead with the elbow. No shrug.

B2 - Seated DB Shoulder Press 10 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Ribs down. Full lockout.

B3 - Cable Face Pull 15 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Pull to forehead. Pause at the end range.

### Day 3 - Program Only | Full Body + Running Conditioning

Warm up
5:00 Easy Jog

3 Sets
10 Air Squats
10 Walking Lunges (total reps)
10 Banded Pull-Aparts
5 Inch Worms

Specific Warm-Up
1 Set
5 DB Hang Power Clean @light
5 Push Press @light

STRENGTH BLOCK A - POWER + PRESS | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 - DB Hang Power Clean 6 x 4 sets @7 RPE
*Explosive hips, fast elbows. Do not turn this into a curl.

A2 - DB Push Press 8 x 4 sets @7-8 RPE
*Leg drive first. Lock out strong.

STRENGTH BLOCK B - SINGLE LEG + PULL | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 60 sec after B2.

B1 - Bulgarian Split Squat 8 x 3 sets (each leg) @7-8 RPE
*Controlled descent. Drive through the front foot.

B2 - Pull-Up or Assisted Pull-Up 6 x 3 sets @7-8 RPE
*Full hang. Chin clears the bar.

CONDITIONING
