STRENGTH BLOCK A — POWER + SQUAT | 3 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Hang Power Clean 4 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Hook grip. Initiate from the hips — not the arms. Hip contact before the pull. Elbow punch. This is your first-step explosion on court. Record load.

A2 — Goblet Squat 12 x 3 sets @moderate KB @7 RPE
*Full depth. Elbows inside knees. Drive the floor away. Conditioning load — not a grind. Focus on position quality.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — PULL + HIP STABILITY | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 60 sec after B2.

B1 — Chest-Supported DB Row 10 x 3 sets (each arm) @7 RPE
*Flat bench at 30–45°. Lead with the elbow. No shoulder shrug. Controlled descent.

B2 — Side-Lying Hip Abduction 20 x 3 sets (each side) @band
*Band above knees. Stack hips vertically. Top leg lifts with control — not momentum. Pause at the top. This is lateral hip stability for court cutting — small movement, big carry-over.

*DB HPC is conditioning weight — crisp mechanics, not max load.
