============================================================
EM TAN — PHASE 2 | DAY 1–4 | HEAVY / PEAK
4-Day Format: Oly / Body Building / Oly / Body Building
============================================================

Notes:
W5 rebuild complete. Day 1–4 is the peak week — push to near-max attempts on Snatch and C&J.
Squat cycle peaks this week. Body building days maintain @8.5–9 RPE with heavier loads and lower reps than W5.
Shoulder and hip flags still active — monitor OHS position on Day 1, front rack comfort on Day 3.
If any lift feels off or position breaks down at high percentages — hold at the last clean weight. Do not grind a miss.

------------------------------------------------------------
Day 1 — SNATCH (PEAK)
------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

BAR ONLY
3 Sets:
3 Snatch DL + 3 Hang Snatch + 3 OHS

2 Sets:
20s OHS Hold
5 Sots Press

PART 1: OLYS — Snatch

@80% – 1 rep x 3 sets
@85% – 1 rep x 3 sets
@90% – 1 rep x 2 sets
@93–95% – 1 rep x 1–3 sets if sharp and receiving position is locked in

If feels good → attempt PR or match best

Then:
Snatch Pull @95% of Snatch — 2 reps x 3 sets

PART 2: SQUAT CYCLE — Back Squat (Peak)

@82.5% – 3 reps x 3 sets
@87.5% – 2 reps x 3 sets
@90% – 1 rep x 2 sets
@92.5% – 1 rep x 1–2 sets if moving well

Tempo: 2-1-1 on the first rep of every set

------------------------------------------------------------
Day 2 — BODY BUILDING (HEAVY / PEAK)
------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

2 Sets:
10 Banded Good Mornings
10 Scap Push-Ups
10 DB RDL
20s Hollow Hold

PART 1: BODY BUILDING

Snatch-Grip RDL with 3-sec lowering
6 reps x 4 sets @9 RPE
*Heaviest RDL of the block. Maintain the slow eccentric — no rushing the descent.

Paused Barbell Bench Press
6 reps x 4 sets @8.5–9 RPE
*Pause 1 sec at the chest. Full lockout at the top. Controlled return.

Pendlay Row
6 reps x 4 sets @8.5–9 RPE
*Bar returns to the floor between every rep — full reset. Explosive pull to lower chest.

Front-Foot Elevated DB Split Squat
8 reps/leg x 4 sets @8.5–9 RPE

PART 2: ACCESSORY

Ring Push-Up or Weighted Push-Up
10 reps x 3 sets @9 RPE

DB Hammer Curl
15 reps x 3 sets @9 RPE

Weighted Plank
40–50s x 3 sets

Finisher
Banded Chest Fly 15 reps + 6 Push-Ups — 2 sets @9 RPE

------------------------------------------------------------
Day 3 — CLEAN & JERK (PEAK)
------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

BAR ONLY
3 Sets:
2 Clean Pull + 2 Hang Clean + 2 Front Squat + 2 Push Press + 2 Split Jerk

2 Sets:
20s Front Rack Hold
5 Jerk Dip + Drive

Build Set — 3 Sets:
1 Clean + 1 Jerk — up to 75–80%

PART 1: OLYS — Clean & Jerk

@80% – 1 rep x 3 sets
@85% – 1 rep x 3 sets
@90% – 1 rep x 2 sets
@93–95% – 1 rep x 1–2 sets if sharp

If quality is there → attempt PR or match best

Then:
Split Jerk from Rack @90% – 2 reps x 3 sets

PART 2: SQUAT CYCLE — Front Squat (Peak)

@82.5% – 2 reps x 3 sets
@87.5% – 2 reps x 3 sets
@90–92% – 1 rep x 2–3 sets

Pause: 1 sec at the bottom on the first rep of each set

------------------------------------------------------------
Day 4 — BODY BUILDING (HEAVY / PEAK)
------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

2 Sets:
10 Banded Face Pulls
10 Glute Bridges
8/leg Lunges
20s Dead Hang

PART 1: BODY BUILDING

Barbell Hip Thrust
8 reps x 4 sets @9 RPE
*Peak load for the block. 2-sec squeeze at the top of every rep. Drive through the full foot.

Ring Row with 2-sec squeeze
10 reps x 4 sets @9 RPE
*Body as horizontal as possible. Full scapula retraction at the top.

DB Z-Press (seated on floor, legs straight)
8 reps x 4 sets @9 RPE
*No back support — full shoulder and core demand. Full lockout at the top.

Front Rack DB Walking Lunge
8 reps/leg x 4 sets @9 RPE
*Elbows up in the rack position throughout. Long step — controlled descent.

PART 2: ACCESSORY

DB Lateral Raise
20 reps x 3 sets @9 RPE

Barbell Curl
12 reps x 3 sets @9 RPE

Hollow Body Hold
40s x 3 sets

Finisher — Shoulder Burnout Complex
10 DB Lateral Raises + 10 DB Front Raises + 10 Band Pull-Aparts — 2 sets @9 RPE

Shoulder pain + tight hips: monitor OHS position Day 1. If overhead discomfort
increases at 90%+ — hold at last clean weight, do not chase the number.
Front rack Day 3: add 2 sets 90/90 Hip Rotation before front squat block if hips are tight.
Day 1–4 is peak week — record every PR attempt. These numbers set the baselines for the next block.
