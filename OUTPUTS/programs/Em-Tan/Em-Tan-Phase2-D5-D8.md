============================================================
EM TAN — PHASE 2 | DAY 5–8 | SUSTAIN THRESHOLD
4-Day Format: Oly / Body Building / Oly / Body Building
============================================================

Notes:
Post-peak sustain block. Goal is to maintain the strength base built in Day 1–4 — not to push beyond it.
Oly days: cap at 85–87%. Crisp, technical lifts. No PR attempts. Stop a set early if position breaks.
Body building days: hold @8.5–9 RPE. Same weight or within 5% of Day 2 and Day 4 loads.
This block cements the gains — the next build cycle starts fresh after this.
Shoulder and hip flags remain active.

------------------------------------------------------------
Day 5 — SNATCH (THRESHOLD)
------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

BAR ONLY
3 Sets:
3 Snatch DL + 3 Hang Snatch + 3 OHS

2 Sets:
20s OHS Hold
5 Sots Press

PART 1: OLYS — Snatch

@70% – 2 reps x 2 sets (technique warm-up — no rush)
@75% – 2 reps x 2 sets
@80% – 1 rep x 3 sets
@85–87% – 1 rep x 3 sets

*No PR attempts this block. 85–87% ceiling. If a lift feels sharp, add one more set at that weight — do not go higher.

Then:
Snatch Balance @60–65% of Snatch — 3 reps x 3 sets
*Footwork, receiving position, and confidence drill. Not a strength movement here.

PART 2: SQUAT CYCLE — Back Squat (Sustain)

@80% – 3 reps x 3 sets
@85% – 2 reps x 3 sets

Tempo: 2-1-1 on the first rep of each set. Same cue as peak week — no change.

------------------------------------------------------------
Day 6 — BODY BUILDING (SUSTAIN)
------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

2 Sets:
10 Banded Good Mornings
10 Scap Push-Ups
10 DB RDL
20s Hollow Hold

PART 1: BODY BUILDING

Snatch-Grip RDL with 3-sec lowering
5 reps x 4 sets @9 RPE
*Match or stay within 5% of Day 2 load. Slow eccentric stays — no rushing the descent.

Barbell Bench Press (no pause — full range)
8 reps x 4 sets @8.5–9 RPE
*Removes the pause from Day 2 — focus on full ROM and speed off the chest.

Pendlay Row
6 reps x 4 sets @8.5–9 RPE
*Same load target as Day 2. Bar to floor between reps — no cheating the reset.

Rear-Foot Elevated DB Split Squat
10 reps/leg x 3 sets @8.5 RPE
*Unilateral leg volume sustain. Controlled descent — knee tracks over second toe.

PART 2: ACCESSORY

Weighted Push-Up (plate on back)
12 reps x 3 sets @8.5 RPE

EZ Bar Curl
15 reps x 3 sets @8.5 RPE

Ab Wheel Rollout or Hollow Rock
10 reps x 3 sets

Finisher
Band Pull-Apart 20 reps + Face Pull 12 reps — 2 sets

------------------------------------------------------------
Day 7 — CLEAN & JERK (THRESHOLD)
------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

BAR ONLY
3 Sets:
2 Clean Pull + 2 Hang Clean + 2 Front Squat + 2 Push Press + 2 Split Jerk

2 Sets:
20s Front Rack Hold
5 Jerk Dip + Drive
2 Sets 90/90 Hip Rotation if hips are tight before front squat block

Build Set — 3 Sets:
1 Clean + 1 Jerk — up to 70–75%

PART 1: OLYS — Clean & Jerk

@70% – 1C + 1J x 2 sets
@75% – 1C + 1J x 2 sets
@80% – 1C + 1J x 3 sets
@85–87% – 1C + 1J x 2–3 sets

*Same ceiling as Day 5 — 85–87%. If the clean is sharp and the jerk is crisp, add one set. Do not go heavier.

Then:
Clean Pull @90% of Clean — 3 reps x 3 sets
*Stay vertical on the pull. No early arm bend.

PART 2: SQUAT CYCLE — Front Squat (Sustain)

@80% – 3 reps x 3 sets
@85–87% – 2 reps x 3 sets

Pause: 1 sec at the bottom on the first rep of each set. Same cue as peak week.

------------------------------------------------------------
Day 8 — BODY BUILDING (SUSTAIN)
------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

2 Sets:
10 Banded Face Pulls
10 Glute Bridges
8/leg Lunges
20s Dead Hang

PART 1: BODY BUILDING

Barbell Hip Thrust
10 reps x 4 sets @8.5–9 RPE
*Match or stay within 5% of Day 4 load. 2-sec squeeze at top — no change.

Weighted Pull-Up or Ring Row
8 reps x 4 sets @9 RPE
*If Pull-Up — use same band or weight as last logged set. Ring Row — body as horizontal as possible.

Seated DB Shoulder Press
10 reps x 4 sets @8.5–9 RPE
*Swapped from Z-Press — seated for volume sustainability. Full ROM, controlled return.

Barbell or DB Walking Lunge
10 reps/leg x 3 sets @8.5 RPE
*Same load range as Day 4 Front Rack Lunge — adapt to barbell back rack or dual DB depending on feel.

PART 2: ACCESSORY

DB Lateral Raise
20 reps x 3 sets @9 RPE

Chest-Supported Rear Delt Fly or Cable Face Pull
15 reps x 3 sets @8.5 RPE

Plank Hold
45s x 3 sets

Finisher — Shoulder Burnout Complex
10 DB Lateral Raises + 10 DB Front Raises + 10 Band Pull-Aparts — 2 sets @9 RPE

or Day 7 jerk — drop to 80% ceiling for that session. Do not push through it.
Hip flag: 90/90 Hip Rotation warm-up added to Day 7 before front squat block.
