============================================================
EM TAN - PHASE 3 | DAY 9-12 | TECHNICAL RELOAD UNDER 80%
4-Day Format: Oly / Body Building / Oly / Body Building
============================================================

Notes:
This week keeps the same training pattern: Snatch day, Body Building day, Clean & Jerk day, Body Building day.
All Olympic lifts and squat-cycle prescriptions stay at or below 80%.
Goal: sharpen positions, rebuild speed, and add clean/snatch technical volume without pushing intensity.
Shoulder and hip flags remain active.

------------------------------------------------------------
Day 9 - SNATCH (TECHNICAL RELOAD)
------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

BAR ONLY
3 Sets:
3 Snatch Deadlift + 3 Hang Power Snatch + 3 Overhead Squat

2 Sets:
20s Overhead Squat Hold
5 Sots Press

PART 1: OLYS - Snatch

TODAY'S GOAL: Receive the bar in a stable bottom position without chasing load.

@60% - 3 reps x 2 sets
@65% - 2 reps x 3 sets
@70% - 2 reps x 3 sets
@75% - 1 rep x 4 sets
@80% - 1 rep x 2 sets

*80% is the ceiling today. No extra jumps. If shoulder position feels off, stay at 70-75%.

Then:
Snatch Pull @80% of Snatch - 3 reps x 3 sets
*Pull stays vertical. Same start position as the snatch. No early arm bend.

PART 2: SQUAT CYCLE - Back Squat (Reload)

@65% - 5 reps x 3 sets
@70% - 4 reps x 3 sets
@75% - 3 reps x 3 sets

Tempo: 3-sec eccentric on the first rep of each set.

------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------

10 Scap Push-Ups
10 DB RDL

Snatch-Grip RDL with 3-sec lowering
6 reps x 4 sets @8 RPE

Barbell Bench Press

Pendlay Row

12 reps x 3 sets @8 RPE

Hollow Rock

------------------------------------------------------------
Day 11 - CLEAN & JERK (TECHNICAL RELOAD)
------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

BAR ONLY
3 Sets:
2 Clean Pull + 2 Hang Clean + 2 Front Squat + 2 Push Press + 2 Split Jerk

2 Sets:
20s Front Rack Hold
5 Jerk Dip + Drive
10 90/90 Hip Rotation each side if hips are tight before front squat block

PART 1: OLYS - Clean & Jerk

TODAY'S GOAL: Clean turnover and stable jerk footwork at submaximal load.

@60% - 1 Clean + 1 Jerk x 2 sets
@65% - 1 Clean + 1 Jerk x 3 sets
@70% - 1 Clean + 1 Jerk x 3 sets
@75% - 1 Clean + 1 Jerk x 3 sets
@80% - 1 Clean + 1 Jerk x 2 sets

*80% is the ceiling. If the jerk feels slow or shoulder discomfort appears, stay at 70-75%.

Then:
Clean Pull @80% of Clean - 3 reps x 3 sets
*Vertical pull. Legs finish before arms.

PART 2: SQUAT CYCLE - Front Squat (Reload)

@65% - 5 reps x 3 sets
@70% - 4 reps x 3 sets
@75% - 3 reps x 3 sets
@80% - 2 reps x 2 sets

Pause: 1 sec at the bottom on the first rep of each set.

------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------

PREP

2 Sets:
10 Banded Face Pulls

8 Lunges/leg
20s Dead Hang

PART 1: BODY BUILDING

Barbell Hip Thrust
10 reps x 4 sets @8 RPE
*2-sec squeeze at the top. Same or slightly lighter than last week.

Weighted Pull-Up or Ring Row
8 reps x 4 sets @8 RPE

Seated DB Shoulder Press
10 reps x 4 sets @7-8 RPE

15 reps x 3 sets @8 RPE

Chest-Supported Rear Delt Fly or Cable Face Pull
15 reps x 3 sets @8 RPE

Plank Hold
45s x 3 sets

10 DB Lateral Raises + 10 DB Front Raises + 20 Band Pull-Aparts - 2 sets @7-8 RPE

------------------------------------------------------------
COACH FLAG - ONGOING

No Olympic lift over 80% this week.
No PR attempts.
If shoulder pain appears on snatch receiving or jerk lockout, drop to 70-75% and keep the session technical.
If hips are tight before front squat, run the 90/90 Hip Rotation before loading.
Log every working set. These loads decide whether the next week returns to higher intensity or stays technical.
