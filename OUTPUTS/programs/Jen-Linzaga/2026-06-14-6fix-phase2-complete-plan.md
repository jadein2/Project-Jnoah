## Training — 6-week home progression (2x/week)

Two sessions: **A = Lower-focus, B = Upper/Full-body**, plus running folded into the week. Progressive overload comes from reps, tempo (slow eccentrics), harder variations, and density — not load.

| Week | Theme | Session A (Tue) | Session B (Thu) | Run |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Reset & screen | Movement assessment (BW squat, RDL, push-up, lunge, plank) — note pain/depth | Repeat screen + easy mobility | 2.5 km easy, RPE 5–6 |
| 2 | Foundation | BW squat 3×12 · BW RDL 3×12 · incline push-up 3×10 · band/DB row 3×12 · plank 3×30s | Reverse lunge 3×10/leg · glute bridge 3×15 · pike/knee push-up 3×8 · dead bug 3×10 | 3.0 km easy |
| 3 | Load intro | Tempo squat (3s down) 3×10 · single-leg RDL 3×8/leg · push-up 4×8 · row 4×10 · side plank 3×20s/side | Split squat 3×10/leg · hip thrust 3×15 · pike push-up 3×8 · band pull-apart 3×15 | Run/walk intervals 3.5 km |
| 4 | Mid-review | Squat 3×12 (controlled) · RDL 3×12 · push-up 3×10 · row 3×12 (deload volume) | Lunge 3×12/leg · glute bridge 3×20 · plank 3×40s | 3.0 km easy + **mid-program review** |
| 5 | Push | Tempo squat + pulse 4×10 · single-leg hip thrust 3×10/leg · decline/feet-up push-up 4×8 · row 4×8 (slow) | Bulgarian split squat 3×8/leg · push-up 4×max · row 4×10 · hollow hold 3×20s | Tempo run: 4×400m brisk / walk |
