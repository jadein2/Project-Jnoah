## Session A — Movement Assessment (bodyweight)

Goal is to *see* the movement, not chase a score. Note pain, depth, balance.

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Notes for Fame |
|---|---|---|
| Bodyweight squat | 3 × 10 | Box/chair squat if depth or knees complain |
| Hip hinge (RDL, no weight) | 3 × 10 | Pattern practice — flat back, hinge from hips |
| Push-up | 3 × max | **Incline (counter/wall) to start** — clean reps over numbers |
| Walking lunge | 2 × 10 each leg | Hold a wall/chair for balance if needed |
| Plank | 3 × 20–30 sec | Knees down is fine — keep a straight line |

Rest 60–90 sec. Total ~30 min.

## Session B — Aerobic Run/Walk (longer, easy stimulus)

Per setup: running is the conditioning default — built for an **aerobic-base stimulus**, started conservatively.

- **Run/walk intervals: 2.0 km total**, ~25–30 min — e.g. 2 min easy jog / 1 min walk, repeat.
- Intended stimulus: Zone 2 aerobic base + joint-friendly recovery. Build the engine, protect the knees.
- Effort RPE 5 — never breathless. If a jog interval spikes breathing, walk longer.
- Finish: 10 min mobility (hip flexors, hamstrings, thoracic spine) + 5 min breathing.
