STRENGTH BLOCK A — SQUAT + HINGE (TEST PIECES) | 3 Rounds
Perform A1, rest 2 min, then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2 before returning to A1.

A1 — Back Squat — Work up to a 3-Rep Max attempt
*This is the test piece for the cycle. Start at your D-16 load. Add 2.5–5kg per set until you find your heaviest 3 reps with solid form. Log the weight. This number anchors Cycle 2.

A2 — Sumo Deadlift 5 x 3 sets @8 RPE
*New movement — wide stance, toes pointed out 30–45 degrees. Hips drop lower than conventional. Bar stays close to the body. Lock out tall at the top. If it feels unfamiliar, keep the load moderate and focus on position.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — UPPER (BARBELL) | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Barbell Bench Press 5 x 3 sets @8–9 RPE
*Progression from DB bench — bar gives more loading potential. Retract scapula before unracking. 2-count descent. Drive the bar up and slightly back toward the rack. Record load.

B2 — Single-Arm DB Row 10 x 3 sets (each arm) @8 RPE
*Brace the non-working arm on the bench. Elbow drives back and up. Full stretch at the bottom — don't cut the range short to load heavier.

STRENGTH BLOCK C — ACCESSORY | 2 Rounds
Perform C1 → C2 → C3. Rest 60 sec after C3.

C1 — Cable Face Pull 15 x 2 sets @7 RPE
C2 — DB Lateral Raise 12 x 2 sets (each arm) @7 RPE
C3 — Pallof Press 10 x 2 sets (each side) @cable
*Anti-rotation core work. Brace hard. No trunk lean in either direction.

METCON
3 Rounds For Time (15:00 cap)

15 Cal Row
10 DB Hang Power Clean @moderate
10 DB Push Press @same weight
200m Air Runner

*The row starts each round. HPC and push press use the same DBs — cycle them without putting them down if you can. Air Runner closes the round — pace it so you can start the next row within 20 seconds of finishing.
*Cycle closer. Log your total time and note your energy across rounds 1–3. That gap is the conditioning baseline for the next block.
