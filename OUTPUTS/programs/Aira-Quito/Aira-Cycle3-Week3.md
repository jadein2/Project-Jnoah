### Day 1 - Coached | Olympic Lifting (Power Clean + Front Squat)

Warm up
5:00 Row (moderate)

3 Sets
10 PVC Good Mornings
10 Kang Squats
10 Greatest Stretch (each side)
5 Inch Worms

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets - building
5 Hang Muscle Clean @empty bar
5 Front Squat @empty bar
3 Power Clean @empty bar

OLYMPIC LIFTING BLOCK - POWER CLEAN | TECHNIQUE VOLUME
Rest 2-3 min between sets.

Power Clean 2 x 6 sets @7-8 RPE
*Build off Week 2. Keep the same top load if bar speed slowed last week. Add only one small jump if every catch was sharp.

STRENGTH BLOCK A - FRONT SQUAT + HIP ADDUCTION | 3 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 - Front Squat 5 x 3 sets @7-8 RPE
*Full squat as much as possible. Up the weight if perceived light and the clean rack stays solid.

A2 - Hip Adductor Machine 12 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Rotate back to the machine anchor. Full range, controlled return, no bouncing the stack.

METCON
AMRAP 11

9 Cal Row
6 Hang Power Clean @light-moderate
9 V-Ups

*The barbell is conditioning weight, not a second strength piece. Keep hip contact and fast elbows under fatigue. Log total rounds + reps.

### Day 2 - Program Only | Machine Lower

- complete independently -

Warm up
5:00 Air Runner (easy)

3 Sets
10 Bodyweight Squats
10 Hip Circles
10 Glute Bridges
10 Leg Swings front-to-back (each leg)

Specific Warm-Up
1 Set
10 Hack Squat Machine or Leg Press @very light

STRENGTH BLOCK A - SQUAT PATTERN + HAMSTRING | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 60-90 sec after A2.

A1 - Hack Squat Machine 10 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*If Hack Squat is not available, use Leg Press. Build off Week 2 effort, not ego. Full range every rep.

A2 - Seated Leg Curl 12 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Use a controlled 2-count return. Add load only if all 12 reps stay clean.

STRENGTH BLOCK B - SINGLE LEG + HIP ABDUCTION | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 60 sec after B2.

B1 - DB Step-Up 10 x 3 sets (each leg) @7-8 RPE
*Drive through the full foot on the working leg. Do not push off the floor leg.

B2 - Hip Abductor Machine 15 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Knees press out hard, controlled return, no momentum.

METCON
3 Rounds For Time (13:00 cap)

12 Cal Air Bike
12 DB Goblet Squats @light-moderate
12 Sit-Ups

*Moderate pace. This follows lower-body volume, so the goal is steady output and clean reps. Log total time and all loads.

### Day 3 - Coached | Functional Fitness + Gymnastics (Back Squat + Box Jump + Pull-Up)

Warm up
5:00 Air Bike (moderate)

3 Sets
5 Inch Worms
10 Kang Squats
10 Greatest Stretch (each side)
10 Hip Bridges
10 Cossack Squats

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets - building
5 Back Squat @empty bar
3 Box Jump Step-Down @20in
3 Assisted Pull-Ups

STRENGTH BLOCK A - SQUAT + HIP ADDUCTION | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 - Back Squat 5 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Build off Week 2 if load was logged. If no log, repeat Week 2 effort and add only if form and strength are good.

A2 - Copenhagen Plank Hold 20 sec x 3 sets (each side)
*Quality hold. Hips stay high. Regress to bent-knee Copenhagen if position breaks.

GYMNASTICS BLOCK - BOX JUMP + PULL-UP SKILL | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 - Box Jump Step-Down 7 x 3 sets @20in
*Progress one rep from Week 2 only if landings stayed soft. Step down every rep.

B2 - Assisted Pull-Up 7 x 3 sets
*Progress one rep from Week 2. Keep full dead hang and chin over bar.

METCON
4 Rounds For Time (17:00 cap)

12 Cal Ski Erg
10 DB Front Rack Lunges @light-moderate
8 Burpee Box Jump Overs @20in

*New combo. The Ski Erg opens each round, lunges stay controlled, and burpee box jump overs are steady. Log total time.

STRENGTH BLOCK A - VERTICAL PRESS + HORIZONTAL PULL | 4 Rounds

*Lead with the elbows. Pause at peak contraction.

*Keep the dumbbells manageable. Push Press is leg drive first. Renegade Rows stay braced with minimal hip rotation. Log rounds + reps.

### Day 5 - Coached | Strength + Gymnastics (Push Press + Toes-to-Bar)

Warm up
5:00 Row (easy)

3 Sets
10 Lu-Raises @5lbs
10 Banded Pull-Aparts
5 Inch Worms with Push-Up
10 Hip Bridges

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets - building
5 Push Press @empty bar
5 Pendlay Row @empty bar
3 Toes-to-Bar or Knees-to-Chest

STRENGTH BLOCK A - PUSH PRESS + ROW | 3 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 - Push Press 5 x 3 sets @7-8 RPE
*Build off Week 2 if the last set was smooth. Dip shallow, drive hard, lock out fully.

A2 - Pendlay Row 8 x 3 sets @7-8 RPE
*Reset every rep from the floor. Flat back, pull to lower chest.

STRENGTH BLOCK B - UNILATERAL POSTERIOR CHAIN | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 60 sec after B2.

B1 - Single-Leg Romanian Deadlift 8 x 3 sets (each leg) @light-moderate
*Hip square to the floor. Slight knee bend. Balance before load.

B2 - Cable Hip Abduction 12 x 3 sets (each side) @7 RPE
*Stay tall and control the return.

GYMNASTICS BLOCK - TOES-TO-BAR SKILL | 3 Rounds

B1 - Toes-to-Bar or Knees-to-Chest 7 x 3 sets
*Progress one rep from Week 2 only if the kip and hollow position stay clean.

METCON
For Time (17:00 cap)

500m Row
21-15-9
KB Deadlift @24kg
Toes-to-Bar or Knees-to-Chest
500m Row

*Bookend format. Row strong but controlled. KB Deadlift replaces swings this week to reduce repeated swing exposure. Break gymnastics early. Log total time.
