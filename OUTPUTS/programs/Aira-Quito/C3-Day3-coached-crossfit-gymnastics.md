STRENGTH BLOCK A — SQUAT + HIP ADDUCTION | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Back Squat 5 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Cycle 3 baseline. Start just below your D-16 working weight — this is Week 1, quality over load. Full depth. Drive the floor away. Knees track over the toes.

A2 — Copenhagen Plank Hold 20 sec x 4 sets (each side)
*Lie on your side, top leg rests on a bench. Bottom leg hangs free. Create tension through the inner thigh and obliques — do not let the hips sag. Rest the same on each side before moving to the next A1.

GYMNASTICS BLOCK — BOX JUMP + PULL-UP SKILL | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Box Jump Step-Down 5 x 3 sets @20in
*New movement. Jump with full hip and knee extension at the top. Land soft — knees bent, absorb the landing. Step down after each rep. Full reset before the next jump. This is skill work — do not rush.

B2 — Assisted Pull-Up 5 x 3 sets (band or machine)
*Full dead hang at the bottom — do not start from a bent-arm position. Chin fully clears the bar at the top. Choose a band level where you can complete all 5 reps with control, not a bounce. This is the foundation for unassisted pull-ups across this cycle.
