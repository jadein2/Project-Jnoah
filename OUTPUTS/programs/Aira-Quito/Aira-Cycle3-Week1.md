### Day 1 — Coached | Olympic Lifting (Power Clean + Front Squat)

Warm up
5:00 Row (moderate — open the hips and thoracic before bar work)

3 Sets
10 PVC Good Mornings
10 Kang Squats
10 Greatest Stretch (each side)
5 Inch Worms

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Hang Muscle Clean @empty bar (bar to hips, hip contact, shrug, elbows punch through to rack position)
5 Front Squat @empty bar (elbows high, clean rack position, full depth)

OLYMPIC LIFTING BLOCK — POWER CLEAN | 4 Sets
Rest 2–3 min between sets.

Power Clean 3 x 4 sets — build from empty bar
*Cycle 3 baseline — first time Power Clean is the main lift. Start at the empty bar or a load that lets you move with speed. Bar starts below the knee. Hip contact before the pull — not arms. Shrug, elbows punch through fast. Catch at quarter squat. Step 1 is positions, not weight.
*Build set to set only if mechanics stay clean. Log every set load.

STRENGTH BLOCK A — FRONT SQUAT + HIP ABDUCTION | 3 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Front Squat 5 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Elbows stay up — this is the whole session. If elbows drop, bar drops. High bar, clean rack position. Drive knees out. Full depth. Heels down. Cycle 3 baseline — log the load.

A2 — Hip Abductor Machine 15 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Seated upright. Full range. Slow controlled return — do not let it slam back.

METCON
AMRAP 10

12 Cal Row (hard effort — not a sprint, but push)
6 Hang Power Clean @same bar (reduce to ~50% of your working weight)
9 Front Rack Lunge @same bar (alternating legs)

*Short and technical. The bar stays loaded from the Olympic block — strip to a comfortable conditioning weight. HPC mechanics must stay clean under fatigue — hip contact, elbows through. Front Rack Lunges: elbows up even when the legs are burning.
*Log total rounds + reps.

### Day 2 — Program Only | Machine Lower

— complete independently —

Warm up
5:00 Air Runner (easy)

3 Sets
10 Bodyweight Squats
10 Hip Circles
10 Glute Bridges
10 Leg Swings front-to-back (each leg)

Specific Warm-Up
1 Set
10 Leg Press @very light (position check — full range, feet mid-plate)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — QUAD + HAMSTRING (MACHINE) | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 60 sec after A2.

A1 — Leg Press 12 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Cycle 3 baseline — start at a load you can move for all 4 sets and note it. Full range every rep, no partial reps to chase load.

A2 — Leg Curl (Lying or Seated) 10 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Controlled movement. Brief hold at full contraction. Slow 2-count eccentric.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — GLUTE + HIP HEALTH (MACHINE) | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 → B2 → B3. Rest 60 sec after B3.

B1 — Leg Extension 12 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*2-count slow negative. Do not hyperextend at the top.

B2 — Hip Adductor Machine 15 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Seated upright. Full range adduction — feel the inner thigh. Controlled return.

B3 — Hip Abductor Machine 15 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Upright position. Full range abduction. Do not lean to one side to assist.

METCON
3 Rounds For Time (12:00 cap)

18 Cal Row (steady effort — not a sprint)
12 KB Goblet Squat @16kg
10 Banded Side Steps (each direction)

*Moderate intensity — this follows a full machine session. Keep the row smooth and consistent across all 3 rounds. Goblet squat: chest up, elbows inside knees at the bottom. Log your total time.
*Log all loads.

### Day 3 — Coached | CrossFit + Gymnastics (Back Squat + Box Jump + Pull-Up)

Warm up
5:00 Air Bike (moderate)

3 Sets
5 Inch Worms
10 Kang Squats
10 Greatest Stretch (each side)
10 Hip Bridges
10 Cossack Squats

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Back Squat @empty bar (full depth, bar speed, drive the knees out)
3 Box Jump Step-Down @20in @50% effort (jump with intent, land soft with bent knees, step down — do not jump off)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — SQUAT + HIP ADDUCTION | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Back Squat 5 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Cycle 3 baseline. Start just below your D-16 working weight — this is Week 1, quality over load. Full depth. Drive the floor away. Knees track over the toes.

A2 — Copenhagen Plank Hold 20 sec x 4 sets (each side)
*Lie on your side, top leg rests on a bench. Bottom leg hangs free. Create tension through the inner thigh and obliques — do not let the hips sag. Rest the same on each side before moving to the next A1.

GYMNASTICS BLOCK — BOX JUMP + PULL-UP SKILL | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Box Jump Step-Down 5 x 3 sets @20in
*New movement. Jump with full hip and knee extension at the top. Land soft — knees bent, absorb the landing. Step down after each rep. Full reset before the next jump. This is skill work — do not rush.

B2 — Assisted Pull-Up 5 x 3 sets (band or machine)
*Full dead hang at the bottom — do not start from a bent-arm position. Chin fully clears the bar at the top. Choose a band level where you can complete all 5 reps with control, not a bounce. This is the foundation for unassisted pull-ups across this cycle.

METCON
4 Rounds For Time (16:00 cap)

15 Cal Air Bike (sprint — near max effort each round)
12 KB Swings @20kg (American or Russian — confirm with coach)
6 Box Jump Step-Down @20in
4 Assisted Pull-Ups

*Round 1 sets the pace. The Air Bike sprint is the hardest piece each round — commit to it. KB Swings are hip-driven: hinge back, drive forward with the glutes — not a squat. Box Jumps: step down after every rep, no rushing the landing. Pull-Ups: same band as the skill block.
*Log total time.

### Day 4 — Program Only | Machine Upper

— complete independently —

Warm up
5:00 Air Runner (easy)

3 Sets
10 Arm Circles (forward and backward)
10 Band Pull-Aparts
10 Scapular Push-Ups
10 Cat-Cow (slow, full thoracic range)

Specific Warm-Up
1 Set
10 Chest Press Machine @very light
10 Lat Pulldown @very light

STRENGTH BLOCK A — HORIZONTAL PRESS + VERTICAL PULL (MACHINE) | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 60 sec after A2.

A1 — Chest Press Machine 10 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Cycle 3 baseline. Retract scapula before pressing. Controlled return — do not let the stack drop.

A2 — Lat Pulldown 10 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Wide overhand grip. Bar to upper chest. Slight lean back — not a full recline. Full extension at the top every rep.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — SHOULDER + CHEST ISOLATION (MACHINE/CABLE) | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 → B2 → B3. Rest 60 sec after B3.

B1 — Shoulder Press Machine 10 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Core braced throughout. Full lockout at the top. Controlled descent.

B2 — Pec Deck / Cable Fly 12 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Slight bend in the elbows — do not straighten. Stretch at the open position. Squeeze hard at the close. Slow tempo — 2-count open, 1-count squeeze.

B3 — DB Lateral Raise 12 x 3 sets (each arm) @6–7 RPE
*Lead with the elbow. Stop at shoulder height. No body lean to generate momentum.

METCON
AMRAP 12

14 Cal Assault Bike (moderate — 80% effort, not a sprint)
10 DB Sumo Deadlift @moderate
8 DB Arnold Press @light
8 Hollow Body Rocks (or Ab Wheel Rollout x 5)

*Moderate intensity — program only session. Keep the bike at a pace you can sustain all 12 minutes. DB Sumo DL: wide stance, toes out, hips drop into the movement. Arnold Press: start with palms facing you, rotate to palms out at the top. Hollow Rocks: lower back pressed to the floor throughout.
*Log all loads and total rounds + reps.

### Day 5 — Coached | Strength + Gymnastics (Push Press + Toes-to-Bar)

Warm up
5:00 Row (easy)

3 Sets
10 Lu-Raises @5lbs
10 Banded Pull-Aparts
5 Inch Worms with Push-Up
10 Hip Bridges

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Push Press @empty bar (dip shallow and fast, drive with the legs, full lockout overhead)
5 Pendlay Row @empty bar (bar starts on the floor each rep — pull to lower chest)
3 Hang from bar + Knees-to-Chest (introduce the position — hollow body on the bar, pull the knees up with control)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — PUSH PRESS + ROW | 3 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Push Press 5 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Cycle 3 baseline. Dip shallow — not a deep squat. Drive with the legs first, the arms finish. Full lockout overhead — active shoulders, push the ceiling. Control the descent back to the rack. Log the load.

A2 — Pendlay Row 8 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Bar starts on the floor between every rep — this is not a touch-and-go. Reset the brace, pull explosively to the lower chest. Upper back drives the movement. Flat back throughout.

GYMNASTICS BLOCK — TOES-TO-BAR SKILL | 3 Rounds
B1 — Toes-to-Bar / Knees-to-Chest 5 x 3 sets
*New movement — introduce the hang first. Grab the bar in a dead hang, create a hollow body position (ribs down, slight posterior pelvic tilt). Pull the knees to the chest with control — no kipping yet. If the knees-to-chest feels solid, try toes to bar. Keep the movement strict. This is skill acquisition, not volume.

METCON
For Time (18:00 cap)

30 Cal Row
25 Air Squats
20 DB Push Press @light-moderate (same DBs for both movements below)
15 DB Hang Power Clean @same weight
10 Toes-to-Bar (or Knees-to-Chest)

*Descending chipper — starts with the aerobic piece, finishes with the skill piece. Pace the row at 85% — not a sprint, you have a lot of work ahead. Air Squats should be unbroken. DB Push Press and HPC use the same DBs — keep them close. Break the TTB/KTC into sets of 3–5 if needed. The last 10 reps should feel earned.
*Log your finishing time.
