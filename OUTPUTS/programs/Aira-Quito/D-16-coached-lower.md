STRENGTH BLOCK A — SQUAT + HINGE | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Back Squat 6 x 4 sets @8–9 RPE
*New rep range — heavier than D-13. Feel the weight and add if form and strength is good.

A2 — Romanian Deadlift 6 x 4 sets @8 RPE

STRENGTH BLOCK B — LUNGE + POSTERIOR CHAIN | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 → B2 → B3. Rest 60–90 sec after B3.

B1 — Bulgarian Split Squat 8 x 3 sets (each leg) @2xDB @8 RPE
B2 — Cable Pull-Through 15 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Hinge at the hips — not a squat. Push the hips back, feel the hamstring stretch, drive forward with the glutes. Keep the cable between the legs throughout.
B3 — Banded Side Steps 15 x 3 sets (each direction)

METCON
3 Rounds For Time (12:00 cap)

200m Air Runner (steady pace — not a sprint)
12 DB Sumo Deadlift @moderate (pick a weight you can move fast but feel)
10 Box Step-Ups @light DB (each leg — first time doing these loaded)

*The Air Runner sets the rhythm for each round. Sumo DL reinforces what you just did in the strength block — keep the hinge clean even under fatigue.
