Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Conventional Deadlift @empty bar (bar path, brace, hinge)
5 Push Press @empty bar (dip and drive, lock out overhead)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — HINGE + OVERHEAD | 4 Rounds
Perform A1, rest 2 min, then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2 before returning to A1.

A1 — Conventional Deadlift 5 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Full brace before the pull. Bar stays close to the shins the entire way up. Lock out tall — no hyperextension at the top.

A2 — Push Press 8 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Dip shallow and fast. Drive with the legs. Full lockout overhead before bringing the bar down. Control the descent.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — SQUAT + PULL | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Back Squat 6 x 3 sets @8 RPE
*Heavier than usual rep range — focus on quality at the bottom. No forward lean.

B2 — Barbell Bent-Over Row 8 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Flat back. Elbows drive back and up. Bar contacts lower chest.

METCON
3 Rounds For Time (12:00 cap)

8 Deadlift @60% of working weight
12 Cal Row
10 Push-Ups

*Bar stays loaded from Block A — drop to 60% for the metcon. Keep the hinge pattern clean even under fatigue.
