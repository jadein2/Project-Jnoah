### Day 1 — Coached | Olympic Lifting (Power Clean + Front Squat)

Warm up
5:00 Row (moderate — open the hips and thoracic before bar work)

3 Sets
10 PVC Good Mornings
10 Kang Squats
10 Greatest Stretch (each side)
5 Inch Worms

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Hang Muscle Clean @empty bar (bar to hips, hip contact, shrug, elbows punch through to rack position)
5 Front Squat @empty bar (elbows high, clean rack position, full depth)

OLYMPIC LIFTING BLOCK — POWER CLEAN | 5 Sets
Rest 2–3 min between sets.

Power Clean 3 x 5 sets — build off last week's working weight
*Added a set from last week. If mechanics stayed clean at your top set last time, start there and hold or take one small jump. Hip contact before the pull — not arms. Shrug, then elbows punch through fast. Catch at quarter squat. Log every set load.

STRENGTH BLOCK A — FRONT SQUAT + HIP ADDUCTION | 3 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Front Squat 5 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Build off last week's working weight — *up the weight if perceived light. Same standard as last week: elbows stay up, high bar, clean rack position, full depth, heels down.

A2 — Cable Hip Adduction 12 x 3 sets (each side) @7 RPE
*New hip health movement this week. Stand side-on to the cable, ankle cuff on the inside leg, pull the leg across the body and control the return. Torso stays tall — no leaning into the pull.

METCON
AMRAP 10

10 Cal Ski Erg
5 Hang Power Clean @same bar (reduce to ~50% of your working weight)
8 V-Ups

*Ski Erg replaces the Row this week. Bar stays loaded from the Olympic block — strip to a comfortable conditioning weight. HPC mechanics stay clean under fatigue: hip contact, elbows punch through. V-Ups: control the descent, don't collapse into the floor.
*Log total rounds + reps.

### Day 2 — Program Only | Machine Lower

— complete independently —

Warm up
5:00 Air Runner (easy)

3 Sets
10 Bodyweight Squats
10 Hip Circles
10 Glute Bridges
10 Leg Swings front-to-back (each leg)

Specific Warm-Up
1 Set
10 Leg Press @very light (position check — full range, feet mid-plate)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — QUAD + HAMSTRING (MACHINE) | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 60 sec after A2.

A1 — Leg Press 12 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Build off last week's load — *up the weight if perceived light. Full range every rep, no partial reps to chase load.

A2 — Leg Curl (Lying or Seated) 10 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Same as last week. Controlled movement, brief hold at full contraction, slow 2-count eccentric. Add load if last week felt easy.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — GLUTE + HIP HEALTH (MACHINE) | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 → B2 → B3. Rest 60 sec after B3.

B1 — Bulgarian Split Squat (DB) 10 x 3 sets (each leg) @7–8 RPE
*New this week. Rear foot elevated on a bench, one DB in each hand. Front knee tracks over the toes, drive through the front heel to stand. Light load first round to find balance.

B2 — Banded Side Steps 15 x 3 sets (each direction)
*Hip abduction work — band sits above the knees, stay low in a quarter-squat, small controlled steps. Knees press out the whole time, no standing tall between steps.

B3 — Roman Chair Back Extension 10 x 3 sets @45lbs
*New posterior chain anchor. Hips hinge over the pad, flat back the whole way down, squeeze the glutes to come back up. Do not hyperextend at the top.

METCON
3 Rounds For Time (12:00 cap)

15 Cal Air Bike
12 DB Walking Lunges (alternating, total reps) @light-moderate
12 Sit-Ups

*Moderate intensity — this follows a full machine session. Walking Lunges: tall posture, back knee taps light, drive through the front heel each step. Sit-Ups: controlled, full range, no yanking the neck. Log your total time and all loads.

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Back Squat @empty bar (full depth, bar speed, drive the knees out)
3 Box Jump Step-Down @20in @50% effort (jump with intent, land soft with bent knees, step down — do not jump off)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — SQUAT + HIP ADDUCTION | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Back Squat 5 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Build off last week's working weight — *up the weight if perceived light. Full depth, drive the floor away, knees track over the toes.

A2 — Wide Stance Goblet Squat 12 x 4 sets @7 RPE (light-moderate KB or DB)
*New hip health movement this week. Wide stance, toes turned out, sit down between the heels — squeeze the inner thighs to stand back up. Doubles as squat prep for the day's main lift.

GYMNASTICS BLOCK — BOX JUMP + PULL-UP SKILL | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Box Jump Step-Down 6 x 3 sets @20in
*Added a rep from last week. Full hip and knee extension at the top, land soft with bent knees, step down every rep, full reset before the next jump.

B2 — Assisted Pull-Up 6 x 3 sets (band or machine)
*Added a rep from last week. Full dead hang at the bottom, chin fully clears the bar at the top. If 5 reps felt smooth across all 3 sets last week, hold the same band — the extra rep is this week's progression.

METCON
4 Rounds For Time (16:00 cap)

15 Cal Row
10 DB Thrusters @light-moderate (2xDB)
8 Burpees
5 Assisted Pull-Ups

*New combo this week. Thrusters: full front squat depth into the press, no pause at the top. Burpees: chest to the floor, full extension at the top — steady pace, don't blow up round 1. Same band as the skill block for the pull-ups. Log total time.

### Day 5 — Coached | Strength + Gymnastics (Push Press + Toes-to-Bar)

Warm up
5:00 Row (easy)

3 Sets
10 Lu-Raises @5lbs
10 Banded Pull-Aparts
5 Inch Worms with Push-Up
10 Hip Bridges

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Push Press @empty bar (dip shallow and fast, drive with the legs, full lockout overhead)
5 Pendlay Row @empty bar (bar starts on the floor each rep — pull to lower chest)
3 Toes-to-Bar / Knees-to-Chest (revisit the position from last week — hollow body on the bar)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — PUSH PRESS + ROW | 3 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Push Press 5 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Build off last week's working weight — *up the weight if perceived light. Dip shallow, drive with the legs first, full lockout overhead, control the descent back to the rack.

A2 — Pendlay Row 8 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Same as last week. Bar starts on the floor every rep — reset the brace, pull explosively to the lower chest, flat back throughout.

ACCESSORY — HIP HEALTH | 3 Sets
Cable Hip Abduction 12 x 3 sets (each side) @7 RPE
*New this week. Ankle cuff on the outside leg, cable anchored low at the side, drive the leg out and away from the body. Controlled return, stay tall — no leaning.

GYMNASTICS BLOCK — TOES-TO-BAR SKILL | 3 Rounds
B1 — Toes-to-Bar / Knees-to-Chest 6 x 3 sets
*Added a rep from last week. Dead hang start, hollow body position, pull the knees (or toes) up with control. Push toward toes-to-bar if knees-to-chest felt solid last week — keep it strict either way.

METCON
For Time (16:00 cap)

400m Run
21-15-9
KB Swings @20kg (American)
Toes-to-Bar (or Knees-to-Chest)
400m Run

*Bookend format — the runs cap the rep ladder. KB Swings are hip-driven: hinge back, drive forward with the glutes — not a squat. Break the TTB/KTC into manageable sets early (e.g. 7-7-7 on the first round of 21) so you're not grinding late. Pace both runs even — the second shouldn't fall off much from the first. Log your finishing time.
