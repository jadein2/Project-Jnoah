STRENGTH BLOCK A — HORIZONTAL PUSH + PULL (MACHINE) | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 60 sec after A2.

A1 — Chest Press Machine 10 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Add one increment over D-15. Retract scapula before pressing.

A2 — Seated Cable Row 10 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Drive elbows back. Full scapula retraction at the end.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — CHEST + SHOULDER ISOLATION (CABLE/MACHINE) | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 → B2 → B3. Rest 60 sec after B3.

B1 — Pec Deck / Cable Fly 12 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Slight bend in the elbows throughout — do not straighten. Feel the stretch at the open position. Squeeze at the close.

B2 — Cable Face Pull 15 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Rope attachment at face height. Pull to forehead. External rotation at the end — thumbs back. Slow tempo, this is shoulder health work.

B3 — Cable Lateral Raise 12 x 3 sets (each arm) @6–7 RPE
*Lead with the elbow. Stop at shoulder height. No body lean to assist.

METCON

18 Cal Row
12 DB Hang Power Clean @light-moderate (pick a weight you can cycle for 12 reps unbroken)
10 DB Push Press @same weight

*The hang power clean and push press use the same DBs — pick up, go. Drive with the legs on the press. If form breaks before 10 reps, drop the weight next round.
