STRENGTH BLOCK A — QUAD + HAMSTRING (MACHINE) | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 60 sec after A2.

A1 — Leg Press 12 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Cycle 3 baseline — start at a load you can move for all 4 sets and note it. Full range every rep, no partial reps to chase load.

A2 — Leg Curl (Lying or Seated) 10 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Controlled movement. Brief hold at full contraction. Slow 2-count eccentric.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — GLUTE + HIP HEALTH (MACHINE) | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 → B2 → B3. Rest 60 sec after B3.

B1 — Leg Extension 12 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*2-count slow negative. Do not hyperextend at the top.

B2 — Hip Adductor Machine 15 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Seated upright. Full range adduction — feel the inner thigh. Controlled return.

B3 — Hip Abductor Machine 15 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Upright position. Full range abduction. Do not lean to one side to assist.
