JEMINA APOSTOL — PRE-ACL PREHAB BLOCK 1 — SESSION 1
FOUNDATIONAL STRENGTH & STABILITY

------------------------------------------------------------
INSTRUCTIONS
------------------------------------------------------------
Controlled-range strength session. Stay inside pain-free range on every movement. Cap effort at RPE 6 — this is not a push session. Pain during or after should stay under 3-4/10. If anything sharp, pinching, or a give-way feeling shows up, stop that movement, swap to the listed regression, and note it in the debrief below.

------------------------------------------------------------
STIMULUS TAG
------------------------------------------------------------
Intensity: Low
Primary System: Strength / Motor Control
CNS Load: Low
Expected RPE: 4-6
Recovery Demand: Light

------------------------------------------------------------
WARM UP
------------------------------------------------------------
General:
- Air Bike or stationary bike — 5 min, easy pace, no resistance

Specific:
- Ankle pumps — 15 reps
- Double-leg glute bridge (bodyweight) — 10 reps
- Single-leg balance hold (hand support allowed) — both sides, hold to first wobble or touch-down, record time in debrief
- Box squat to tall box — 5-8 reps, find pain-free depth only

------------------------------------------------------------
STRENGTH BLOCK A — Squat Pattern + Bridge
------------------------------------------------------------
A1 — Box Squat to Tall Box 8-10 x 3 sets @RPE 4-6
Coach cue: 3 sec down, light pause on the box, stand tall through mid-foot. Depth is whatever is pain-free, even if that means a high box.

A2 — Double-Leg Glute Bridge 12-15 x 3 sets @RPE 4-6
Coach cue: 2 sec hold at the top, squeeze the glutes, ribs down — don't arch the lower back to get there.

Rest 60-90 sec between rounds.

------------------------------------------------------------
STRENGTH BLOCK B — Hip Stability
------------------------------------------------------------
B1 — Side-Lying Hip Abduction 12-15/side x 3 sets @RPE 4-6
Coach cue: hips stacked, lift from the side of the hip, no rocking back.

B2 — Side-Lying Hip Adduction (top leg propped) 10-12/side x 3 sets @RPE 4-6
Coach cue: slow and controlled — stop immediately if there's any pull behind the knee.

Rest 60-90 sec between rounds.

------------------------------------------------------------
CORE
------------------------------------------------------------
Dead Bug 10/side x 3 sets
Bird Dog 10/side x 3 sets
Plank (knees down) 20-30 sec x 3 sets

------------------------------------------------------------
CONDITIONING
------------------------------------------------------------
Folded into warm-up — Air Bike or stationary bike, 5 min easy. No separate conditioning piece for Block 1.

------------------------------------------------------------
ACCESSORY
------------------------------------------------------------
Double-Leg Heel Raise 12-15 x 3 sets
Supine Hamstring Curl (heels on slider or abmat, hips lifted) 10-12 x 3 sets

------------------------------------------------------------
REPLACEMENT / SCALING OPTIONS
------------------------------------------------------------
- Box Squat to Tall Box: if pinching at depth, raise the box. If bodyweight feels under RPE 4, hold a light dumbbell at chest.
- Double-Leg Glute Bridge: if the lower back takes over, regress to isometric holds — 5 x 10 sec at the top, no reps.
- Side-Lying Hip Abduction / Adduction: add a light band above the knees once 3x15 feels like RPE 4 or under.
- Supine Hamstring Curl: if hamstring cramps, regress to an isometric hold at mid-range, 5 x 10 sec.
- Plank: if knees-down is still RPE 7+, regress to a wall sit, 20-30 sec.

------------------------------------------------------------
MOBILITY
------------------------------------------------------------
- Quad stretch (standing or supported), pain-free range — 30 sec/side x 2
- Heel prop / passive knee extension, supported — 2 min, gentle, no bounce

Note: this is a standalone closing block, not folded into warm-up. Deliberate call for this prehab block — passive extension work pre-op has direct value. This is a deviation from the wod-brain default (mobility lives in warm-up only), flagged per the conflict-resolution rule. Drop it from S2 onward if you'd rather keep mobility warm-up-only.
