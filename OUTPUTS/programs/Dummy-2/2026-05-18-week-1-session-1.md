------------------------------------------------------------

SESSION 1 — UPPER BODY FOUNDATION
Week 1 | Horizontal Push + Pull Pattern | Full Gym

Stimulus: Strength Primer + Short Aerobic Conditioning
Intensity: Low–Moderate
CNS Load: Low–Medium
RPE Target: 6–7
Recovery Demand: Light–Moderate
Duration: ~55 minutes
Session Summary: Establish horizontal push and pull baselines. Short conditioning to close. No redline.

------------------------------------------------------------

WARM-UP — 10 minutes

- Bike or row easy — 3 min
- Arm circles forward and back — 10 each direction
- Band pull-apart or light cable face pull — 15 reps
- Push-up — 8 reps controlled
- DB bent-over row light — 10 reps each side
- Shoulder CARs (controlled articular rotations) — 5 each side

------------------------------------------------------------

STRENGTH BLOCK A — Horizontal Push + Pull | 4 Rounds | 18 min
Superset format. Perform A1 then immediately A2. Rest 90 sec after A2 before returning to A1.

A1 — DB Bench Press 10 x 4 sets @RPE 6
Feet flat. Shoulder blades retracted and depressed. Control the descent — 2 sec down. Press strong.

A2 — DB Bent-Over Row 10 ea x 4 sets @RPE 6
Hinge to 45 degrees. Lead with the elbow, not the hand. Full range — touch the hip at the top.

------------------------------------------------------------

STRENGTH BLOCK B — Shoulder + Mid-Back Accessory | 3 Rounds | 12 min
Superset format. Perform B1 then immediately B2. Rest 75 sec after B2.

B1 — DB Shoulder Press 10 x 3 sets @RPE 6
Seated or standing. Core braced. Press directly overhead — do not flare the ribs.

B2 — Cable Face Pull or Band Pull-Apart 15 x 3 sets @RPE 5
External rotation at the finish. Elbows high. Squeeze the rear delt and hold 1 sec.

------------------------------------------------------------

CONDITIONING — ~10 minutes | Short Aerobic
AMRAP 8

10 Cal Row or Bike
10 Push-Up
10 DB Row (light — each side)

Target: 3–4 rounds at a sustainable pace. Breathing controlled throughout.
Scaling: 8 Cal / knee push-up / reduce DB row to 8 reps.

------------------------------------------------------------

COOL-DOWN — 5 minutes

- Doorway chest stretch or pec minor stretch — 60 sec each side
- Cross-body shoulder stretch — 45 sec each side
- Thread-the-needle thoracic rotation — 8 reps each side
- Nasal breathing — 2 min

SESSION DEBRIEF
Log your RPE for Block A and Block B separately. Note which side felt weaker on the row — that matters next session. If everything felt below RPE 5, we add load in Session 2. Goal is to feel capable and recovered within 2 hours of finishing.
