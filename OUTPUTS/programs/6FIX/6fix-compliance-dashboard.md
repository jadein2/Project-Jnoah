# 6FIX Compliance Dashboard + Metrics System
**Framework:** ADHERE Compliance System
**Last updated:** 2026-05-26

---

## Purpose

Five numbers tell you whether the system is working. Review them every Saturday after check-ins. If any fall below target, adjust the protocol — not the client.

---

## Core Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target | Where to track |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 14 adherence rate | % of clients who hit 5+ of their first 6 activity/habit targets | ≥ 80% | Trainerize habit log |
| 30-day retention | % still actively checking in at Day 30 | ≥ 85% | Check-in submissions |
| Phase 1 completion | % who complete all 6 weeks | ≥ 70% | Trainerize program completion |
| Habit consistency score | Average daily habit completion rate (self-reported) | ≥ 75% | Saturday check-in data |
| Check-in completion rate | % of Saturday check-ins submitted on time | ≥ 90% | Trainerize or manual log |

## Weekly Coach Review (Saturday, after all check-ins)

Takes 10–15 minutes. Run this every week of the cohort.

**Step 1 — Count the numbers**
- How many clients submitted check-ins this week? ÷ total enrolled = check-in rate
- Of those who submitted: average habit completion score
- Any client who missed last week AND this week → trigger escalation

**Step 2 — Flag at-risk clients**
A client is at-risk if ANY of the following:
- Missed 2+ consecutive check-ins
- Habit score below 40% for 2 consecutive weeks
- Stopped logging food after Week 2
- No response to DMs within 48 hours
- Stated they "want to restart" or "fell off"

**Step 3 — Escalation protocol by tier**

| Signal | Response |
|---|---|
| Missed 1 check-in | Send a DM: "Hey — missed you at check-in. How's it going?" |
| Missed 2 consecutive check-ins | Voice message or call: "I noticed you've been quiet. Not here to judge — just checking in. What's going on?" |
| Habit score < 40% for 2 weeks | Re-run friction audit: "What's the one thing that's making this hard right now?" Reduce, don't escalate. |
| Silent for 5+ days | Personal outreach: one genuine message. Then give space. Do not chase repeatedly. |
| Wants to quit | "Let's talk before you decide. 15 minutes. I want to understand what happened." |

**Step 4 — Update your cohort log**
One line per client per week. Format:

`[Name] — W[X] — [habit score]/7 — [check-in: submitted/missed] — [note]`

Example:
`Yana — W3 — 6/7 — submitted — strong week, hit all swaps`
`Fame — W3 — 3/7 — submitted — struggling with meal prep, adjust Week 4`
`Jen — W3 — 0/7 — MISSED — send DM, at-risk`

## Automation Opportunities for Solo Operator

These are the places where a solo operator should systematize to protect coaching time:

| Task | Manual or Automated | How |
|---|---|---|
| Day 1–42 client messages | Automated | Trainerize auto-message cadence |
| Enrollment confirmation email | Automated | Wix email automation |
| Intake form reminder | Automated | Wix email automation (48hr trigger) |
| Welcome Kit email | Automated | Wix email automation (Day -3) |
| Week 3 check-in nudge | Automated | Wix email automation (Day 15) |
| Week 5 upgrade preview | Automated | Wix email automation (Day 29) |
| Day 42 close + upgrade offer | Automated | Wix email automation (Day 42) |
| Saturday check-in reminder | Semi-automated | Trainerize scheduled message or recurring calendar event |
| Weekly Story posts (retention) | Manual (real-time) | 15 min/day — no template needed |
| Weekly feed posts | Pre-scheduled | Meta Business Suite — batch before cohort starts |
| Cohort log update | Manual | 15 min every Saturday after check-ins |
| At-risk client DM | Manual | Triggered by Saturday review — cannot automate judgment |
| Upgrade conversation | Manual | Week 5 + Week 6 — personal, not templated |

**Estimated weekly time commitment (active cohort):**
- Saturday check-ins: 2–3 hours (10–15 min per client)
- Saturday review + log: 15 minutes
- Daily Stories: 15 minutes
- DMs and responses: 20–30 minutes
- At-risk follow-ups: 0–30 minutes depending on cohort
- **Total: 3–4 hours/week** on top of scheduled check-in calls
