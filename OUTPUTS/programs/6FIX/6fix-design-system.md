# 6FIX Design System
**Brand:** Restart Fitness PH
**Program:** 6FIX
**Last updated:** 2026-05-26

---

## Design Philosophy

Calm authority. Not hardcore fitness. Not hype. The design says: "This is structured. This person knows what they're doing."

Clients should look at 6FIX materials and feel clarity — not pressure. The visual identity reinforces the brand message: you don't need more motivation, you need a system.

---

## Visual Identity

**Background:** Beige / warm off-white (#F5F0E8 or similar)
**Primary text:** Dark charcoal / near-black (#1A1A1A)
**Accent:** Warm brown / burnt orange (#C47A3A or similar — matches Season 2 branding)
**Secondary accent:** Deep forest green or dark teal for Phase 2 differentiation (optional)
**Typography:** Clean serif or editorial sans-serif for headlines. Simple, readable sans-serif for body text.

**Avoid:**
- Bright neon colors
- Hardcore gym aesthetics (chains, heavy metal, extreme transformation imagery)
- Overcrowded layouts
- Hype language in design elements
- Stock photo gym images — use real client imagery where possible

**Image style:**
- Real food photography (plated meals, meal prep, clean kitchen settings)
- Real client progress photos (with permission, focused on behavior/lifestyle, not just body)
- Coach imagery: coaching environment, not a posed gym selfie
- Mobile-first framing — all graphics readable on a phone screen

## Canva Template Library

Build these templates once. Reuse every cohort with text/image swaps only.

---

### Template 1 — Weekly Topic Card (Phase 1)
**Size:** 1080×1350px (portrait, Instagram feed)
**Use:** One per week, posted during active cohort

**Layout:**
- Top left: Restart Fitness PH logo (small)
- Top center: "6FIX • Week [X]" pill/badge in accent color
- Headline: Week theme (large, bold — e.g., "Awareness & Tracking")
- Subheadline: Weekly tagline (e.g., "Don't Guess. Learn Yourself.")
- Body: Two-column layout
  - Left: This Week's Habits + Weekly Focus (checklist style)
  - Right: Food/movement image
- Bottom: Weekly Mission box (accent color background) — mission statement + Saturday check-in items
- Bottom strip: hashtag line (#6Fix #restartfitnessph #CoachJap)

**Variables to swap each week:** Week number, theme, tagline, habit list, weekly focus, mission text, image

## Design Production Workflow (Solo Operator)

**Per cohort (one session, ~2 hours):**
1. Open Canva template library
2. Duplicate all 6 Weekly Topic Card templates
3. Update week numbers, themes, habits, images for each
4. Export all 6 as PNG
5. Duplicate pre-launch templates — update dates, season number, investment
6. Export all pre-launch assets
7. Schedule via Meta Business Suite

**Per week during cohort (15 minutes):**
- Post scheduled weekly topic card (already done)
- Create 1–2 real-time Stories (no template needed — just post live)
- No new design work required

**Post-cohort (30 minutes):**
- Create graduation post (testimonial template + cohort results summary)
- Update next cohort announcement template with new dates
- Export and schedule
