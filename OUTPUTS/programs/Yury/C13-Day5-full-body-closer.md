Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Power Clean @empty bar (bar from floor, hip contact, fast elbows)
3 Broad Jump @50% effort (max distance intent, soft landing, full reset)
3 Hang from bar + Toes-to-Bar or Knees-to-Chest (introduce or reinforce the hang position)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — POWER + EXPLOSIVENESS | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 2 min after A2.

A1 — Power Clean 3 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Lighter than Day 1 C&J — the goal here is speed and bar path, not load. Reset the pull mechanics. Record load.

A2 — Broad Jump 5 x 4 sets (max distance)
*Stand behind the line. Full hip extension — jump as far as possible. Land soft with bent knees. Full reset between reps. This is Cycle 13 plyometric baseline — note landing quality.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — PULL + PUSH | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Weighted Chin-Up 5 x 3 sets @8 RPE
*Full dead hang at the bottom. Chin fully over the bar. Add load from the last pull-up session if bodyweight felt under RPE 7.

B2 — Kneeling Landmine Press 10 x 3 sets (each arm) @7–8 RPE
*Half-kneeling position. Core braced — no trunk lean. Full extension overhead. Control the return.

HIP ADDUCTION (Hip Health) | 2 Rounds
Copenhagen Plank Hold 20 sec x 2 sets (each side)
*Lie on your side, top leg on a bench. Inner thigh and obliques create the tension — do not let the hips sag. Different from the machine — same stimulus.

METCON
5 Rounds For Time (20:00 cap)

12 Cal Ski Erg
8 Thrusters @moderate barbell (front squat + push press in one movement)
6 Toes-to-Bar (or Knees-to-Chest)
4 Strict Pull-Ups

*The Ski Erg sets the pace each round. Thrusters — squat drives the bar, arms just finish the press. Don't collapse the squat under fatigue. TTB/KTC — break into manageable sets rather than grinding through. Strict Pull-Ups — same standard as Day 3.
*Cycle closer — log total time + note energy drop between rounds 1–3 and rounds 4–5. That gap is the conditioning baseline for Week 2.
