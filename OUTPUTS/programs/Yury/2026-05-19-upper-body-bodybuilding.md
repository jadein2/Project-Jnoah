STRENGTH BLOCK A — PUSH + PULL COMPOUND | 4 Rounds | ~20 min
Superset format. Perform A1 then immediately A2. Rest 60 sec after A2 before returning to A1.

A1 — Bench Press 10 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Control the descent — 2-count to chest. Press to near lockout. Retract scapula before unracking.

A2 — T-Bar Row 10 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Drive elbows back and up. Chest stays on pad. Full stretch at the bottom.

------------------------------------------------------------

STRENGTH BLOCK B — MACHINE HYPERTROPHY | 3 Rounds | ~18 min
Perform B1 → B2 → B3 → B4 back to back with minimal rest between exercises. Rest 60–90 sec after B4.

B1 — Pec Deck Flye 12 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Full range — feel the stretch at the open position. Slow the eccentric.

B2 — Lat Pulldown (supinated grip) 12 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Initiate with the lats, not the biceps. Pull to upper chest. Control the return.

B3 — Cable Face Pull 15 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Elbows high and flared. Pull to forehead level. Pause at peak contraction.

B4 — Tricep Pushdown 12 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Rope or straight bar. Full extension at the bottom. Squeeze at lockout. Control the return.

*Alternate DB snatch arm each round if grip becomes a limiting factor.
