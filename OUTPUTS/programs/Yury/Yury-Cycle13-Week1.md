### Day 1 — Coached | Olympic Lifting (Clean & Jerk + Front Squat)

Warm up
5:00 Row (moderate)

3 Sets
10 PVC Good Mornings
10 Kang Squats
10 Greatest Stretch (each side)
5 Inch Worms

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
3 Hang Muscle Clean @empty bar (bar to hips, hip contact, shrug, elbows punch through to rack)
3 Front Squat @empty bar (elbows up, full depth)

Build Set
3 Sets
1 Hang Clean + 1 Clean + 1 Jerk — build from empty bar to @65–70%

OLYMPIC LIFTING BLOCK — CLEAN & JERK | Wave Loading
Rest 2–3 min between sets. Record every weight.

@65% — 3 reps x 3 sets
@70% — 2 reps x 3 sets
@75% — 2 reps x 2 sets
@80% — 1 rep x 3 sets
*Cycle 13 baseline. Build to a heavy clean and jerk — stop when mechanics compromise. Log every set.
*Bar starts at the floor. Full squat clean if receiving position is solid; power clean acceptable this week.

STRENGTH BLOCK A — FRONT SQUAT + HIP ABDUCTION | 3 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Front Squat 5 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Elbows up throughout — non-negotiable. Clean rack position. Full depth. Cycle 13 baseline — log the load.

A2 — Hip Abductor Machine 15 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Seated upright. Full range. Controlled return — do not let it slam.

METCON
AMRAP 12

12 Cal Ski Erg (hard, controlled effort)
6 Hang Power Clean @50% of C&J working weight (same bar — strip it down)
9 Front Rack Lunge @same bar (alternating legs)

*Keep bar mechanics clean even when the lungs are burning. Elbows stay up on the lunge. Log rounds + reps.

### Day 2 — Coached | Lower Body Strength

Warm up
5:00 Air Bike (moderate)

3 Sets
5 Inch Worms
10 Kang Squats
10 Greatest Stretch (each side)
10 Hip Bridges
10 Cossack Squats

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Back Squat @empty bar (full depth, bar speed, knees drive out)
5 Romanian Deadlift @empty bar (hinge, soft knee, hamstring stretch)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — SQUAT + HINGE | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Back Squat 5 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Cycle 13 baseline. Full depth. Drive knees out. Record load — this anchors the squat cycle going forward.

A2 — Romanian Deadlift 8 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Slow eccentric — 2-count down. Hamstring stretch is deliberate at the bottom. Bar stays close to the legs.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — LUNGE + POSTERIOR CHAIN + HIP HEALTH | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 → B2 → B3. Rest 60–90 sec after B3.

B1 — Bulgarian Split Squat 10 x 3 sets (each leg) @2xDB @8 RPE
*Rear foot elevated. Step long. Control the descent — knee tracks over the toe.

B2 — Roman Chair Back Extension 12 x 3 sets @45lbs
*Full extension at the top. Controlled return. Do not hyperextend.

B3 — Hip Adductor Machine 15 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Seated upright. Full range. Controlled return.

METCON
4 Rounds For Time (16:00 cap)

15 Cal Air Bike (sprint — all out)
12 KB Swings @24kg
6 Box Jump Step-Down @24in

*Each Air Bike sprint should be near-max. KB Swings are hip-driven — hinge back, drive forward. Box Jumps: full extension at the top, step down after every rep. Round 4 should feel like overtime.
*Log total time.

### Day 3 — Coached | Upper Body Push/Pull

Warm up
5:00 Row (easy)

3 Sets
10 Lu-Raises @5–10lbs
10 Banded Pull-Aparts
5 Inch Worms with Push-Up
10 T-Raises @10lbs

Specific Warm-Up
1 Set
5 Bench Press @empty bar (retract scapula, 2-count descent)
5 T-Bar Row @light (elbows drive back, chest to pad)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — HORIZONTAL PRESS + PULL | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 90 sec after A2.

A1 — Bench Press 5 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Retract scapula before unracking. 2-count descent. Drive the bar up and slightly toward the rack. Full lockout. Cycle 13 baseline — record load.

A2 — T-Bar Row 10 x 4 sets @8 RPE
*Chest to the pad. Drive elbows back past the body. Full stretch at the bottom. No shrugging at the top.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — VERTICAL PUSH + PULL | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Push Press 5 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Cycle 13 baseline overhead. Dip shallow and fast. Drive with the legs. Full lockout overhead.

B2 — Weighted Pull-Up 5 x 3 sets @8 RPE
*Full dead hang at the bottom. Chin fully clears the bar. If unweighted Pull-Up is at RPE 7 or below — add load. Log weight used.

STRENGTH BLOCK C — CHEST + SHOULDER ISOLATION | 3 Rounds
Perform C1 then C2. Rest 60 sec after C2.

C1 — Pec Deck 12 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Slight bend in the elbows. Stretch at the open position. Squeeze hard at the close.

C2 — Cable Face Pull 15 x 3 sets @7 RPE
*Rope at face height. Pull to forehead. External rotation at the end — thumbs back. Slow tempo.

METCON
3 Rounds For Time (14:00 cap)

15 Cal Row (hard effort)
10 Barbell Shoulder-to-Overhead @moderate (push press or jerk — Yury's call)
10 Burpee Box Jump Overs @24in

*S2OH — the legs start every rep. Don't press from a dead stop under fatigue. Burpee Box Jump Overs: chest hits the floor, stand tall, jump over. Pace the row — 3 hard rounds.
*Log total time.

### Day 4 — Coached | Posterior Chain + Conditioning

Warm up
5:00 Air Runner (easy)

3 Sets
10 Kang Squats
10 Greatest Stretch (each side)
10 Good Mornings @empty bar
10 Banded Side Steps (each direction)

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Conventional Deadlift @empty bar (brace, hinge, bar close to shins, lock out tall)
5 Hip Thrust @bodyweight (drive through full foot, squeeze at top)

STRENGTH BLOCK A — PRIMARY HINGE | 4 Rounds
A1 — Conventional Deadlift 5 x 4 sets @8–9 RPE
*Cycle 13 baseline. Full brace before the pull. Bar stays close to the shins the entire way up. Lock out tall — no hyperextension. Record load.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — GLUTE + HIP HEALTH | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 → B2 → B3. Rest 60–90 sec after B3.

B1 — Barbell Hip Thrust 12 x 3 sets @8 RPE
*Drive through the full foot. Squeeze hard at the top. Upper back on the bench — not the neck. Cycle 13 baseline — record load.

B2 — Banded Side Steps 15 x 3 sets (each direction)
*Band above knees. Upright torso. Full range step — don't let the knees cave inward on the return.

B3 — GHD Back Extension 10 x 2 sets @bodyweight–light
*Full hip extension at the top. Controlled descent. 2 sets only — Deadlift and Hip Thrust are already primary posterior chain today.

ACCESSORY | 2 Rounds
C1 — Seated Cable Row 10 x 2 sets @8 RPE
*Drive elbows back. Full scapula retraction. Sit tall.

METCON
3 Rounds For Time (14:00 cap)

300m Air Runner (hard effort)
12 KB Sumo Deadlift High Pull @24kg
12 GHD Sit-Ups

*SDHP: wide stance, drive through the hips and pull high — elbows above the hands at the top. GHD Sit-Ups: full extension at the back, controlled return. Air Runner: pace at 85% — this closes a heavy posterior chain session.
*Log total time.

### Day 5 — Coached | Full Body Closer (Power + Gymnastics)

Warm up
5:00 Air Bike (moderate)

3 Sets
10 Kang Squats
10 Banded Pull-Aparts
5 Inch Worms
10 Hip Bridges
10 Cossack Squats

Specific Warm-Up
2 Sets — building
5 Power Clean @empty bar (bar from floor, hip contact, fast elbows)
3 Broad Jump @50% effort (max distance intent, soft landing, full reset)
3 Hang from bar + Toes-to-Bar or Knees-to-Chest

STRENGTH BLOCK A — POWER + EXPLOSIVENESS | 4 Rounds
Perform A1 then A2. Rest 2 min after A2.

A1 — Power Clean 3 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Lighter than Day 1 C&J — the goal here is speed and bar path, not load. Reset the pull mechanics. Record load.

A2 — Broad Jump 5 x 4 sets (max distance)
*Full hip extension at the top — jump as far as possible. Land soft with bent knees. Full reset between reps. Cycle 13 plyometric baseline — note landing quality.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — PULL + PUSH | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 then B2. Rest 90 sec after B2.

B1 — Weighted Chin-Up 5 x 3 sets @8 RPE
*Full dead hang at the bottom. Chin fully over the bar. Add load if bodyweight felt under RPE 7 on Day 3.

B2 — Kneeling Landmine Press 10 x 3 sets (each arm) @7–8 RPE
*Half-kneeling position. Core braced — no trunk lean. Full extension overhead. Control the return.

HIP ADDUCTION — Hip Health | 2 Rounds
Copenhagen Plank Hold 20 sec x 2 sets (each side)
*Lie on your side, top leg on a bench. Inner thigh and obliques create the tension — do not let the hips sag.

METCON
5 Rounds For Time (20:00 cap)

12 Cal Ski Erg
8 Thrusters @moderate barbell (front squat + push press in one movement)
6 Toes-to-Bar (or Knees-to-Chest)
4 Strict Pull-Ups

*The Ski Erg sets the pace each round. Thrusters — squat drives the bar, arms just finish the press. Don't collapse the squat under fatigue. TTB/KTC — break into sets rather than grinding. Strict Pull-Ups — same standard as Day 3.
*Cycle closer — log total time + note energy drop between rounds 1–3 and rounds 4–5. That gap is the conditioning baseline for Week 2.
