OLYMPIC LIFTING — POWER CLEAN + PUSH JERK | ~25 min
Build by feel. Stop at RPE 9. Do not attempt a true max today.

5 x (2 Power Clean + 1 Push Jerk) @70%
4 x (2 Power Clean + 1 Push Jerk) @75–80%
3 x (1 Power Clean + 1 Push Jerk) @82–85%

*Drive under — not just up. Full catch on the jerk before re-racking.
*Drop the load if bar path drifts forward on consecutive reps.
