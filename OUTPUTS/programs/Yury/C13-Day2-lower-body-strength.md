STRENGTH BLOCK A — SQUAT + HINGE | 4 Rounds

A2 — Romanian Deadlift 8 x 4 sets @7–8 RPE
*Slow eccentric — 2-count down. Hamstring stretch is deliberate at the bottom. Bar stays close to the legs.

STRENGTH BLOCK B — LUNGE + POSTERIOR CHAIN + HIP HEALTH | 3 Rounds
Perform B1 → B2 → B3. Rest 60–90 sec after B3.

B1 — Bulgarian Split Squat 10 x 3 sets (each leg) @2xDB @8 RPE
*Rear foot elevated. Step long. Control the descent — knee tracks over the toe.

B2 — Roman Chair Back Extension 12 x 3 sets @45lbs
*Full extension at the top. Controlled return. Posterior chain pool — do not hyperextend.

B3 — Hip Adductor Machine 15 x 3 sets @7–8 RPE
*Seated upright. Full range. Controlled return.
