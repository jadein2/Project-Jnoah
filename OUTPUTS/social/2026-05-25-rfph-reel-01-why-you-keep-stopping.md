## Concept

**Title:** Why you keep stopping (and starting again)

**Core message:** The problem isn't discipline. It's the absence of structure. When there's no clear path, any obstacle is enough to quit. A system removes the guesswork so the decision to show up is already made.

---

## Hook (0–3 seconds)

**On-screen text:**
"Why do you keep stopping?"

**Voiceover / Spoken:**
"Here's why most people keep restarting the same program over and over."
