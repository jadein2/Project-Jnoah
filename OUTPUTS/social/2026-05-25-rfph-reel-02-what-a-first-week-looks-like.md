## Concept

**Title:** What your first week looks like with a coach

**Core message:** Most people don't know what online coaching actually involves. This reel demystifies the onboarding process — what happens when someone signs up. Transparency builds trust. Shows the system is organized, not random.

## Hook (0–3 seconds)

**On-screen text:**
"What happens when you start coaching?"

**Voiceover / Spoken:**

## Script outline (30–45 seconds)

**Scene 1 — Hook (0–3s)**
Text: "What happens when you start coaching?"
Coach: "Here's exactly what your first week looks like."

**Scene 2 — Step 1: Intake (4–12s)**
Coach: "First — we do a proper intake. Your current habits, schedule, training history, health context. No guessing where you're starting."
Text: "Step 1 — Clarity"
B-roll: intake form, notes, client profile

**Scene 3 — Step 2: Program build (13–22s)**
Coach: "Based on that — I build your program. Not a template. A plan built around what you have, where you're going, and what's realistic."
Text: "Step 2 — Your program is built"
B-roll: program sheet, Trainerize, phone with plan

**Scene 4 — Step 3: First session (23–32s)**
Coach: "Then you start. And I'm checking in — making sure you understand the sessions, adjusting if needed."
Text: "Step 3 — You train. I check in."
B-roll: client training, coach messaging, check-in

**Scene 5 — CTA (33–42s)**
Coach: "That's the start of a system — not a program you're left to figure out alone."
Text: DM "RESTART" to start yours.
Coach: "DM me RESTART."
