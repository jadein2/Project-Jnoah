## Strategy architecture — referral engine first

**Track R — Referral engine (primary).** Sequence:
1. **Jul 1–5 — Completer proof week.** Each completer's story as its own post (with permission): who they are, what their schedule looks like, what changed. Real phrasing from their check-ins, not marketing copy.
2. **Jul 6–12 — Direct activation (low-volume, personal).** Jap personally messages each completer: "Cohort 2 opens — who in your circle needs this? Buddy mechanic: 10% off at Week 3 for both of you." Completers invite; the campaign doesn't broadcast this — it equips them. (Batched/scheduled around Grand Opening week.)
3. **Jul 13–22 — Public conversion window.** Spots framing (real scarcity, stated once per conversion post), schedule-fit messaging, buddy mechanic public, DM "6FIX" deadline Jul 22 visible.

**Track B — Cold seeding (secondary, low effort).** 2 Threads long-forms + 1 carousel on the schedule-fit problem ("the program didn't fail because you're lazy — it failed because it assumed a life you don't have"). No enrollment pressure; builds the audience that converts in Cohort 3.

**Enrollment flow:** DM "6FIX" → overview + onboarding call invite (Jul 24) → attends call (orientation, baseline, offer) → pays by Jul 25 → Trainerize invite (/ENGINES/6fix/ onboarding system + Phase 2 intake forms already built) → starts Jul 27.
