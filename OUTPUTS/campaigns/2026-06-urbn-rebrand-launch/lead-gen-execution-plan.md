# URBN Athletics — 3-Week Lead-Gen → Member Execution Plan

**Window:** Soft Opening (Jun 11) → ~Grand Opening (Jul 11) · **Goal:** acquire leads → convert to paying Founding Members.
**Strategy base:** `lead-to-member-strategy-framework.md` · **Ads:** `video-ads-plan.md` · **Pricing:** `pricing-plan.md`
**Coverage:** Jap + employee, **8am–6pm** live follow-up · after-hours = auto-reply + 8am action.
**Locked offers:** Founding Member status · Value stack (PFT/InBody + nutrition guide + friend pass) · Show-Up Guarantee (show-up-or-free) · 6-Week Kickstart Challenge. **20% = deadline, not the hook.**

---

## 1. The finalized offer (one clear path)

**The pitch:** *"Try us free → join the Founding 15 → start your 6-Week Kickstart, risk-free."*

### A. Founding Member (the status play)
- **First 15 only** (ties to the Pioneer cap), locks at Grand Opening.
- Gets: **Pioneer locked rate** · Founding badge + name on the wall · priority class booking · **Kickstart included** · founding-member events · **Founding shirt at Grand Opening**.
- Why it works: people pay to belong early — sell the *status*, not the discount.

### B. Value stack (join during Soft Opening → get more, not cheaper)
- **Free PFT / InBody** baseline + a progress re-test.
- **Nutrition starter guide** (simple URBN one-pager).
- **Friend guest pass** (bring someone — referral engine).
- *(Founding shirt drops at Grand Opening — tease it now.)*

### C. Show-Up Guarantee (kills the risk)
- **"Show up 8× in your first 30 days. If you do and don't feel stronger or move better — your next month is on us."**
- Rewards the behavior that drives results + retention; near-zero risk to you (8× attendees almost always stay).

### D. 6-Week Kickstart Challenge (the results engine)
- **Structure:** PFT/InBody → simple plan → coached classes → **weekly check-ins** → private group + **leaderboard** → before/after photos+measurements → **finish-line day**.
- **Who:** beginners route in via FIT45/On-Ramp; performance via HYBRID/PFT.
- **Positioning:** included for Founding Members who join during Soft Opening → urgency + proof content for future ads.
- **Cohort 1 start:** ~Jun 16 (after first trials close). Keep cohorts rolling.

## 2. The trial SOP (2 sessions, engineered to convert)

> Target: 30–45% trial→paid. The arc matters more than the offer.

**Booking → Pre-session:** capture lead → **<5-min reply** → book both sessions up front → send a **10–15 min intake** (goals, history, fears) by DM or on arrival.

**Session 1 — the win.**
- Warm welcome by name; quick tour. Scale everything so they get a **"that felt good / I can do this"** moment.
- Coach connects 1:1; introduce them to **1–2 members** (belonging).
- Do NOT pitch. End with: *"Next session we'll build on this."*

**Between (same day):** thank-you text + a single tip + Session 2 reminder.

**Session 2 — the path + the close.**
- Reference their goal from intake; show the **plan** and where Kickstart takes them.
- **Membership conversation at the END** (not earlier): present the path (FIT45 → On-Ramp → URBN FIT / HYBRID) + **Founding 15** + **value stack** + **guarantee** + **20% Soft-Opening deadline**. **Ask for the sale.**
- Handle objections (price → reframe to result + guarantee; time → schedule fit; nerves → On-Ramp).

**After:** if closed → onboarding (below). If not → nurture sequence (§3).

## 3. Follow-up sequences (templates)

**Response rule:** in-hours (8–6) reply **<5 min** — both Jap + employee notified. **After-hours:** auto-reply → *"Thanks for reaching out to URBN Athletics! We'll lock your free trial slot first thing at 8 AM. — Reply with your name + preferred time."* → action at 8am.

| Trigger | Timing | Message (seed) |
|---|---|---|
| New lead (form/DM) | <5 min | "Hey [name]! Excited to get you in. Your free 2-session trial is FIT45 or URBN HYBRID — which sounds more you, and what days work? We'll lock it in." |
| Booked confirm | on book | "You're in for [day/time]. Come 10 min early — we'll do a quick goals chat. What's the #1 thing you want from training right now?" |
| After Session 1 | same day | "Great first session, [name] — you moved well on [X]. Tomorrow/[next] we build on it. 💪 (note: no emoji unless Jap uses) See you [time]." |
| Day before Session 2 | −1 day | "Reminder: Session 2 [time]. After this you'll have a clear plan + your options as a Founding member (only 15 spots)." |
| After Session 2 (no close) | same day | "Loved having you. Here's your path + the Founding offer [recap]. 15 spots, locks at Grand Opening — want me to hold yours?" |
| Nurture (no reply) | Day 3 | Value + soft nudge: a quick win/tip + "still keen to start? happy to hold a Founding spot." |
| Last call | Day 5–7 | Scarcity: "[X] of 15 Founding spots left before Grand Opening — want in?" |
| No-show | within 1 hr | "Missed you today — life happens. Want to grab another slot this week?" |

*(Strip emoji per brand rule unless Jap uses them. Keep the friend-pass mention in the welcome onboarding so new members refer immediately.)*
