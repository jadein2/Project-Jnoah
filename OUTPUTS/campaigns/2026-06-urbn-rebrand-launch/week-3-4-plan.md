## Week 3 — HYBRID + PFT, sharpened (Jun 15–21)

Purpose: convert the validated segment — gym-experienced, Hyrox-curious, referred — from free trial into PFT and HYBRID membership. Every post this week speaks to someone who already trains.

**Offer naming rule (Jap, 2026-06-12):** Founding Member Rate = previous Urban Strong Fitness clients only (comeback offer). Pioneer rate = new members from soft-opening marketing onward. Never conflate. Founding posts speak to old clients by name ("if you trained with us as Urban Strong…"); Pioneer posts speak to new prospects.

Operational: Jun 20–21 is the move-out window — both posts pre-scheduled by Jun 18, no live dependencies. Never imply official HYROX affiliation; "Hyrox-style preparation" and "fitness racing" are the approved phrasings.

Referral mechanic for the week: any current member who brings a friend to a trial gets named recognition in stories (with permission). Low cost, feeds the proven channel.
