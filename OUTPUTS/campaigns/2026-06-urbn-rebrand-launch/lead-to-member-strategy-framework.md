# URBN Athletics — Lead → Member Strategy Framework

**Purpose:** Organize the thinking before we research deep + build the execution plan.
**Window:** This week trials are live · next 3 weeks = acquire leads → convert to paying members.
**Created:** 2026-06-03 · Status: FRAMING (decisions at the end gate the execution plan)

---

## 0. The reframe (the core idea)
**Discount is the deadline, not the hook.** Keep the 20% Soft Opening purely as urgency ("locks at Grand Opening"). The *reason to join* is a result + belonging + coaching + a risk-free path. Sell the identity — *the version of them who moves better* — not a lower price. Leading with discount trains price-shoppers and cheapens a premium rebrand. Lead with **value + result**; let the discount close.

## 1. The funnel (next 3 weeks)
**ACQUIRE → CAPTURE → TRIAL (2 sessions) → CLOSE → ONBOARD.**

| Stage | What happens | Owner | Win condition |
|---|---|---|---|
| Acquire | Video ads + organic (Soft Opening) | Ads | Cheap qualified leads |
| Capture | Lead form / DM, **<5-min** first reply | Employee | Booked trial |
| Trial | 2 sessions, designed to convert | Coach | Show + "aha" moment |
| Close | Membership convo at **end of session 2** | Coach/Jap | Paid member |
| Onboard | Day 1/3/7 touchpoints, community | Team | Retained, refers |

**KPIs:** lead count · trial show-rate · **trial→paid % (target 30–45% with a real process vs 10–20% without)** · first-response time (<5 min ≈ ~100× connect odds).

## 2. Offer architecture (non-discount-first)

### A. Hook offer (what the ad sells — top of funnel)
Sell **experience + a plan**, not "free stuff":
- **Free 2-session trial** (FIT45 / URBN HYBRID) **+ a free assessment** → "come in, get a plan, feel a win."
- Beginner framing: **"Start Line Session"** — assessment + first coached session + a simple 7-day starter plan.

### B. Conversion offers — the non-discount menu (what closes at trial end)
> The ideas you asked for — motivators that aren't price cuts.

1. **Status / scarcity — Founding Member.** Lean into the *status* of Pioneer, not the %: first 15, founding badge, name on the wall, priority booking, "founding member" identity. People pay to belong to something early.
2. **Challenge / transformation — "URBN 6-Week Kickstart."** Coached challenge: assessment → plan → weekly check-ins → leaderboard → before/after → finish-line. Sells the *result* + community + urgency. A premium add-on, not a discount.
3. **Risk reversal — "Show-Up Guarantee."** Commit to 8 sessions in month one; show up and don't feel stronger/better → next month free (or money back). Removes the risk *without dropping the price*.
4. **Value stack — "more, not cheaper."** Join during Soft Opening → get a stack: free PFT/InBody, branded shirt, 1 free PT session, nutrition starter guide, a guest pass for a friend. Raises perceived value, protects price.
5. **Community / belonging — bring-a-friend, accountability pods, member spotlights, founding events.** Belonging is the #1 retention driver and a referral engine.
6. **Gamification / momentum — attendance streaks, milestone rewards, a 14-day check-in challenge** spanning trial → first month.
7. **Identity — "Built to Move Better."** The whole message: become someone who trains. Better coaching, a positive community, a high standard, a better space.

**Recommended spine:** lead with **#2 Kickstart Challenge + #1 Founding status + #3 Guarantee**, sweeten the "join now" with **#4 value stack**, and keep **20% as the Soft-Opening deadline only.**

> **LOCKED 2026-06-03 (Jap):** Build around **all four** — Founding Member status · Value stack · Show-Up Guarantee · 6-Week Kickstart Challenge. Guarantee = **show-up-or-free** (must attend 8× to qualify). 20% stays the deadline, not the hook.

### C. The 2 free trial sessions ("culturize them") — designed to convert
- **Before:** 10–15 min intake convo (goals, history, fears). Personalizes + triggers reciprocity.
- **Session 1:** engineer a **win / "aha"** + coach connection + introduce 1–2 members (belonging).
- **Between:** same-day text + "here's what we'd work on next."
- **Session 2:** show a glimpse of the plan/progress, then have the **membership conversation at the end** (never pitch early — too pushy; never after — urgency gone).
- **Close:** present the path (FIT45 → On-Ramp → URBN FIT / HYBRID) + value stack + founding status + guarantee + the deadline. Ask for the sale.
- **Follow-up:** <5-min first reply to every lead; structured Day 1 / 3 / 7; non-closers → nurture sequence.

## 3. How the video ad must work (creative principle)
**Hook (first 2s: pain or identity) → show people + experience (belonging) → the offer (free trial + plan) → CTA (sign up, pick a time).** Discount appears only as a small deadline line — never the headline. Each class ad = its own audience + offer (see `video-ads-plan.md`).

## 4. Why this approach (research-backed)
- **Follow-up & speed beat trial type** — responding within 5 min ≈ ~100× more likely to connect.
- A **structured trial arc converts 30–45%** vs 10–20% with no process.
- **Value-add > discount:** members "feel they're getting more, not that the gym is worth less"; **challenges** drive urgency + retention; **founding/bonus stacks** convert without eroding price or brand.

## 5. Decisions that gate the execution plan
1. **Which conversion offers do we build around?** (Kickstart Challenge · Founding status · Guarantee · Value stack · Bring-a-friend)
