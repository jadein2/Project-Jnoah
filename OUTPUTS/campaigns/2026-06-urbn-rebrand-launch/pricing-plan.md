## 1. The three-tier offer ladder

| Tier | What it is | Discount | Who qualifies | When |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Free Trial** | Try-before-you-buy | Free (2 sessions) | New leads | Now → ongoing |
| **Soft Opening Promo** | Early-bird sign-up rate | **20% off list** | Anyone who signs up in the soft-opening window | **Jun 11 – Jul 10** |
| **Grand Opening Pioneer** | Locked loyalty rate | **30% off list** | First **15** Soft Opening signees · + legacy Urban Strong members | Locks **Jul 11** |

The lever: **sign up during Soft Opening at 20% → be one of the first 15 → lock the 30% Pioneer rate for as long as you stay a member.** Scarcity (15 seats) + a deadline (Grand Opening) drive June sign-ups.

## 2. Free Trial (entry offer)

- Applies to **FIT45 and URBN HYBRID only** — never URBN FIT.
- **2 sessions**, must be **booked in advance** before attending the trial class.
- Must be used within **1 week** · non-transferable · non-extendable.
- After the trial, the person can **join regular classes on the June schedule.**
- The June schedule is **subject to change with early notice** to ongoing clients if a class or track is changed or a class is added for a program/track.

## 5. Pioneer rules (retain / forfeit)

- Pioneer is a **locked rate**, not a one-time discount — it holds for as long as the member stays active.
- **Legacy Urban Strong members** carry into Pioneer automatically, on one condition: they **do not stop their membership unless there is a valid reason**, and they **must inform admin / reception** of that reason. Lapse without a valid, reported reason → Pioneer forfeited, member returns to list price.
- **New Pioneer seats are capped at 15** and reserved for Soft Opening signees. Once the 15 are filled, new sign-ups get list pricing (or the standard post-launch rate).
- Pioneer urgency in all copy points to **Grand Opening (Jul 11)**: "Sign up before Grand Opening to lock Pioneer pricing — 15 seats."
