## Per-day caption direction

- **Mon (On-Ramp):** Lead with the beginner fear ("I'm not fit enough to start"). On-Ramp removes it — learn the movements, build a base, then join class. Outcome-first, reassuring.
- **Tue (Countdown):** Short, punchy, date-forward. Build anticipation. Mention Free Trial is open for FIT45 & URBN HYBRID.
- **Wed (Pioneer comeback):** Speak directly to former members. Warm, "the door's open," + scarcity (15 seats, locks at Grand Opening). CTA: ask about Pioneer Price.
- **Thu (Soft Opening):** The hero post. Same coaching-first community, now better — better coaching and client experience, a positive community, a high standard, a better space. Invite everyone in.
- **Fri (Pathway):** Simple visual logic. Removes "which one am I?" confusion. Beginners → FIT45/On-Ramp; ready → URBN FIT/HYBRID.
- **Sat (On-Ramp schedule):** Practical. Times + how to start. Reinforce 20% is live.
- **Sun (Roundup):** Use the 3-pillar services overview (Classes / Open Gym / Private Training). Good catch-all for new followers from the week.
