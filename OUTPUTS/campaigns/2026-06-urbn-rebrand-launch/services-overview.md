## Full version

**URBN ATHLETICS — What We Offer**
*Built to Move Better*

### Functional & Hybrid Training Classes
Coached group classes that build strength, conditioning, and movement that holds up in real life.

- **FIT45** — 45-minute, beginner-friendly functional training. Low-impact, scalable, fully coached. The best place to start.
- **On-Ramp (Foundation Course)** — your bridge into the bigger classes. Learn the movements and build a base, 1-on-1 or small group, before you join.
- **URBN FIT** — the main coached class. Structured strength and conditioning with real progression, accountability, and a community that shows up.
- **URBN HYBRID** — endurance meets strength. Fitness-racing and HYROX-style conditioning for people who want a performance edge.

### Open Gym
Train on your own terms. Use the floor and equipment at your own pace in a coached space, with our team nearby when you need a cue. Drop in by the session or roll it into your membership.

### Private Training
Coaching focused entirely on you and your goals.

- **Personal Training** — one-on-one, fully coached, the fastest path to results.
- **Small Group Training** — semi-private coaching for 2–4 people. A coach's attention with the energy of a small crew.

**Try us free** — Free Trial on FIT45 and URBN HYBRID, 2 sessions booked in advance. Walk in and our team sets you up.
