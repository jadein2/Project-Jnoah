## 1. Campaign Objective

June is the public rebrand, soft-opening, and lead-generation month for URBN Athletics.

The goal is to make the market understand that Urban Strong Fitness is becoming URBN Athletics without making current members feel like the gym is changing direction.

The campaign must communicate:

- Same coaching
- Same community
- Same standard
- Sharper name
- Clearer coaching-first position
- Stronger entry paths through FIT45, Free Trial, On-Ramp, Pioneer rate, and PFT

The commercial goal is to support the move from 44 current members toward the 100-member target by creating qualified leads, converting beginners through FIT45 and On-Ramp, attracting fitness-background leads through URBN HYBRID and PFT, and bringing old members back through the Pioneer Price comeback offer.

## 2. June Brand Rules

Use **URBN Athletics** as the public brand.

Use **Urban Strong Fitness** only when explaining the origin story or rebrand transition.

Use **Built to Move Better** as the core line across announcement, website, bio, social, and campaign assets.

Every June post should support at least one of these messages:

- Coaching over access
- Built to Move Better
- Results need structure
- Fitness for busy adults
- Community with standards
- Test before Hybrid

Do not use:

- Hardcore intimidation
- Cheap gym positioning
- Random WOD language for cold audiences
- Quick-fix fat loss claims
- Final logo, palette, or typography claims that have not been approved

Temporary visual direction:

- Clean black and white base
- URBN Athletics wordmark placeholder
- Built to Move Better tagline
- Strong real gym photography
- Minimal layout
- One clear CTA per asset

## 3. Monthly Goals

## Brand Awareness

- Make the rebrand clear to current members and local prospects.
- Build public familiarity with URBN Athletics.
- Explain why Built to Move Better fits the coaching system.

## Lead Generation

- Drive inquiries to Free Assessment.
- Drive beginner leads to FIT45 and the FIT45 waitlist.
- Drive qualified class leads to On-Ramp.
- Drive fitness-background leads to URBN HYBRID and PFT.
- Create urgency around the limited Pioneer rate.
- Bring old members back through the Pioneer Price comeback offer.

## Conversion

- Move prospects from inquiry to assessment, On-Ramp, PFT, or membership recommendation.
- Present price after goal, readiness, and pathway are understood.
- Keep the sales message focused on coaching, structure, and accountability.

## Retention

- Use monthly member assessment content to reduce churn.
- Show progress, coaching, member support, and Ascension Day milestones.
- Reassure members during the rebrand and move-out period.

## 9. Lead Capture Flow

## Step 1: Inquiry

Lead asks about classes, price, schedule, On-Ramp, Pioneer rate, or PFT.

Reception or page handler asks:

> What is your main goal right now: fat loss, strength, health, consistency, or performance?

## Step 2: Qualify Pathway

Use the answer to guide the next step:

- Beginner or inconsistent: FIT45 waitlist, FIT45 Free Trial, or Free Assessment
- Interested in URBN FIT: Free Assessment, then On-Ramp / Foundation Course
- Fitness background: URBN HYBRID Free Trial or Free PFT
- Price-aware but qualified: Pioneer rate explanation after pathway fit
- Current member: Monthly member assessment

## Step 3: Recommend

Recommended script:

> Based on your goal and training background, your best starting point is either FIT45, URBN HYBRID, or a Free Assessment for URBN FIT. If you want to join URBN FIT or URBN HYBRID as a member, we will guide you through the Foundation Course first so you are ready for class.

## Step 4: Book

Collect:

- Name
- Main goal
- Training background
- Preferred schedule
- Phone or Messenger contact
- Chosen pathway
- Assigned follow-up owner

## Step 5: Follow Up

Follow-up message:

> Just checking in. You mentioned your goal is to get stronger and stay consistent. The Free Assessment is the best first step because we can check where you are now and recommend the safest starting point. Would you like to book this week?

## 10. Offer Priorities

## Free Assessment

Primary lead conversion step for general prospects.

Use when:

- Prospect is unsure
- Prospect asks price first
- Prospect has a health, weight loss, or consistency goal
- Prospect needs a recommended starting point

## On-Ramp

Foundation Course required before URBN FIT or URBN HYBRID.

Use when:

- Prospect wants to join URBN FIT
- Prospect wants to join URBN HYBRID
- Prospect needs movement foundations before class
- Coach recommends foundations before regular training

On-Ramp paths:

- 1-on-1 On-Ramp: 8 sessions total; first 4 are coached 1-on-1 foundation sessions, remaining 4 are transition / merging sessions into group class
- Group On-Ramp: 4 foundation sessions following the set schedule

Known pricing reference:

- Group Class On-Ramp: PHP 3,000
- Specific Coach and Time Slot On-Ramp: PHP 4,500

Group On-Ramp schedule:

- Tuesday 6:00 PM
- Thursday 6:00 PM
- Saturday 10:00 AM

## FIT45

Low-barrier beginner hook program.

Use when:

- Prospect is new to fitness
- Prospect is inactive or returning after a long break
- Prospect feels intimidated by regular class
- Prospect wants low-impact daily movement
- Prospect wants a simple habit-building program

Known pricing reference:

- FIT45 Monthly: PHP 2,500

Known schedule reference:

- Monday to Friday
- 6:30 AM tentative
- 3:15 PM tentative

Free Trial applies to FIT45:

- 2 sessions only
- Must be used within 1 week
- Cannot be transferred
- Cannot be extended

FIT45 clients can move up to URBN FIT after completing On-Ramp / Foundation Course.

## Pioneer Rate

Limited early-member and old-member comeback offer.

Known pricing reference:

- Pioneer Monthly: PHP 3,000
- Pioneer Annual: PHP 30,000
- Limited to 20 members

Use for:

- Sign-ups before Grand Opening
- Old-member comeback offer
- Qualified early joiners

Do not sell this without clear rules for retaining or forfeiting Pioneer status.

## URBN FIT

Main coached group class.

Use when:

- Prospect wants the main class
- Prospect wants strength and conditioning
- Prospect wants coaching, structure, and consistency
- Prospect has completed On-Ramp / Foundation Course

Known pricing reference:

- Regular Annual: PHP 35,000

- Student Monthly: PHP 3,000
- Student Daily: PHP 400

Free Trial does not apply to URBN FIT.

## URBN HYBRID / PFT

Performance lead path for people with fitness background.

Use when:

- Prospect already trains
- Prospect wants URBN HYBRID
- Prospect wants higher intensity
- Prospect is interested in fitness racing or HYROX-style training

- URBN HYBRID Monthly: PHP 3,000

Free Trial applies to URBN HYBRID:
