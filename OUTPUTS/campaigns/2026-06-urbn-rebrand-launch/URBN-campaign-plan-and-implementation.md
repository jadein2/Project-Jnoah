# PART 1: Analysis of this week's campaign

## What is strong
- A clear ladder and three pillar story (Functional and Hybrid Classes, Open Gym, Private Training).
- Pricing locked across regular, Soft Opening 20%, and Pioneer 30%, with Open Gym added.
- A smart reframe: discount is the deadline, not the hook. Conversion is built on a value stack, Founding status, a show-up guarantee, and a 6-Week Kickstart.
- A real asset to advertise: one edited 63 second video that splits cleanly into three class ads.
- Content drafted: FIT45 and URBN HYBRID captions, programs auto-reply, tarp copy, posters, services overview, member update.

## Gaps and what we can improve (ranked)
1. **Measurement is missing.** The campaign-performance log is empty and there is no live lead tracker, no cost per lead, no trial show or close rate. We are flying blind. **Fix:** stand up a tracker and instrument every source before spend starts.
2. **Ads are planned but not produced.** The video is not cut, end cards are not built, no campaign is live. **Fix:** a 2 day production sprint.
3. **Follow-up is a plan, not a system.** Sub 5 minute response is the top lever, but there are no saved replies loaded, no printed close script, and coverage is only 8am to 6pm. **Fix:** operationalize before leads arrive, plus an after-hours auto-reply.
4. **Thin channel mix.** We rely on IG and FB organic plus planned paid. Missing: Google Business Profile, member referral engine, on-site walk-by capture, and reviews. **Fix:** add local owned and earned channels (high return, low cost).
5. **No proof yet.** The gym just opened, so there are no testimonials or before and after. Cold ads convert worse without proof. **Fix:** capture Kickstart wins in week B and feed them into week B and Grand Opening ads.
6. **Consistency debt.** Some materials still say "PFT" instead of the public "URBN Hybrid Test," and earlier copy overused dashes. **Fix:** one consistency sweep.
7. **Open decisions are blocking execution.** AM or PM on the new class times, PT and Small Group pricing, Open Gym promo eligibility, and ad budget are all unresolved. **Fix:** close these this weekend.
8. **No retargeting.** We are not capturing video viewers or page engagers into custom audiences for cheap re-touch. **Fix:** set up custom audiences at launch.

# PART 2: Campaign brief

## 1. Campaign overview
- **Name:** "Founding 15: Built to Move Better."
- **Summary:** Convert Soft Opening interest into paying Founding Members before the 15 spots close at Grand Opening.
- **Primary objective (SMART):** enroll **15 Founding Members** and grow from 44 toward 100 members, by **Jul 11**, at a trial to paid rate of **30 to 45%**.
- **Secondary:** book a steady flow of free trials, build a warm retargeting audience, and bank proof content for Grand Opening.

## 2. Target audience
- **Primary:** busy adults 28 to 55 in Olongapo and within 10 to 15 km.
  - Beginners and returners (FIT45): pain is intimidation and not knowing where to start.
  - Performance minded who already train (URBN HYBRID): want endurance plus strength, HYROX-style.
  - Structure seekers (URBN FIT): tired of random workouts, want coaching and progression.
- **Secondary:** former Urban Strong members (Pioneer comeback) and friends of current members (referral).
- **Where they are:** Instagram and Facebook, local community groups, word of mouth, walk-by at the gym, Google search and Maps.
- **Buying stage:** mostly consideration. The trial moves them to decision.

## 3. Key messages
- **Core:** The gym you trust, now better. Coaching, structure, and a clear path, not just access.
- **Supporting:**
  1. Start where you are. FIT45 makes beginning simple and coached. Proof: beginner friendly, low impact, 45 minutes.
  2. Train for real performance. URBN HYBRID builds engine and strength. Proof: the free URBN Hybrid Test shows where you stand.
  3. Coaching beats access. URBN FIT gives structure, progression, and accountability. Proof: a coach with every class, a real plan.
  4. Join the Founding 15. Lock a founding rate and perks before Grand Opening. Proof: only 15 spots, show-up-or-free guarantee removes the risk.
- **By channel:** ads stay short and hook first; DMs carry the offer stack and the close; member comms are warm and community led.

## 4. Channel strategy
- **Owned (do first, low cost):** IG and FB organic (the drafted content), member referral engine (friend guest pass), Google Business Profile (hours, photos, reviews link), on-site tarp and front-desk price list, member comms for the new schedule. Effort: low to medium.
- **Paid:** Meta lead-form ads, the three class videos, Olongapo plus 10 to 15 km. Consider TikTok for the HYBRID ad. Effort: medium. Budget needed.
- **Earned:** member word of mouth, ask happy trial-goers for a Google review, local community partnerships. Effort: low to medium.
- **Retargeting:** custom audiences from video viewers and page engagers for cheap re-touch ads.

## 6. Content pieces needed
Must-have: 3 cut ads plus end cards, Founding offer sheet, 6-Week Kickstart one-pager, show-up guarantee terms, nutrition starter guide, new class-times graphic, free trial slots graphic, Founding 15 scarcity graphic, lead and KPI tracker, saved-reply set, printed close script.
Nice-to-have: TikTok cut of the HYBRID ad, Google Business Profile refresh, member referral graphic, proof or testimonial template.

## 7. Success metrics
- **Primary KPI:** Founding Members enrolled (target 15 by Jul 11).
- **Secondary:** total leads, cost per lead, trial show rate, trial to paid rate (30 to 45%), members added (toward 100), first-response time (under 5 minutes), Founding spots left.
- **Tracking:** lead and KPI tracker, per-ad UTM and Meta reporting, daily count of spots left.
- **Cadence:** quick daily check on leads and spend; Sunday review to reallocate.

## 8. Budget allocation (pending your number)
No budget set yet. Framework when you give one: put 60 to 70% on the best performing ad, hold 15 to 20% for testing TikTok or new creative, keep 10 to 15% contingency. Account for any production or print costs (tarp, shirts later). Recommend a small daily test first, then scale the winner.

## 9. Risks and mitigations
- **Production lag** (ads or assets slip). Mitigation: a fixed 2 day sprint this weekend, assets batched.
- **Follow-up gaps** (leads outside 8am to 6pm, or slow reply). Mitigation: after-hours auto-reply, saved replies, a named owner per slot.
- **Schedule disruption** (move-out Jun 20 to 21, HYROX Jun 27 to 29). Mitigation: pre-schedule those days now.
- **Over-discounting** (price becomes the story). Mitigation: keep 20% as the deadline, lead with the value stack and Founding status.
- **No proof yet.** Mitigation: capture Kickstart wins fast and put them into week B ads.
