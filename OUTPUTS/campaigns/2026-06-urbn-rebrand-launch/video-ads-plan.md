## 2. Strategy in one line
Cut the one 63s master into **three 9:16 vertical ads (15–25s each)**, one per class — each with its **own audience, hook, on-screen text, and offer.** Same brand world, three doors in. Research is clear: segmented ads beat one generic message, and the **offer is the biggest lever**.

## 3. Why this approach (research-backed)
- **Vertical, short, captioned** Reels-format video is the top performer; 15–30s for cold audiences.
- **Lead with the offer**, not "join our gym" — a specific, low-risk trial offer wins.
- **Authentic > polished** — real members and real gym beat stock/studio.
- **Segment by audience** — beginners, performance, and structure are different buyers.
- **Speed of follow-up** matters most after the click: contact leads within ~5 min (9× conversion). Your walk-in/employee flow already supports this.

## 4. The three ads

### AD 1 — FIT45 · "Start where you are"
- **Audience:** beginners, returning-after-a-break, intimidated by gyms; busy adults 28–50; all genders. The "I'm not fit enough to start" crowd.
- **Angle:** remove intimidation. Coaching + community + low-impact. No performance pressure.
- **Footage:** huddle (0:00–0:08) → floor core (0:10–0:16) → push-ups (0:36–0:42). End on a smile / coach cue.
- **Hook (first 2s, on-screen + caption):** "You don't need to be fit to start."
- **On-screen beats:** "FIT45 — beginner-friendly." → "45 minutes. Low-impact. Coach-led." → "You won't do it alone."
- **Primary text:** New to fitness, or starting over? FIT45 is a 45-minute coached class built for beginners — simple, low-impact, and done with people just like you. No experience needed. Come try it free.
- **Headline:** Try FIT45 free · **CTA:** Reserve a Free Trial (FIT45).
- **Offer:** Free Trial (2 sessions). Soft Opening tie-in.

### AD 2 — URBN HYBRID · "Train like a hybrid athlete"
- **Audience:** people who already train — runners, endurance/functional, HYROX-curious; 22–45; performance over aesthetics.
- **Angle:** performance and intensity. Endurance + strength. HYROX-**style** racing prep (no official HYROX claim).
- **Footage:** rowing/bikes (0:18–0:24) → rig pull-ups (0:44–0:52) → fast cuts, higher energy.
- **Hook:** "Think you're ready?" / "Endurance meets strength."
- **On-screen beats:** "URBN HYBRID." → "Strength + endurance. HYROX-style." → "Test your fitness — free PFT."
- **Primary text:** Already train? URBN HYBRID is strength and endurance built for fitness racing and HYROX-style conditioning. Find out where you stand with a free Physical Fitness Test — then train with a crew that pushes.
- **Headline:** Take the free PFT · **CTA:** Reserve a Free Trial / Join the Free PFT.
- **Offer:** Free Trial (HYBRID) + Free PFT lead magnet.

### AD 3 — URBN FIT · "More than random workouts"
- **Audience:** wants structure, coaching, accountability, progression — beyond a commercial-gym membership; 28–50; consistency-seekers, professionals/parents.
- **Angle:** coaching and structure. Real progression and community. The main class.
- **Footage:** strength/overhead (0:28–0:34) → rig pull-ups (0:44–0:52) → group energy.
- **Hook:** "Tired of guessing at the gym?"
- **On-screen beats:** "URBN FIT — coached strength & conditioning." → "Structure. Progression. Accountability." → "Built to Move Better."
- **Primary text:** If random workouts aren't getting you anywhere, URBN FIT is coached strength and conditioning with real structure and progression — in a community that shows up. Start with a free assessment and we'll map your path.
- **Headline:** Book a free assessment · **CTA:** **Book a Free Assessment** (NOT a free trial).
- ⚠️ **Rule:** Free Trial does **not** apply to URBN FIT. URBN FIT ads use Free Assessment → On-Ramp. Keep the free-trial language off this one.

## 5. Production specs (all three)
- **Aspect:** 9:16 (1080×1920) primary; also export 1:1 (1080×1080) for Feed. Reframe the 16:9 master (punch-in / reframe key subjects).
- **Length:** 15–25s. Hook in the **first 2 seconds** (text + motion). Designed sound-off → **burned-in captions/text** required.
- **Brand:** `URBN Athletics` wordmark; end card with **Built to Move Better** + the CTA.
- **One CTA per ad.** **No peso prices** on the creative. Soft Opening / scarcity allowed as text.
- **End card CTA frame** per ad (Reserve a Free Trial / Join the PFT / Book a Free Assessment) + "Olongapo · Soft Opening June 11."
- **Deliverables per class:** 1× 9:16 (15s), 1× 9:16 (25s), 1× 1:1 (15s) = flexibility for testing.
