## Objective

Public rebrand + soft-opening + lead-generation month. Make the market understand Urban Strong Fitness is becoming URBN Athletics **without** making current members feel the gym is changing direction. Commercial goal: move from **44 → 100 members** by generating qualified leads and converting through FIT45, On-Ramp, URBN HYBRID/PFT, and the Pioneer comeback offer.

## Audience segments → primary CTA

- Busy adults → Book a Free Assessment
- Beginners / inconsistent → Join the FIT45 waitlist
- URBN FIT prospects → Book a Free Assessment (then On-Ramp)
- Fitness-background leads → Try URBN HYBRID / Free PFT
- Price-aware qualified → Ask about Pioneer Price
- Current members → Book monthly member assessment
- Old members → Come back with Pioneer Price
