## Day-of conversion mechanics (Jul 11)

The event converts through booked next steps, not day-of hard selling:

1. **Sign-in sheet** — name, goal, training background, contact (same fields as lead capture flow §9). Every walk-in becomes a tracked lead.
2. **Trial booking station** — book a Free Trial (FIT45/HYBRID) or Free Assessment on the spot, on a calendar, before they leave.
3. **Mini-PFT slots** — short test windows for the gym-experienced crowd; results delivered as "here's where you'd start in HYBRID."
4. **Member-hosted tours** — members show their own gym; referral channel doing what it already does, in person.
5. **One day-only mechanic (pick one, keep it simple):** waived On-Ramp assessment for anyone who books a trial at the event, or a Grand Opening cohort framing ("first 10 sign-ups this month start On-Ramp together").

Follow-up rule: every sign-in gets a personal message within 48 hours (by Jul 13) routed by goal per lead capture flow Step 2.
