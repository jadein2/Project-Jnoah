## Part 2: Analysis of the handoff

### Strong
- Consultation-first fits the brand better than a free trial. It filters leads, lets a coach recommend the right program, and protects the premium, coaching-led position.
- "First week free with any plan" is value, not a discount, and the internal rule (activates after sign-up, not try-before-buy) is clearly stated.
- It is genuinely executable: carousel copy, 3 supporting posts, DM flow, team briefing, and a checklist are all written.
- Schedule and member WOD access are locked, and AM is confirmed.
- No peso on public, pricing handled in DM. Consistent CTA.

### Gaps and what we can improve
1. **No measurement.** There is no target for consultations booked, show rate, or consult to paid, and no tracker. **Fix:** set targets and stand up a tracker before posting (Stage 0).
2. **No response SLA.** The DM flow is good, but there is no speed rule or coverage hours. Speed to lead is the top conversion lever. **Fix:** reply under 5 minutes, 8am to 6pm, plus an after-hours auto-reply.
3. **"First week free" needs guardrails.** Risk: sign up, use the free week, cancel. **Fix:** define it as the first week of a monthly plan that continues unless cancelled, set a minimum commitment, and write a clear stacking rule with Soft Opening 20% and Founding or Pioneer (does first-week-free stack with the 20%, or is it one or the other?). This needs a decision.
4. **Consultation has no SOP.** Length, slots, who runs it, booking method (DM only or a calendar link), and no-show handling are undefined. **Fix:** a one-page consult SOP plus set slots.
5. **The video ads do not match.** The 3 class ads were built around Reserve a Free Trial and free PFT. **Fix:** change ad CTAs to Book a Free Consultation. The URBN Hybrid Test can live inside the consult, not as the public hook.
6. **No proof yet.** Same gap as before. **Fix:** capture early member and consult wins, feed them into later posts.
7. **10 slides is a lot.** Keep each slide to one idea and very short text, as the handoff says, or some viewers drop off. Consider a 7 to 8 slide tighter cut if engagement is low.
8. **Consistency debt.** Sweep Free Trial and free PFT language out of public assets (Stage 0).
