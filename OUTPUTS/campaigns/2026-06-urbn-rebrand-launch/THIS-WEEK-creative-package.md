# Design system (all assets)
- Black and white, clean, readable, community-announcement tone. Not sales-heavy.
- One idea per slide. 3 to 5 bullets max. Big title, short support line.
- URBN Athletics wordmark on each. Built to Move Better on the final slide or footer.
- Real gym or community photos where available (members training, coach briefing, morning vibe).
- No peso prices anywhere public. CTA clear on the last slide and in every caption.
- No em or en dashes in any on-image text or caption.
