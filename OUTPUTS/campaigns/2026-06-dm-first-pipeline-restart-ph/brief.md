## What carries over from the old campaign

- The 3+1 daily system (3 stories + 1 feed post) — operationally proven, unchanged
- 5 content pillars and weekly rotation
- DM response discipline (reply within 2 hours, morning + evening check)
- Banned language list

## What changes

| Old | New |
|---|---|
| Public CTA split between DM "RESTART" and "Book a Discovery Call" | DM "RESTART" only. The call is offered inside the DM after qualification — never on a post |
| 90-Day pushed as primary public offer | No program-first selling in posts. Lead with outcomes and proof; the offer match happens in the DM |
| Generic proof pillar | Proof = real completer results (6FIX cohort, with permission) + client check-in moments |
| Cold-traffic-first assumption | Referral-warmed-first: content that's easy to share/tag ("send this to the friend who keeps starting over" — phrased without "restart" as a verb) |

## DM flow (updated)

1. DM "RESTART" arrives → acknowledge within 2 hours, ask goal + history (qualifying, not pitching)
2. Qualify: specific goal, prior attempts, schedule reality
3. Introduce The Restart Fitness System briefly — one paragraph
4. **Fit fork:** strong commitment signals → offer the 20-minute call. Hesitant/early → offer 6FIX-style low-friction framing or simply keep the conversation warm. The call is earned by the conversation, not demanded by the post.
5. Log every DM: date, source post (which pillar/angle), outcome — this feeds EXP-2026-06-1 and the weekly count
