## North star sentence

A one-week story-driven content run that names the audience's real problem — they're trying hard but have no structure — and routes them to DM "RESTART" through five repeating story types, told across feed posts, a carousel, a banner, and daily Stories. The goal is qualified DM conversations, not reach.
