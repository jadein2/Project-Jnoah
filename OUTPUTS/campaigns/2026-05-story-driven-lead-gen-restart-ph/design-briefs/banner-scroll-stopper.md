## The title (chosen)

**THE PROBLEM ISN'T EFFORT. IT'S THE PLAN.**

This is the strongest of the five banner seeds from research — it names the audience's frustration (they ARE trying hard) and reframes it as fixable, without shame. Pairs directly with Post 1 and the carousel.

**Backup titles** (if you want to A/B or rotate weekly):
1. Stop guessing. Follow a system. *(evergreen brand line — safest, highest authority)*
2. You don't need more willpower. You need a check-in. *(accountability angle)*
3. Motivation starts you. Structure keeps you. *(habits angle)*

## Rules applied
- One emphasis color only (orange). No gold on this asset (it's action, not proof).
- No gradients. Flat charcoal.
- No-Line Rule — hierarchy through spacing and type scale, not dividers.
- One headline, one CTA, one idea.
- No banned words · DM "RESTART" with quotes · no "restart" as a verb.

## Optional photo treatment
If using a background image instead of flat charcoal: gritty, high-contrast, cinematic — a real person mid-training, unsmiling, focused. Charcoal overlay at high opacity so the headline stays readable. No bright/neon gym aesthetic. Sparse orange highlight only.
