# Daily Story Scripts — One Week (Restart Fitness PH)

**Campaign:** 2026-05 Story-Driven Lead Gen — Restart PH
**Source research:** Layer 5 — Daily Stories structure (5 frames/day)
**Platform:** Instagram Stories
**Built:** 2026-05-22

**Daily frame structure (every day):**
1. Presence — what's happening today
2. Quick value — one coaching insight
3. Interactive — poll / question / "reply to this" (the DM trigger)
4. Behind the scenes — a real coaching moment
5. Close (3x/week) — soft reminder of where to start

Frame 3 is the one that generates DMs. Reply personally to everyone who responds.
Text on frames is short by design — Stories are read in seconds.

---

## MONDAY — paired with Post 1 (Structure Story)

1. **Presence:** "Monday. New training week. Let's not wing it this time." [casual selfie or gym shot]
2. **Quick value:** "Random workouts give you random results. A plan gives you progress you can measure."
3. **Interactive — POLL:** "Be honest — what does your training week look like?"
   - Option A: I follow a plan
   - Option B: I just show up and decide
4. **Behind the scenes:** "Mapping out a client's week. Every session has a reason it's there." [screenshot of program / notebook]
5. **Close:** "If your training has no plan behind it, that's the fix. DM 'RESTART' and I'll walk you through it."

---

## TUESDAY — paired with the Carousel (System)

1. **Presence:** "New post is up. This one's worth a save." [repost carousel cover to story]
2. **Quick value:** "You don't have a motivation problem. You have a structure problem."
3. **Interactive — QUESTION BOX:** "What's the one thing that always knocks you off track? Drop it below — I read every one."
4. **Behind the scenes:** "This is what a coaching check-in actually looks like." [Trainerize check-in screenshot, with permission]
5. *(no close today — let the carousel and question box do the work)*

---

## WEDNESDAY — paired with Post 5 (Coach Jap Story)

1. **Presence:** "Quick story today. The thing I got wrong when I started coaching." [talking-head clip or photo]
2. **Quick value:** "The best program in the world still fails without structure around it."
3. **Interactive — POLL:** "Ever quit a program you knew was good?"
   - Yes, more than once
   - Yeah… that's me
4. **Behind the scenes:** "Reviewing a client's week 4 check-in. This is the part that actually keeps people going." [check-in shot]
5. **Close:** "If you keep starting over, the missing piece probably isn't the plan. DM 'RESTART' and let's find it."

---

## THURSDAY — paired with Post 4 (Accountability)

1. **Presence:** "Thursday. Check-in day for a few clients. My favourite part of the week." [coaching shot]
2. **Quick value:** "Knowing what to do and actually doing it are two different things. A check-in closes the gap."
3. **Interactive — "REPLY TO THIS":** "Want my honest take on what your current program is actually doing for you? Reply to this story and I'll tell you straight."
4. **Behind the scenes:** "Adjusting a client's plan based on her week. This is what 'coached' means — it changes with you." [screen/notebook]
5. *(no close — the reply prompt is today's DM driver)*

---

## FRIDAY — paired with Post 3 (Habits / frame shift)

1. **Presence:** "Made it to Friday. How did the week actually go?" [casual shot]
2. **Quick value:** "Consistency isn't never missing. It's not letting one miss become three."
3. **Interactive — POLL:** "Where did this week fall apart?"
   - Training
   - Nutrition
   - Just staying consistent
4. **Behind the scenes:** "Planning next week so my clients don't have to guess what to do on Monday." [planning shot]
5. **Close:** "Tired of the week-3 drop-off? That's exactly what the system fixes. DM 'RESTART'."

---

## SATURDAY — lighter / human day

1. **Presence:** "Weekend. Rest is part of the plan, not a break from it." [lifestyle shot]
2. **Quick value:** "You don't have to be perfect on weekends. You just can't undo the whole week."
3. **Interactive — QUESTION BOX:** "What's your biggest weekend struggle — food, drinks, or just stopping completely?"
4. **Behind the scenes:** "My own weekend training. I coach the same way I train — with a plan." [personal shot]
5. *(no close — keep Saturday low-pressure)*

---

## SUNDAY — reset / set up the week

1. **Presence:** "Sunday. The day that decides how your week actually goes." [calm shot]
2. **Quick value:** "Ten minutes planning today beats relying on willpower all week."
3. **Interactive — POLL:** "Are you walking into Monday with a plan or hoping for the best?"
   - I've got a plan
   - Hoping for the best
4. **Behind the scenes:** "Setting up the week for my clients. Nobody starts Monday guessing." [planning shot]
5. **Close:** "Want to start a week with a real plan for once? DM 'RESTART' and we'll talk through what that looks like for you."

---

## Notes
- Frame 3 (interactive) is the DM engine. Everyone who replies = a warm lead → route into the DM flow (post-routing.md → Restart PH DM conversation flow).
- Behind-the-scenes frames need real assets — capture coaching moments, check-in screenshots (with permission), planning shots through the week.
- Closes run Mon/Wed/Fri/Sun (4x) — slightly above the 3x floor because this is an active lead-gen week.
