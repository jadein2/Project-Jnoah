# 6FIX PHASE 2 — IN-PERSON ONBOARDING GUIDE
### For Coach Jap | Restart Fitness PH

---

## SESSION OVERVIEW

**Duration:** 60–75 minutes
**Format:** In-person
**Goal:** Client leaves with clarity on their personal system and is fully set up for Week 1.

---

## BEFORE THE SESSION

Coach Prep:
- Pull client's Phase 1 final results (weight, waist, habit score, wins, gaps)
- Prepare blank Personal System Builder sheet
- Have Trainerize open and ready
- Bring printed or digital daily framework template
- Know their goal body weight → protein target pre-calculated

---

## SESSION FLOW

---

### PART 1 — OPEN + CELEBRATE (5–10 min)

Start warm. Don't rush into logistics.

Say something like:
> "Before we talk about Phase 2, I want to acknowledge what you did. Phase 1 is not easy. You showed up. That matters."

Review together:
- Weight change
- Waist change
- Habit score average
- What they're most proud of

**Ask them:**
> "What surprised you most about the last 6 weeks?"

Let them talk. Listen. This builds connection and sets the tone for Phase 2.

---

### PART 2 — WHAT PHASE 2 IS (10 min)

Explain the shift clearly:

> "Phase 1 was about awareness — understanding your habits, your food, your patterns. Phase 2 is about building a system that fits your life. Not a challenge. A structure you actually live inside."

Key points to cover:
- 6 weeks, 1-on-1
- More personal — everything is designed for their schedule, their body, their life
- The goal: they don't need willpower anymore because the system handles the decisions

**The 4 Pillars (show them):**
1. Nutrition — your personal protein targets, your meals
2. Habits — your morning, your evening, your daily anchors
3. Movement — your training schedule, your step targets
4. Accountability — weekly check-ins, food logs, personal feedback

---

### PART 3 — LIFESTYLE AUDIT (10 min)

Ask these questions and write down their answers:

**Schedule:**
- What time do you wake up?
- What time do you sleep?
- What's your work/life schedule on weekdays?
- What's different on weekends?

**Eating Patterns:**
- What's your biggest eating challenge right now?
- When do you eat most of your food?
- What meals are hardest to control?
- Do you eat out? How often?

**Movement:**
- Are you still going to the gym / working out?
- What days are most realistic for training?
- What's your average steps per day right now?

**Energy & Sleep:**
- How's your sleep? Consistent or not?
- What time of day do you have the most energy?
- What's your biggest source of stress right now?

---

### PART 4 — BUILD THEIR PERSONAL SYSTEM (20 min)

Work through this together. Write it down in front of them.

#### Nutrition Targets

Goal body weight: _______ lbs
Protein target: _______ × 0.8 = _______ g/day

Meal breakdown:
- Breakfast goal: _______ g protein
- Lunch goal: _______ g protein
- Dinner goal: _______ g protein
- Snack (optional): _______ g protein

Their 3 default meals:
1. _______________________________
2. _______________________________
3. _______________________________

#### Daily Framework

Morning routine (what time, what steps):
- Wake: _______ AM
- Hydration: 500ml water
- Breakfast with protein by: _______ AM
- Log food: immediately after eating
- Steps/movement: _______ (walk or gym)

Evening routine:
- Last meal by: _______ PM
- Wind-down start: _______ PM
- Lights out by: _______ PM
- Prep for tomorrow: meals / bag / schedule

#### Training Schedule

Training days: _______ and _______
Session type: _______ (gym / home / mix)
Step target: _______ steps/day

---

### PART 5 — TRAINERIZE SETUP (5–10 min)

Walk through Trainerize together:

- Confirm they're in the Phase 2 client group
- Show them where to log habits
- Show them where to log food
- Show them how to submit weekly check-ins
- Assign Week 1 workout (if applicable)
- Confirm messaging is active — they can reach you

**Tell them:**
> "Every Friday, send me your check-in. I review it over the weekend and respond with your adjustments for next week. That's our rhythm."

---

### PART 6 — COMMITMENT + CLOSE (5 min)

End with intention.

Have them write or say out loud:

> "By the end of Phase 2, I will ________________________.
> I commit to showing up for my daily habits, my check-ins, and my system — even when I don't feel like it.
> This is not a challenge. This is my life."

Optional: record a private Phase 2 commitment video (same as Phase 1 commitment video format).

**Final words from you:**
> "Phase 1 showed you what's possible. Phase 2 is where it becomes permanent. Let's build this."
