# OUTREACH-01 — Personal Warm DM

**Asset ID:** OUTREACH-01
**Type:** Direct message — personal outreach
**Date:** June 1 (Day 1)
**Track:** D — Referral + Community
**C/C/V:** Conversion
**Audience:** Existing PT/gym clients of Coach Jap who haven't enrolled in 6FIX
**Engine:** sales-engine.md reference

---

## DM copy

Send individually — not as a mass blast. Personalize the opening with the person's name.

---

"Hey [Name],

I'm opening 6FIX Cohort 1 on June 8. 8 spots.

It's a 6-week program — structured training, nutrition, habit coaching, and weekly check-ins. Done online through Trainerize.

₱3,000 for Cohort 1. Regular rate after this is ₱6,000.

There's a free onboarding call on June 19 — video conference, about 45 minutes. Orientation, baseline, how the system works. No commitment required at that point.

You're one of the first people I'm reaching out to. If this sounds like something you want, or you know someone who would, just reply and I'll send you the details."

---

## Follow-up (if no reply after 3 days)

"Hey [Name] — just checking if you saw this. No rush, just want to make sure it didn't get buried. Onboarding call is June 19 if you want to come and see what 6FIX looks like before deciding."

---

## Notes

- Send this before any social post goes live — the warm list gets first access, not a simultaneous launch
- Keep the tone peer-to-peer — this is a coach reaching out to someone they know, not a sales pitch
- Do not send to people who have already expressed disinterest
- If they say yes: send the onboarding call details (June 19 date, Zoom/Meet link, what to expect)
- If they ask questions: answer directly, don't redirect to a link
- Buddy mechanic can be offered here: "If you bring someone, you both get 10% off at Week 3"
