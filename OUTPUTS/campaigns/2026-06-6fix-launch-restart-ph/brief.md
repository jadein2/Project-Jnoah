## Strategy architecture

This campaign runs two parallel tracks simultaneously.

**Enrollment flow (applies to all tracks)**
DM "6FIX" → Coach Jap replies with overview + free onboarding call details (June 19) → person confirms interest → attends June 19 video call (orientation, baseline, how the system works, enrollment offer) → pays ₱3,000 by June 21 → Trainerize invite sent → Cohort 1 begins June 22. DM "6FIX" is not a direct enrollment CTA — it opens the conversation that leads to enrollment.

**Track D — Referral + Community (Cohort 1 activation)**
Target: existing clients and warm connections. The 10% buddy discount mechanic (Week 3) is already built into the 6FIX system — activate it as a pre-launch incentive. Direct outreach first (personal DM, not a post). Then social content that builds community feel: what happens inside 6FIX, the check-in culture, the accountability structure. Warm audience doesn't need to be convinced 6FIX is good — they need a specific invitation and a reason to act now.

**Track B — Problem-Led Funnel (cold traffic + Cohort 2 seeding)**
Target: cold/semi-cold audience on IG and Threads. Content leads with the exact failure pattern the audience recognizes ("you stop at week 3 because there's no structure, not because you're lazy"). Then reveals the system. Then shows what 6FIX does differently. Conversion ask comes only after 2+ value pieces have landed. This track builds an audience that will convert to Cohort 2 if Cohort 1 fills fast — or top up Cohort 1 if warm-list conversions come in short.

**Convergence at Week 2:** Both tracks point to the same action — DM "6FIX" — and the same cohort start date. The buddy mechanic from Track D can appear in Track B content without needing separate posts.

## Key message angles

From research (6fix-system-integration-research.md) + brand file:

1. **Structure beats effort** — "You've been putting in effort. The missing piece is structure." Pairs with the brand line: Stop guessing. Follow a system.
2. **The week 3 wall** — 60% of clients disengage within 90 days. 6FIX is designed around this window. Name the problem, own the solution.
3. **Connection is what keeps people** — 57% of clients stay for connection, not the workouts. The 6FIX group structure is a retention mechanism, not a bonus feature.
4. **6 weeks is specific** — Not a vague "lifestyle change." Not an 8-week challenge. Six weeks with a clear structure, a coach, and a check-in system.
5. **Phase 2 exists** — Cohort 1 is not a one-off. There's a continuation. This reframes the ₱3,000 entry as a beginning, not a transaction.

## Copy rules for this campaign

- DM "6FIX" — always with quotes around 6FIX in the same way RESTART is handled
- Lead with the problem or the outcome — never with the program name as the first hook
- Phase 1 price (₱3,000) is the launch price — never position it as cheap. Position it as the entry point to a system.
- Buddy mechanic: "Bring someone who needs this. 10% off at Week 3 for both of you." — warm, not salesy
- No countdown timers, no fake scarcity. Scarcity is real (8 spots). State it once per conversion post, not in every caption.
- Avoid: "transform your body" / "results guaranteed" / "no excuses" / any hype language
