# Week 1 Manual DM Flow

**Campaign:** 2026-06 Social Growth + Pipeline - Restart PH  
**Trigger:** DM "RESTART" or reply to a Week 1 Story  
**Goal:** Start a real conversation, qualify the person, and route to the next step without pitching too early.  

---

## Core Rule

Ask one question at a time. Do not send the service ladder in the first reply.

---

## 1. First Reply - DM "RESTART"

Hey, thanks for reaching out.

Quick question before I point you anywhere: what are you trying to fix first right now - training, nutrition, consistency, or accountability?

---

## 2. If They Reply With Training

Got it. What does your training week look like right now? Number of days, and are you following a plan or mostly deciding as you go?

---

## 3. If They Reply With Nutrition

Got it. Are you tracking anything right now, or mostly trying to eat better without a clear target?

---

## 4. If They Reply With Consistency

That is common. When you fall off, what usually happens first - missed workouts, food gets random, schedule gets heavy, or you just lose track?

---

## 5. If They Reply With Accountability

That makes sense. Do you currently have anyone checking your week and helping you adjust, or are you handling it alone?

---

## 6. Bridge To The System

That helps.

The way I coach this is through The Restart Fitness System: training, nutrition, habits, and accountability working together. The goal is not to throw more workouts at you. It is to see what is missing and build the next step from there.

If you want, we can do a short Discovery Call so I can understand your starting point and tell you what path makes sense.

---

## 7. If They Say Yes To A Call

Good. Send me your best day and time window this week, and I will send you the next step.

Before the call, think about these three things:
1. Your main goal
2. What you have already tried
3. What keeps knocking you off track

---

## 8. If They Ask For Price First

I can give you the price range, but I do not want to point you to the wrong thing.

The right option depends on your goal, how independently you can train, and how much coaching support you need.

What are you trying to fix first right now?

---

## 9. If They Are Interested But Not Ready

No problem.

Start here: this week, pick one thing to track consistently. Training days, protein, steps, or sleep. Just one.

If you want help choosing the right one, send me your current routine and I will give you a straight answer.

---

## 10. If They Stop Replying

### Follow-up after 24-48 hours

Quick check - what part are you trying to fix first right now: training, nutrition, consistency, or accountability?

### Follow-up after 7 days

Hey, checking back in. If now is not the right time, no pressure. If you still want help figuring out your starting point, send me one word: training, nutrition, consistency, or accountability.

---
