## Mon Jun 1 - Opening Campaign Reel

**Routing:** Value + The Problem  
**Length:** 30-45 seconds  
**Format:** Talking head with simple gym B-roll  
**Hook:** Most people don't need more motivation. They need a plan that actually works.

### Script

Most people don't need more motivation.

They need a plan that actually works when life gets busy.

Here is what usually happens:

You start strong. You train hard for a week or two. Then work gets heavy, food gets random, sleep drops, and the whole plan starts falling apart.

That is not always laziness.

Sometimes the plan was never clear enough to begin with.

The better place to start is simple:

Know what you are training for.  
Know what you are tracking.  
Know what to do when the week is not perfect.

That is what structure gives you.

If you have been putting in effort but still feel like you are guessing, DM "RESTART". I will help you find the gap.
