# Week 1 Creative Asset Briefs

**Campaign:** 2026-06 Social Growth + Pipeline - Restart PH  
**Week:** Jun 1-7  
**Brand:** Restart Fitness PH  
**Design direction:** Minimalist editorial, dark charcoal, off-white type, sparse orange action accents.  
**Primary Canva kit:** restartfitnessph `kAHBdXD16KU`  

---

## Global Creative System

**Look:** Quiet, structured, coach-led. The visuals should feel like a serious coaching system, not a fitness promo page. Use space, type, and contrast before adding imagery.

**Colors:**
- Background: `#1A1A18`
- Text: `#F5F5F0`
- Body / secondary text: `#A8A89E`
- Action accent: `#E06D22`
- Gold: avoid in Week 1 unless a proof asset is added later

**Type:**
- Headline: Cormorant Garamond Italic, Light 300
- Body and CTA: DM Sans 300-500

**Rules:**
- No gradients
- No decorative divider lines
- One focal message per asset
- Orange only for one action word or CTA
- Use real gym, coaching, check-in, or planning imagery when available

## AI Image Prompts

### General Restart PH Background Prompt

Create a minimalist editorial fitness coaching image for Restart Fitness PH. A real adult in a dim, focused gym environment, resting between sets or listening to coaching feedback. High contrast, cinematic, moody grayscale, sparse burnt orange accent, deep charcoal tones, serious but not aggressive. Large negative space for headline text. No stock-photo smile, no neon lights, no bodybuilding stage look, no clutter, no lens flare. Aspect ratio 4:5.

### Story Background Prompt

Create a vertical Instagram Story background for a coaching brand. Dark charcoal gym environment, real planning or coaching moment, notebook or phone check-in visible without readable private details, soft side light, calm focused mood, high contrast, editorial sports photography, large empty space for text. No bright colors, no clutter, no fake smile, no motivational poster style. Aspect ratio 9:16.
