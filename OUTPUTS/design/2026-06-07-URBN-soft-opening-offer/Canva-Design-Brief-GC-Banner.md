# Canva Design Brief — GC Image Banner
**Asset:** Founding Member Rate announcement banner
**Brand:** URBN Athletics
**Use:** Posted alongside GC letter in Urban Strong / URBN Athletics Facebook Messenger group
**Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080 px (square — Facebook/Messenger standard)
**Visual direction:** Black and white base, clean, message-first, no clutter

---

## Headline Copy (on image)

**Primary headline:**
`FOUNDING MEMBER RATE`

**Sub-headline:**
`Your rate at Urban Strong stays your rate at URBN Athletics.`

**Supporting line:**
`We honor what you already committed to.`

**Urgency line:**
`June 8–22 only.`

**Brand line:**
`URBN Athletics · Built to Move Better`

---

## Layout Structure (top to bottom)

```
[ URBN Athletics wordmark — top left or top center, small ]

[ Large bold headline — center ]
FOUNDING MEMBER RATE

[ Sub-headline — below headline, medium weight ]
Your rate at Urban Strong stays your rate at URBN Athletics.

[ Supporting line — smaller, lighter weight ]
We honor what you already committed to.

[ Thin horizontal rule ]

[ Urgency line — below rule, distinct weight ]
June 8–22 only.

[ Brand line — bottom center, small ]
URBN Athletics · Built to Move Better
```

---

## Typography Direction

- Headline: Bold, uppercase or mixed case — heavy weight
- Sub-headline: Regular weight, sentence case
- Rate block: Bold, larger than sub-headline
- Urgency line: Medium weight, distinct from rate block — could use a line separator above it
- Brand line: Light weight, smallest text on the image

Use a clean sans-serif. Suggested: Montserrat Bold for headline, Montserrat Regular or Light for body lines. Available free in Canva.

---

## Color Direction

Base option (recommended):
- Background: Black (#000000)
- All text: White (#FFFFFF)
- Optional accent: A single thin white horizontal rule separating the rate block from the urgency line

Alternate option:
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- All text: Black (#000000)
- Same rule accent in black

Do not add gradients, colored backgrounds, or decorative elements. Keep it clean and direct.

---

## Photo Option (if you want to include one)

If you include a photo:
- Use a real gym photo — a coaching moment, small group training, or clean facility shot
- Place it as a background at reduced opacity (around 20–30%) so text stays readable
- Avoid stock-looking fitness photos
- No screaming or hardcore imagery

If no photo is available, the text-only version is cleaner and preferred.

---

## What to Avoid

- No sale-poster style (no stars, banners, loud colors)
- No motivational quotes
- No emoji on the image
- No busy backgrounds
- No more than 5 lines of text visible at once
- Do not use "Urban Strong Fitness" as the main brand — this is an URBN Athletics asset
- Do not add a QR code or link on the image (not needed for GC)

---

## Canva Build Notes

1. Start with a blank 1080 x 1080 px canvas.
2. Set background to black (or white if preferred).
3. Add the wordmark text "URBN Athletics" in the top area — small, clean.
4. Add the headline in the center — largest text element on the canvas.
5. Add sub-headline directly below — significantly smaller than headline.
6. Add rate block — bold, slightly larger than sub-headline.
7. Add a thin horizontal line (use Canva's line element) above the urgency line.
8. Add urgency line below the rule.
9. Add brand line at the bottom — smallest text.
10. Check spacing — everything should breathe. No crowding.
11. Export as PNG, 1080 x 1080 px.

---

## Post alongside

This image goes in the same GC message as the Founding Member letter. Post image first, then the text message below it — or together in one post if the platform allows.
