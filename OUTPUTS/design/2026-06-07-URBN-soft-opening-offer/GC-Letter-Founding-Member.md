# GC Letter — Founding Member Rate
**Asset type:** Member-facing Messenger message
**Audience:** All members in the Urban Strong / URBN Athletics GC (active, idle, past)
**Tone:** Recognition leads. Urgency closes.
**Window:** June 8–22, 2026
**Brand:** URBN Athletics

---

## Message

---

To everyone in this group —

You were part of Urban Strong Fitness. That matters to us.

Urban Strong is now URBN Athletics. Same coaches, same community — better programming, better space, higher standard overall. This has been in the works for a while, and the people in this group are the reason it exists.

So here is what we're doing for you.

**Your rate at Urban Strong stays your rate at URBN Athletics.**

Whatever plan you were on — that's your Founding Rate. We're not asking you to sign up at a new price. We're honoring what you already committed to.

This is our way of recognizing the loyalty and trust you gave to Urban Strong before URBN Athletics was even a name.

**This offer is open until June 22 only.**

After that, the regular rate goes live. The Founding Rate will not be available after the window closes.

To lock in your rate:
1. React to this message or send us a message — let us know you're in.
2. Settle any outstanding balance if you have one.
3. Sign the updated URBN Athletics agreement.
4. Pay and you're confirmed.

If you've been away for a while — this is your way back in, at the rate you earned.

Come train with us.

**URBN Athletics**
Built to Move Better
