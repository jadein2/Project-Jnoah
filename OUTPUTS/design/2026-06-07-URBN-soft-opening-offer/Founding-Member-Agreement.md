## What This Membership Covers

Your Founding Membership includes access to:

- Regular coached group classes
- URBN Athletics member community
- Standard class coaching and programming
- Basic coach guidance during class
- Monthly member assessment (when active in the assessment system)
- Recognition as a Founding Member

This membership does not include:

- Personal training
- Semi-private training
- Custom individual programming
- Nutrition coaching or meal plan creation
- Private consultations
- Paid workshops or special events
- Hybrid Track entry (requires Physical Fitness Test and coach approval)
- Open gym outside approved hours unless stated by management

Additional services may be added separately at the applicable rate.

## Founding Rate — Key Terms

**Your rate.** Your Founding Rate is the same rate you held at Urban Strong Fitness. This is URBN Athletics recognizing your loyalty and the trust you gave before this gym existed under its current name.

**Your rate is yours as long as you stay active.** If you miss a renewal without an approved freeze, cancel your membership, or go inactive — your Founding Rate is forfeited. Coming back after that means coming back at the regular rate.

**Your rate is not permanent.** URBN Athletics reserves the right to adjust the Founding Rate for business purposes. You will receive a minimum of 30 days written notice before any change takes effect. Notice will be sent via Messenger, SMS, or posted gym notice.

**Your rate is non-transferable.** You may not transfer, share, or pass your Founding Rate or membership access to another person.

## Payment Rules

- All memberships must be paid before you train. No exceptions unless approved in writing by management.
- Monthly members must renew on or before the renewal date.
- A grace period of up to 3 days is allowed for late renewals.
- If payment is not settled after 3 days, training access is suspended.
- If payment remains unpaid beyond 7 days, Founding Member status may be forfeited.
- Two late renewals within any 6-month period will trigger a review of your Founding status.

---

## Freeze Policy

If you need to pause your membership:

- Submit a freeze request at least 5 days before the intended start date.
- Freeze fee: ₱500 per month.
- Minimum freeze period: 30 days. Partial freezes are not allowed.
- Freeze requests are subject to management approval.
- Freeze cannot be applied after the fact unless management approves.
- Going inactive without an approved freeze forfeits your Founding Rate.

---

## Cancellation

- Monthly members may cancel by notifying management in writing before the next billing cycle.
- Written notice means Messenger message, email, or signed notice submitted to reception. Verbal notice to another member or coach is not accepted.
- Cancellation forfeits your Founding Rate.
- Returning after cancellation means returning at the regular rate unless management approves otherwise.

---

## Refund Policy

All payments are non-refundable once the membership has started.

Exceptions may be considered for medical reasons, relocation, or documented emergencies. Any approved refund, credit, or transfer must be documented in writing by management.

## Health and Safety

I confirm that I am physically capable of participating in fitness training. I understand it is my responsibility to:

- Inform coaches of any injuries, health conditions, or physical limitations before training.
- Stop training immediately if I experience dizziness, chest pain, shortness of breath, or any unusual symptom.
- Follow coach instructions at all times, especially when safety is involved.
- Seek medical clearance if required before joining certain programs or training tracks.
- Accept personal responsibility for training within my current ability.

I understand that URBN Athletics may require a movement assessment before I join certain programs, modify my participation, or pause my training if safety is a concern.
