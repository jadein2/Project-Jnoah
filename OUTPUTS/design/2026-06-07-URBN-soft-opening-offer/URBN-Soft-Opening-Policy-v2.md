# URBN Athletics Soft Opening Offer — Policy Document
**Version 3 — June 2026**
**Offer Type:** Founding Member Rate / Pioneer Member Rate / Soft Opening Policy
**Brand:** URBN Athletics
**Core Line:** Built to Move Better

---

## 1. Purpose

The URBN Athletics Soft Opening Offer establishes two priority access tiers as the gym transitions from Urban Strong Fitness to URBN Athletics.

**Founding Rate** — a loyalty recognition for everyone who was a member of Urban Strong Fitness at any point. Founding members do not get a new rate — they keep the exact rate from the plan or membership they held at Urban Strong Fitness. This is URBN Athletics honoring the trust and commitment those members already gave.

**Pioneer Rate** — a soft opening rate for new URBN Athletics clients who join during the limited Pioneer window.

The Founding Rate is not a discount offer. It is a retention of what was already earned. The Pioneer Rate and Regular Rate are separate tiers for new clients.

---

## 2. Rate Structure

### Founding Rate
Available to all past, current, and idle Urban Strong Fitness members.

The Founding Rate is not a fixed number. Each Founding Member retains the exact rate from the plan or membership they held at Urban Strong Fitness.

| Member | Founding Rate |
|---|---|
| Current active member | Retains current ongoing rate |
| Idle or past member | Retains the rate from their last active membership |
| Member with multiple past plans | Management verifies and confirms the applicable retained rate |

The retained rate applies to the equivalent plan type (monthly retains monthly, annual retains annual). Any upgrade to a different plan type is subject to the current rate for that plan.

**Operational note for reception:** Before confirming a Founding Member's rate, verify their membership record at Urban Strong Fitness. If records are unclear or unavailable, management determines the applicable retained rate. All confirmed Founding Rates must be documented in the Founding Member Tracker before the member is confirmed.

### Pioneer Rate
Available to new URBN Athletics clients during the soft opening period.

| Plan | Rate |
|---|---|
| Pioneer Monthly | ₱3,600 |
| Pioneer Annual | ₱30,000 |

### Regular Rate
Active after the soft opening period ends or slots are filled.

| Plan | Rate |
|---|---|
| Regular Monthly | ₱4,500 |
| Regular Annual | ₱35,000 |
| Walk-In / Per Session | ₱500 |

## 3. Founding Rate — Eligibility

The Founding Rate is available to:

1. Any person who held a membership at Urban Strong Fitness at any time, regardless of year.
2. Members who are currently active.
3. Members who went idle or cancelled at any point.
4. Student members from Urban Strong Fitness (folded into the standard Founding Rate — no separate student tier).
5. Members who claim the rate within the Founding Rate window (June 8–22, 2026).
6. Members who sign the updated URBN Athletics membership agreement.
7. Members approved by management.

A member is in good standing if:
- No unpaid balance at the time of claiming.
- No unresolved conduct or safety issues.
- No unauthorized membership transfers.

---

## 4. Founding Rate — Not Eligible

The following are not automatically eligible for the Founding Rate:

- Walk-ins with no prior Urban Strong Fitness membership.
- New clients with no membership history at Urban Strong Fitness.
- Anyone who refuses to sign the updated agreement.
- Members with unresolved unpaid balances (balance must be settled first).

Management may approve special cases. All exceptions must be documented.

---

## 5. Pioneer Rate — Eligibility

The Pioneer Rate is for new URBN Athletics clients only.

Available to:
1. New clients with no prior Urban Strong Fitness membership history.
2. Referred new leads.
3. Soft opening walk-ins.
4. Management-approved prospects.

The Pioneer Rate is not for Urban Strong members. Urban Strong members qualify for the Founding Rate, not the Pioneer Rate.

---

## 6. Slot Limit

The Founding Rate and Pioneer Rate are limited to a combined total set by management.

A slot is confirmed only when:
1. The member or client signs the updated agreement.
2. Payment is submitted.
3. Payment is officially recorded.
4. The member is added to the Founding / Pioneer Member Tracker.

A verbal reservation does not hold a slot.

---

## 7. Founding Rate Window

The Founding Rate window opens **June 8, 2026** and closes **June 22, 2026**.

All Urban Strong Fitness members — active, idle, and past — must claim their Founding Rate within this window to lock it in.

After June 22, 2026:
- Remaining unclaimed slots may be offered at management's discretion.
- Founding Rate is no longer automatically available.
- Members who missed the window return under the Regular Rate unless management approves an exception.

---

## 8. Claiming the Founding Rate

To claim the Founding Rate, the member must:

1. Confirm interest with reception or management before June 22, 2026.
2. Settle any unpaid balance from previous membership.
3. Choose monthly or annual Founding plan.
4. Sign the updated URBN Athletics membership agreement.
5. Submit payment.
6. Receive payment confirmation.
7. Be listed in the official Founding Member Tracker.

No payment, no confirmed slot.

---

## 9. Rate Stability and Change Notice

The Founding Rate — each member's retained Urban Strong Fitness rate — is not permanent.

URBN Athletics honors the retained rate as a recognition of loyalty and trust. However, URBN Athletics reserves the right to adjust any Founding Rate for business purposes at any time.

Members on the Founding Rate will receive a minimum of **30 days written notice** before any rate change takes effect.

Notice will be sent via:
- Messenger or SMS
- Posted gym notice
- Group chat announcement

A member who receives notice and does not respond or act within the notice period will be moved to the current applicable rate after the 30 days.

The retained rate recognition is a privilege, not a contractual guarantee beyond the notice period.

---

## 10. Payment-First Rule

All memberships must be paid before the member trains.

No member may join a class, use the open gym, or enter any program with an unpaid balance unless approved in writing by management.

---

## 11. Renewal Rule

Monthly Founding and Pioneer members must renew on or before the membership renewal date.

- A grace period of up to 3 days is allowed.
- If unpaid after 3 days, access is suspended.
- Two late renewals within a 6-month period triggers a review of Founding or Pioneer status.

---

## 12. Late Payment Rule

If a Founding or Pioneer member misses renewal:

1. Reception sends a payment reminder.
2. Member has up to 3 days grace period.
3. If unpaid after 3 days, training access is suspended.
4. If unpaid for more than 7 days, Founding or Pioneer status may be forfeited.
5. If forfeited, the member returns under the Regular Rate unless management approves an exception.

---

## 13. Annual Plan Rule

Members who choose an annual plan must pay the full amount upfront unless management approves a written installment arrangement.

Annual Plan terms:
- Valid 12 months from start date.
- Non-transferable.
- Non-refundable once started.
- Subject to all house rules and the membership agreement.
- Freeze rules apply.

---

## 14. Membership Inclusions

Founding and Pioneer memberships include:

- Access to regular coached group classes.
- Access to the URBN Athletics member community.
- Standard class coaching.
- Regular programming.
- Basic coach guidance during class.
- Monthly member assessment (if active in the current assessment system).
- Recognition as a Founding or Pioneer Member.

Final inclusions are confirmed in the signed member agreement.

---

## 15. Membership Exclusions

Founding and Pioneer memberships do not automatically include:

- Personal training.
- Semi-private training.
- Custom individual programming.
- Nutrition coaching.
- Meal plan creation.
- Private consultation.
- Paid workshops or special events.
- Hybrid Track (requires PFT and coach approval — see Section 22).
- Merchandise.
- Open gym outside approved hours (unless included by management).

These may be purchased separately or added as an upgrade.

---

## 16. Class Booking and Attendance

Members must follow the class booking or attendance system set by URBN Athletics.

- Book or confirm attendance if required.
- Arrive on time. Late arrivals may only join after coach approval.
- Class capacity, equipment, and safety may limit participation.
- Coach and management decisions on participation are final.

## 17. Soft Opening Schedule

During the soft opening, the schedule may be adjusted as operations stabilize.

Members understand that:
- Class times may change.
- Some areas may still be under setup.
- Equipment layout may be adjusted.
- Temporary schedule changes may occur.

URBN Athletics will communicate major schedule changes as early as possible.

## 18. Facility Use

Members must follow all facility rules:

- Return equipment after use.
- Wipe down equipment when needed.
- Do not drop equipment unnecessarily.
- Respect coach instructions.
- Respect other members.
- Keep bags and personal items in designated areas.
- Do not interrupt ongoing classes.
- Do not coach other members unless authorized.
- Report damaged equipment immediately.

---

## 19. Coach Authority

Coaches have the authority to:

- Scale or modify movements.
- Stop unsafe movement.
- Adjust load or intensity.
- Ask a member to rest.
- Require proper warm-up.
- Deny participation if safety is a concern.
- Enforce house rules.
- Report conduct or safety issues.

Members must respect coaching decisions, especially when safety is involved.

---

## 20. Health and Safety

All members must disclose relevant health concerns, injuries, or limitations before training.

Members are responsible for informing coaches about:
- Injuries or pain during movement.
- Medical restrictions.
- Recent illness.
- Pregnancy, if applicable.
- Dizziness, chest pain, or unusual symptoms.
- Any condition that may affect training safety.

URBN Athletics may require medical clearance for certain members before allowing full participation.

---

## 21. Movement Assessment

URBN Athletics may require a movement assessment or coach review before a member joins certain programs, classes, or training tracks.

Assessment may be required for:
- Beginners.
- Returning members after extended inactivity.
- Members with injury history.
- Members joining Hybrid training.
- Members requesting advanced movements.
- Members showing unsafe mechanics.

---

## 22. Hybrid Track

Entry to the Hybrid Track is not automatic.

Members may be required to complete:
- Physical Fitness Test (PFT).
- Coach screening.
- On-Ramp / Foundation Course.
- Movement assessment.
- Conditioning readiness check.

This ensures correct placement in the training level.

---

## 23. Freeze Policy

Freeze requests must be submitted at least 5 days before the intended freeze start date.

Freeze rules:
- Freeze fee: ₱500 per month.
- Minimum freeze period: 30 days.
- Partial freeze is not allowed.
- Subject to management approval.
- Cannot be applied retroactively unless management approves.
- If freeze exceeds 30 days, a new request and fee may be required.

A member who goes inactive without an approved freeze may lose Founding or Pioneer status.

---

## 24. Cancellation

Monthly members may cancel by notifying management before the next billing cycle.

- Cancellation must be in writing (Messenger, email, or signed notice).
- Verbal notice to another member or coach is not accepted.
- Once cancelled, Founding or Pioneer status is forfeited.
- A returning cancelled member returns under the Regular Rate unless management approves an exception.

---

## 25. Refund Policy

All payments are non-refundable once the membership, program, or service has started.

Exceptions may be reviewed by management for:
- Medical reasons.
- Relocation.
- Documented emergency.
- Business-side scheduling issues.

Approved refunds, credits, or transfers must be documented in writing.

---

## 26. Transfer Policy

Founding and Pioneer memberships are non-transferable.

Members may not:
- Transfer membership to another person.
- Share membership access.
- Sell their slot.
- Pass their status to a friend or family member.

Unauthorized transfer results in forfeiture of Founding or Pioneer status.

---

## 27. Discount Stacking

The Founding Rate and Pioneer Rate cannot be combined with other discounts unless approved in writing by management.

Not allowed by default:
- Founding Rate + referral discount.
- Founding Rate + promotional discount.
- Pioneer Rate + any additional discount.

One member, one approved rate.

---

## 28. Referral Rule

Founding and Pioneer members may still participate in the referral program.

Standard referral terms:
- Referring member receives ₱300 cash or gym credit when the referred member pays.
- Referred member receives 10% off an approved plan or package.

The 10% referral discount does not automatically apply to Pioneer or Founding slots unless management approves.

---

## 29. Forfeiture of Founding or Pioneer Status

A member loses Founding or Pioneer status if:

- Renewal payment is missed beyond the grace period.
- Membership is cancelled.
- Member goes inactive without an approved freeze.
- Membership is transferred or shared without approval.
- Member violates house rules.
- Member has repeated late payments.
- Member has unresolved unpaid balance.
- Member behaves disrespectfully toward staff, coaches, or members.
- Member refuses to sign or follow the updated agreement.
- Member uses the facility in an unsafe or abusive manner.

Once forfeited, Founding or Pioneer status is not automatically restored.
