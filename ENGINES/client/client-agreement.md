# Client Agreement Engine

The one-page agreement sent to every client before the onboarding call.

Source: Coach Jap system design session — 2026-05-19

Load this to generate or review a client agreement. The agreement template is below. Copy it, fill in the client name and program details, and send it.

---

## What this file is

This is not a legal contract. It is a clear, written statement of how the coaching relationship works — what the client can expect from Coach Jap, and what Coach Jap expects from the client. Sending it before the onboarding call sets the standard early and removes ambiguity later.

Every client gets this. No exceptions.

---

## When it's sent

Stage 5 of the onboarding pipeline — immediately after payment is confirmed. Sent in the same message as the Trainerize link and the onboarding call booking options.

The client is asked to read and reply to confirm they understand before the onboarding call begins.

## Client Agreement Template

---

**RESTART FITNESS PH — CLIENT AGREEMENT**

Client Name: [Full name]
Program: [Program name and duration]
Start Date: [Confirmed start date]
Coach: Japhet Felipe

---

**1. The coaching relationship**

This is a coaching program. You will be given a structured training plan, regular feedback, and direct access to your coach through Trainerize. The results depend on both parties doing their part — the program is only as effective as the consistency behind it.

**2. Communication**

All coaching communication — session questions, schedule changes, feedback, check-ins — happens through Trainerize. Not Instagram. Not Facebook. Not WhatsApp. One channel. This keeps your coaching organized and ensures nothing gets missed.

If you message outside Trainerize, expect to be redirected back.

**3. Session cancellations and no-shows**

You may cancel or reschedule a session without penalty if you give at least 12 hours' notice.

Cancellations with less than 12 hours' notice will count as a used session.
No-shows (missed without any notice) will count as a used session.

Sessions cannot be banked, rolled over, or extended beyond the program period.

**4. Check-ins**

Weekly check-ins are part of the program. You will receive a 5-field check-in prompt each week via Trainerize. Completing it on time allows your coach to make the right adjustments before your next session. Skipping check-ins breaks the feedback loop — your program defaults to the previous week's data.

**5. Program Only clients**

If you are on a Program Only plan: your program is built and updated based on your Trainerize logs and bi-weekly assessments. Logging your sessions accurately is how your coach tracks your progress and adjusts the plan.

**6. What to expect from your coach**

- A program built for your specific goal and schedule
- Weekly feedback based on your check-ins
- Direct responses to messages within Trainerize (response time: within 24 hours on weekdays)
- Honest assessment of your progress — not what you want to hear, what you need to hear

**7. What your coach expects from you**

- Complete sessions as programmed or communicate in advance when you cannot
- Submit check-ins on time
- Use Trainerize as your primary coaching channel
- Be honest in your feedback — about soreness, missed sessions, and anything else affecting your training

**8. Program adjustments**

Your program will be adjusted based on your check-in data. You will not be consulted on every small change — that is the coach's job. If you have a concern about a specific movement or session structure, raise it in Trainerize and it will be addressed.

**9. Refunds**

Once the program has started, no refunds are issued. If you need to pause due to a medical reason, notify your coach immediately. Pauses are handled on a case-by-case basis.

## Instructions for sending

1. Copy the template above.
2. Fill in: client name, program name and duration, confirmed start date.
3. Remove the "[Full name]" and bracketed fields — replace with real data.

5. Ask them to reply to confirm they've read it before the onboarding call.
