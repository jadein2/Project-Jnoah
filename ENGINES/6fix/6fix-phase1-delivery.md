# 6FIX Phase 1 — Full Delivery System
**Program:** 6FIX — Restart Fitness PH
**Duration:** 6 weeks (Days 1–42) + Week 0 onboarding
**Framework:** ADHERE Compliance System integrated
**Last updated:** 2026-05-26

---

## Phase 1 Identity

Phase 1 is not a transformation challenge. It is a compliance installation. The client's job in Phase 1 is to show up consistently, build three core habits, and finish. The coach's job is to protect that streak — especially in weeks 1–2.

**Core philosophy:** Results = Fitness + Nutrition + Accountability. Phase 1 installs all three at the minimum effective dose.

**Non-negotiables:**
- Minimum 3 physical activity sessions or movement targets per week (based on activity track)
- One nutrition habit active from Day 1
- One sleep habit active from Day 1
- Weekly Saturday check-in completed every week
- Food/habit log submitted weekly

### WEEK 1 — Awareness and Tracking
**Theme:** Awareness creates control. Don't guess. Learn yourself.
**ADHERE focus:** Rescue Zone — frequency over everything.

**Coach posture:** High-touch. Daily presence. No coaching advice — just acknowledgment. Frame everything as discovery, not performance.

**This week's habits:**
- Log food daily (photo or written — client's preferred method)
- Weigh daily, same time, same scale
- Hit minimum steps target (based on activity track)

**Daily tasks:**
- Take a photo of each meal or write it down
- Log food intake in Trainerize
- Track steps daily
- Stay honest — no judgment, just data

**Weekly focus:**
- Track food to identify patterns, not to be perfect
- Identify 2 biggest eating triggers
- Notice what is missing from meals (usually protein)

**Progress check-in (Saturday):**
- Weight + waist measurement
- Food log review
- Upload results in app

**Weekly mission:**
"Here's how many I did" post — tag #6Fix #restartfitnessph #CoachJap
*(Habit check-ins: how many days did you hit your target?)*

**Auto-messages this week:**
- Day 1: Welcome message + Day 1 instructions (see auto-message cadence below)
- Day 3: Midweek nudge
- Day 7: End of Week 1 + check-in reminder

**Rescue Zone rules (Days 1–7):**
- Primary goal: hit 3 activity sessions or movement targets. That is the only metric.
- No nutrition tracking complexity yet. Only the one nutrition habit from intake.
- No body metrics pressure. Weight is data only — no commentary.
- Daily 30-second client check-in: thumbs-up + one line ("Slept 7hrs, mood 7/10, logged food.")
- Coach response: one-line acknowledgment. No advice. No coaching. Just presence.

### WEEK 2 — Swaps and Add-Ons
**Theme:** Replace, don't restrict. Better decisions start with better replacements.
**ADHERE focus:** Rescue Zone Days 8–14 — layer one addition.

**Coach posture:** High-touch. Still presence-first. Begin friction audit.

**This week's habits:**
- Remove liquid sugar (sugary drinks replaced with water or zero-cal option)
- 1 food swap per day
- Eating order: Vegetables → Protein → Carbs

**Daily tasks:**
- Food log (photo or written)
- Steps target daily
- Remove or avoid liquid sugar
- Complete 1 food swap

**Swap examples:**

| Instead of | Try this |
|---|---|
| Sweet drink | Water / zero-cal option |
| Chips | Fruit + protein |
| Fast food | Grilled protein + vegetables |
| Dessert craving | Fruit in yogurt + protein |

**Weekly focus:**
- Reduce 2 bad habits from Week 1 observations
- Use simple swaps during cravings
- Keep meals anchored with protein and vegetables

**Optional supplementation introduced:**
- Whey protein (if client is struggling to hit protein)
- Creatine (if client is ready to train)
- Electrolytes (if client is increasing movement significantly)

**Weekly mission (Challenge Task):**
"He made me do it" post — tag #6Fix #restartfitnessph #CoachJap
*Points: 10 completed / 5 effort / 0 none*

**Auto-messages this week:**
- Day 8: Week 2 kickoff + swap challenge introduced
- Day 10: Midweek friction audit DM ("What was hardest this week? Tell me one thing.")
- Day 14: End of Week 2 + decision point

**Rescue Zone — End of Week 2 Decision Point:**
Run the full Saturday check-in script. Use the outcome to set Week 3 strategy:

| Result | Action |
|---|---|
| Hit 5–6 of 6 sessions/targets | Escalate. Add second nutrition habit. Move into Week 3 normally. |
| Hit 3–4 of 6 sessions/targets | Hold steady. Repeat Week 2 protocol for one more week before adding complexity. |
| Hit 0–2 of 6 sessions/targets | Return to onboarding. Re-run the intake. Identify what broke. Do not push forward. |

### WEEK 6 — Autonomy and Retention
**Theme:** Own your path forward. Transition from six weeks of guidance to a sustainable, independent lifestyle.
**ADHERE focus:** E — self-direction fully activated. Client owns the system.

**Coach posture:** Low-touch. Validate and challenge. Prepare for graduation or upgrade.

**Retain habits:**
- Keep the meal plan and activity level
- Stick to weekend rules
- Repeat habits that felt natural
- Plan ahead

**Assess progress:**
- Reflect on what worked — specific behaviors, not just outcomes
- Identify growth areas honestly
- Adjust goals for the next 30 days if needed
- Find the balance between structure and flexibility

**Schedule consistency (for life after 6FIX):**
- Set a weekly check-in day with yourself
- Use the same meal plan format
- Schedule "you time" — movement, recovery, prep

**Long-term actions:**
- Continue logging weekly data (weight trend, not obsessive daily)
- Add new habits slowly — one at a time
- Problem-solve challenges as they come, don't abandon the system

**Final check-in (Saturday Week 6):**
- Final weight + waist measurement
- After photos
- 1-on-1 evaluation call or voice message
- Top 3 habits identified (the ones that changed everything)
- 30-day continuation plan written together

**Final mission:**
1 Hour of 6FIX Time — reflect, review, plan forward
*Points: 10 completed / 5 effort / 0 none*

**Key message:**
"The challenge ends. The system continues. You're in control now."

## Saturday Check-In System

**Format:** 10–15 minutes. Same four steps every week. Consistency builds trust.

**Step 1 — Wins (3 minutes)**
Ask: "What went well this week? What did you do that you're proud of?"
Reinforce the behavior, not the outcome. ("You logged every meal even on the hard day" — not "Great job!")
Do not rush to challenges.

**Step 2 — Challenges (4 minutes)**
Ask: "What felt hard? Where did you fall short of what you wanted?"
Listen without judgment. Do not problem-solve in this step.
Reflect back what you heard before moving on.

**Step 3 — Adjustments (5 minutes)**
Ask: "What's one thing we could change to make next week easier?"
Client speaks first. Co-create the solution.
Pick ONE adjustment. Not three.

**Step 4 — Next Steps (2 minutes)**
State: "So next week, your focus is ___. Yes?"
Confirm verbally. Send the same line in writing within 1 hour.
Schedule next check-in before ending.

**Check-in data to collect each Saturday:**
- Weight (kg)
- Waist measurement (cm)
- Steps average for the week
- Habit completion rate (self-reported, 0–7 days)
- Hardest moment of the week (1 line)
- Best win of the week (1 line)

## Compliance Dashboard — Phase 1 Targets

Review these numbers every Saturday. If any fall below target, adjust the protocol — not the client.

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Day 14 adherence rate | % of clients who hit 5+ of first 6 activity targets | ≥ 80% |
| 30-day retention | % still actively checking in at Day 30 | ≥ 85% |
| Phase 1 completion | % who complete all 6 weeks | ≥ 70% |
| Habit consistency score | Average daily habit completion rate (self-reported) | ≥ 75% |
| Check-in completion rate | % of Saturday check-ins submitted | ≥ 90% |

## Scoring System

**Body Composition Score**
Relative improvement — not absolute weight lost.

Formula: (Starting BF% − Ending BF%) ÷ Ending BF% × 100
*Example: 30% → 28% = 2 ÷ 28 = 7.14% improvement score*

**Weekly Challenge Points**
- Full completion = 10 points
- Effort shown = 5 points
- No completion = 0 points
- Tie-breaker: weight-loss percentage
