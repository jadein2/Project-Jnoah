## Wix Enrollment Page Brief

**Page URL:** restartfitnessph.com/6fix (or /join-6fix)
**Page purpose:** Convert warm leads into enrolled clients. This page is the destination after "DM 6FIX" flow and all social bio links.

---

### Page Structure

**Section 1 — Hero**
- Headline: "Stop Guessing. Follow a System."
- Subheadline: "6FIX is a 6-week habit and health reset built around structure, not motivation."
- CTA button: "Reserve Your Spot" → scrolls to enrollment form or payment link
- Background: clean, minimal — beige or dark with a strong coaching image

**Section 2 — Is This You?**
- 4–5 pain point statements in a card layout:
  - "I'll start on Monday." (and never do)
  - "I don't know where to begin."
  - "I always stop after a few weeks."
  - "I'm tired of trying the same things."
  - "I need someone to actually guide me."
- Closing line: "You don't need motivation. You need a system."

**Section 3 — What You Get**
- Clear, structured 6-week roadmap
- Universal training standards for all levels
- Practical whole-food nutrition guidance
- Habit-building systems for consistency
- App-based tracking for accountability (Trainerize)
- Weekly coaching check-ins
- A repeatable process beyond the challenge
- Closing line: "No guessing. Clear actions every week."

**Section 4 — How It Works**
- 3-step simple visual:
  1. Free Onboarding — orientation, baseline assessment, how the system works
  2. 6 Weeks of Coaching — habits, nutrition, accountability, weekly check-ins
  3. Keep the System — what you build is yours after Day 42

**Section 5 — Social Proof**
- 3–4 client testimonials (photo + 2–3 sentences each)
- Focus on the experience: "I expected it to be harder. It was manageable and guided."
- Not just physical results — include mindset and habit shifts

**Section 6 — Pricing + Enrollment**
- Program investment: ₱[amount]
- Group discount: 10% off when joining with a friend
- What's included (bullet list — simple)
- CTA: "Join Now" → payment link or QR code
- Secondary CTA: "Have questions? DM '6FIX' on Instagram"

**Section 7 — About Coach Jap**
- 3–4 sentences. Credentials, experience, why he built this.
- Photo — coaching environment, not a gym selfie.

**Section 8 — FAQ**
- Do I need gym access? (No — activity is based on your level)
- What app do I need? (Trainerize — free download)
- Is this online or in-person? (Online coaching + optional in-person for Olongapo clients)
- What if I miss a week? (The system handles it — we adjust, not restart)
- When does the next cohort start? ([Date])

**Footer CTA:** "Reserve Your Onboarding Spot" — links to enrollment form

## Email Automation Sequence

Set up in Wix Automations (or Mailchimp if preferred). Triggered by enrollment form submission or payment confirmation.

### Email 1 — Enrollment Confirmation

**Subject:** You're in. Here's what happens next.

>

>
> Here's what happens next:
>
> 1. You'll receive the intake form within 24 hours. Complete it within 48 hours — this is how I learn what you need.

## Notes for Solo Operator

Emails 3–6 contain personalized references (nutrition habit, sleep habit) that need to be updated per cohort or per client if doing individual plans. Template the variable parts in brackets and update them in the Wix email editor before each cohort starts. This takes 15–20 minutes per cohort — not per client.
