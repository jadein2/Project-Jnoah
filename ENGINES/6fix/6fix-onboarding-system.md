# 6FIX Onboarding System
**Program:** 6FIX — Restart Fitness PH
**Phase:** Week 0 (Day -7 to Day 0)
**Framework:** ADHERE Compliance System
**Last updated:** 2026-05-26

---

## Purpose

This document is the complete onboarding protocol for every 6FIX client. It runs from 7 days before program start to Day 0. No client enters the program without completing this sequence.

The onboarding has two jobs: collect the data Jap needs to coach the client, and install the identity shift before the program begins. Clients who complete this protocol are significantly less likely to drop out in weeks 2–4.

---

## Onboarding Sequence Overview

| Day | Action | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Day -7 | Send intake form + 48-hour completion deadline | Jap (automated) |
| Day -5 | 30-minute onboarding call / video conference | Jap |

| Day -1 | Confirm Day 1 in writing | Jap |
| Day 0 | Client added to Trainerize, program assigned | Jap |
| Day 0 | Commitment video recorded by client | Client |

## Tool 1 — ADHERE Intake Form

Deliver as a Google Form, Typeform, or Trainerize intake. Every field maps to one ADHERE letter. Set a 48-hour completion deadline from send date.

---

### A — Anchor (Baseline Assessment)

1. What is your primary goal for the next 6 weeks? Be specific — a number, a feeling, or a behavior change.
2. Why now? What changed in the last 30–60 days that brought you here?
3. Previous attempts: What have you tried before? What worked, what didn't, what got in the way?
4. Walk me through a typical weekday — morning to night. (Free text)
5. Average sleep hours per night: ____
6. Sleep quality right now, 1–10: ____
7. Stress level today, 1–10: ____
8. Energy level today, 1–10: ____
9. Complete this sentence honestly: "When I think about starting a fitness or nutrition program, I feel ___."

---

### D — Decide Minimums

10. Maximum training sessions or physical activity days per week you can realistically commit to: ____
11. Minimum — your non-negotiable floor, the number you will hit no matter what: ____
12. Preferred days and times for training or movement: ____
13. One nutrition habit you can commit to for the next 6 weeks, no excuses. Keep it simple:
    *(Examples: drink 2L water daily / eat protein at every meal / no sugary drinks on weekdays)*
14. One sleep habit you can commit to for the next 6 weeks:
    *(Examples: in bed by 11pm / no phone 30 min before sleep / same wake time daily)*

---

### H — Habit Stack

15. List 3 things you do every single day without thinking — habits you never skip:
    - Anchor 1: ____
    - Anchor 2: ____
    - Anchor 3: ____
16. Which new habit will attach to which anchor?
    *(Example: "After my morning coffee, I will log yesterday's weight.")*

---

### E — Engineer Environment

17. Where will you do your physical activity? *(Home / Gym / Outdoors / No fixed location)*
18. What are your top 3 obstacles to being consistent right now? Be honest:
    - Obstacle 1: ____
    - Obstacle 2: ____
    - Obstacle 3: ____
19. Who shops for food in your household? Who cooks?
20. What does your fridge typically look like on a weekday evening?
21. What currently interrupts your best intentions? *(Schedule, family, work, phone, etc.)*

---

### R — Record

22. How do you prefer to track things? *(App / Notebook / Spreadsheet / Photo log / In-app)*
23. How will you log your food? *(App / Photo / Written / I won't — be honest)*
24. Are you willing to share your weekly log with the coach? *(Yes / Yes but nervous / Prefer not)*

---

### E — Engage (Accountability Design)

25. How do you want to be held accountable?
    *(DM check-in / Group chat / Weekly call / Progress post / Financial stake / Combination)*
26. Best time of day for check-ins: ____
27. What should the coach do if you go silent for 48 hours?
    *(Send a DM / Call / Leave me alone / Escalate to a family member I'll name)*
28. Anything else you want the coach to know before we start?

## Activity Assessment (Jap reviews after intake, before onboarding call)

Based on intake answers, assign one of three starting activity tracks. This is the "case-by-case basis" decision for Phase 1.

| Track | Criteria | Phase 1 Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| **Movement Only** | Sedentary baseline, high stress, poor sleep (≤6hrs), no recent exercise history | Walking targets only. 5,000–8,000 steps/day. No structured training yet. |
| **Light Active** | Some activity history, moderate stress, occasional exercise, sleep 6–7hrs | Walking + 1–2 bodyweight or low-intensity sessions/week. Keep it easy to win. |
| **Ready to Train** | Active history, good sleep (7+hrs), stress manageable, requesting structure | Walking + 2–3 sessions/week. Can follow a basic training block. |

**Rule:** Default down, not up. If unsure between tracks, assign the easier one. Week 2 decision point will escalate if the client is underloaded.

## Onboarding Call Guide (30 minutes)

Run this call before Day 0. Video conference preferred.

**Minutes 1–5 — Welcome and reframe**
- Thank them for completing the intake.
- State one specific thing from their intake that shows you read it.
- Reframe the program: "This is not about punishment or perfection. It's about building a system that works for your actual life."

**Minutes 6–15 — Review intake together**
- Confirm their goal (make it specific if it isn't).
- Review their minimum commitment floor. Get verbal agreement.
- Confirm their one nutrition habit and one sleep habit. Write them down together.
- Confirm habit stacks: "So after [anchor], you will [new habit]?"
- Walk through their top 3 obstacles. Fix the environment, not the motivation: "What's one thing we can remove or change right now to make [obstacle] less likely?"

**Minutes 16–25 — Co-design accountability**
- Ask: "How do you want me to hold you accountable? What works for you?"
- Set check-in format and time.
- Address the 48-hour silence protocol. Get verbal agreement on what happens.
- Confirm their activity track and what Week 1 looks like.

**Minutes 26–30 — Confirm Day 1**
- Walk through what Day 1 looks like: Trainerize setup, first habit, first check-in.
- Send the Welcome Kit within 24 hours.
- End with: "I'll see you on Day 1. You already did the hard part — you decided."

## Welcome Kit Contents

Send Day -3 to Day -1. Can be automated via Wix email or Trainerize message.

1. **Program overview** — 6 weeks, what each week focuses on, what success looks like
2. **Week 1 focus card** — the specific habits, tasks, and daily mission for Week 1
3. **Their confirmed minimum commitment** — written back to them exactly as they said it
4. **Their one nutrition habit** — written as a clear rule, not a suggestion
5. **Their one sleep habit** — same
6. **Tracking setup** — how to log in Trainerize, what to submit each day
7. **Saturday check-in reminder** — what to expect, what to prepare
8. **Commitment video prompt** (see below)

## Commitment Video Protocol

**When:** Day 0, before the first workout or habit action.

**What the client records:**
- Their name and start date
- Their specific 6-week goal
- One sentence about why this time is different
- A promise to themselves — in their own words

**Rules:**
- Video stays private. Client stores it themselves.
- Jap does not view it unless client chooses to share.
- Revealed on Day 42 (program completion) as a reflection tool.
- Used as transformation storytelling content only with explicit written permission.

**Prompt to send:**
> "Before we start — record a 60-second video. Just you, honest. Say your name, your goal, why now, and one promise to yourself. Keep it private. On Day 42, you'll watch it back. That's the moment."

## Onboarding Completion Checklist

Before a client is considered fully onboarded:

- [ ] Intake form completed (all fields)
- [ ] Onboarding call completed
- [ ] Activity track assigned
- [ ] Minimum commitment floor confirmed verbally
- [ ] One nutrition habit confirmed
- [ ] One sleep habit confirmed
- [ ] Habit stacks identified (at least one)
- [ ] Accountability format agreed and logged
- [ ] Welcome Kit sent
- [ ] Trainerize account active and program assigned
- [ ] Commitment video recorded (client confirms)
- [ ] Day 1 confirmed in writing

If any item is incomplete, delay program start. Do not onboard a client halfway.
