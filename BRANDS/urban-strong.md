## Positioning

**Primary positioning statement:**
Urban Strong Fitness is a coached fitness system — not a standard gym. Every member trains with intent, accountability, and professional guidance.

**Short line:**
A coached fitness system for busy adults who want to get leaner, stronger, and more consistent — built in Olongapo.

**Business core (use as message anchors):**
- We are not a standard gym. We are a coached fitness system.
- Every member trains with intent, accountability, and professional guidance.
- Coach-to-member accountability inside a city where no other fitness provider operates at this standard.
- We don't just give you equipment access. We give you a coach.
- No shortcuts. Just results.

**What the brand is not:**
- A standard open gym with floor access and no coaching
- A cheap monthly membership play
- A competition gym or CrossFit box
- A bootcamp business
- A motivation page or transformation hype brand

**Competitive position:**
Urban Strong Fitness is the only coaching-grade fitness provider in Olongapo with a structured program ladder, mandatory onboarding (Free Assessment), and outcome-oriented professional coaching. The local market is dominated by open gyms with zero professional coaching — Urban Strong is in a different category.

## Audience

**Primary age range:** 25–50
**Location:** Olongapo City and Subic area
**Audience label:** "busy adults" is acceptable for Urban Strong. (This differs from Restart Fitness PH, where "busy adults" as a headline label is banned.)

**Common profiles:** Professionals, government employees, military-adjacent personnel, parents, small business owners. People who understand the value of fitness, have tried unstructured gym access, and failed without a system, coach, or accountability structure.

**Core problems they have:** No clear plan, random workouts, inconsistent follow-through, no accountability, gym memberships they don't use, tried programs and quit at 2–4 weeks.

**Desired outcomes:** Get leaner, get stronger, build consistency, feel and perform better, know what they're doing, get coached — not just handed equipment.

**Emotional drivers:** Supported, clear on the plan, accountable, part of a serious community, not wasting time, progress they can see.

## Voice

**Brand voice profile:**
- Motivational and empowering — not hype, not bro-science. Empowering means action-oriented, outcome-focused, real.
- Confident but approachable — serious coaching standard delivered without intimidation
- Progress and discipline focused — the work is the message
- Clear, action-oriented language — short sentences, strong verbs

**Urban Strong voice vs. Restart Fitness PH voice:**
Restart PH is calm, editorial, structured. Urban Strong is more direct, more energetic, more local. Both are anti-hype — but Urban Strong leads with momentum; Restart PH leads with clarity.

**Tone by platform:**

| Platform | Tone |
|---|---|
| Website | Professional, direct, locally grounded, credible |
| Instagram / Facebook | Punchy, motivational, real, action-driven |
| Blog | Educational, practical, coach perspective, outcome-led |
| Email | Direct, personal, progress-oriented |
| Sales / assessment | Confident, proof-driven, supportive |

**Avoid sounding:** Like a hype gym, cheap promo flyer, CrossFit competition page, motivational poster with no substance. Motivational ≠ empty. Every motivational line should anchor to something real.

## Offer architecture

Every new member enters through the Free Assessment and progresses based on goals, budget, and commitment. No program is sold in isolation — each feeds the next.

| Stage | Offer | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Entry | **Free Assessment** | Mandatory for all new members. No exceptions. |
| Onboarding | **ON-RAMP** | Beginner pathway into coached programs |
| Core coached | **UrbanFit** | Group coaching membership — primary product |
| Intensive | **Semi-Private Training** | Small group, higher coach attention |
| Individual | **Personal Training** | 1-on-1 coaching |
| Specialist | **Nutrition Coaching** | Add-on coaching track |
| Campaign | **Urban Strong 6-Week Reset** | Time-bound reset for new and returning clients |
| Coming: Version 2 | **Coached-Access** | PHP 1,500–2,200/month floor access with coach on post |

**Membership rules:**
- Pay-first. No payment, no access. No exceptions.
- Late payment fee: PHP 300 per instance
- Membership freeze: 30-day maximum per year
- Cancellation: 30 days written notice required
- Assessment required: all new members before joining any program

## Color system

| Token | Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| usf-black | Urban Black | `#111111` | **Primary color** — backgrounds, dominant text, dark surfaces |
| usf-blue | Urban Blue | `#0292b7` | **Secondary color** — CTA, emphasis, energy, key accents |
| usf-white | Clean White | `#F1FAEE` | **Accent color** — text on dark, light backgrounds, negative space |
| usf-gray | Steel Gray | `#514d52` | **Neutral tone** — body text, secondary copy, muted elements |

### Color logic
- Urban Black is the foundation — dominant background and text
- Urban Blue is the action and energy color — CTA, emphasis, accent moments
- Clean White provides contrast and breathing room
- Steel Gray handles secondary information and body copy

### Color rules
- Red — only where action, emphasis, or energy is required
- Never use red as a large background
- Never use more than two dominant colors in one visual
- Maintain strong contrast — text must be readable against background at all times
- No gradients (consistent with universal design standard)

### Approved combinations

| Combination | Use |
|---|---|
| Clean White on Urban Black | Hero sections, social posts, primary layouts |
| Urban Blue on Urban Black | CTA emphasis, key words, energy moments |
| Urban Black on Clean White | Light-background editorial layouts |
| Steel Gray on Urban Black | Body copy on dark backgrounds |

## Typography

### Locked font stack

| Role | Font | Weight / Style |
|---|---|---|
| Headings / Emphasis | **Montserrat** | **Bold** |
| Body / Descriptions | **Open Sans** | Regular |

### Rules
- Montserrat Bold is the identity font. Use for headlines, taglines, CTAs, section titles, emphasis words.
- Open Sans Regular handles all body copy, descriptions, captions, secondary information.
- No more than two font weights in a single layout.
- Short, punchy headlines — Urban Strong copy is action-oriented, not long-winded.

### Retired fonts (do not use for Urban Strong)
Cormorant Garamond · DM Sans · Playfair Display · Bodoni · Helvetica
*(Note: Cormorant Garamond + DM Sans are Restart Fitness PH's locked fonts — keep them separate.)*

## Imagery style

**Visual style:** High-contrast, gritty gym environments. Real athletes in motion. Focus on strength, sweat, and determination.

**Rules:**
- Minimal filters — authentic tones, not over-edited
- Real people training — not stock photo fitness models
- Emphasize: strength movements, coaching moments, effort, progress, sweat, focus
- High contrast is the visual standard — Urban Black + Urban Blue + Clean White should feel present even in photography choices

**Avoid:** Overly polished commercial fitness photography · excessive filters · neon gym aesthetics · stock photo smiles · posed fitness marketing looks · anything that looks like it could be any gym anywhere.
