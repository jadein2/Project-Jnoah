### Idea #7 — Both (Instagram & Facebook)

- **Format:** Educational Infographic (single image or mini-carousel, 3 slides)
- **Hook / Headline:** "3 fitness myths that are keeping you stuck (backed by science)"

  - Final slide: "Stop Googling. Start asking a coach."
- **Caption Starter:** "I hear these three myths at least once a week — and they're holding so many people back from the results they deserve. Let's set the record straight."
- **CTA:** "Save this and send it to someone who still believes myth #2. Let's spread facts, not fear."
