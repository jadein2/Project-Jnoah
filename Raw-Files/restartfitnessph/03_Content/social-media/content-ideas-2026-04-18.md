## Idea #1 — Platform: Both (Instagram + Facebook)

- **Format:** Carousel (6–8 slides)
- **Hook / Headline:** "5 Beginner-Friendly Moves That Build Real Strength (No Gym Needed)"
- **Key Points / Talking Points:**
  - Bodyweight squat — form cue: knees track over toes, chest proud
  - Glute bridge — squeeze at the top for 2 seconds, don't arch the low back
  - Incline push-up (against wall or bench) — protect the shoulders while building pressing strength
  - Bird dog — the underrated core + stability builder
  - Dead bug — teaches bracing without straining the neck
  - Bonus slide: "Do 2 rounds, 3x per week — progress shows up in 3 weeks"
- **Caption Starter:** "You don't need a gym to get strong — you need consistency and the right basics. Save this carousel and start tomorrow."
- **CTA:** "Save this for your next workout and tag someone who's just getting started."
- **Hashtag Themes:** beginner fitness, home workout, strength training, fitness coaching, movement basics

## Idea #4 — Platform: Instagram (Carousel) + Facebook (Feed post)

- **Format:** Carousel (5 slides) — myth vs. truth format
- **Hook / Headline:** "3 Nutrition Myths That Are Quietly Wrecking Your Progress"
- **Key Points / Talking Points:**
  - Myth: "Carbs make you gain weight." Truth: Excess calories do — carbs fuel your workouts and recovery.
  - Myth: "Skipping meals helps you lose weight faster." Truth: It usually leads to overeating later and tanks your energy.
  - Myth: "Eating clean means cutting out everything you love." Truth: Sustainable nutrition includes the foods you enjoy — in the right portions.
  - Bottom line slide: "Extreme rules don't work because they aren't livable. Balance is what sticks."
- **Caption Starter:** "If you've been stuck in a cycle of restrict → binge → guilt, it's not your willpower — it's the rules you've been handed. Let's fix that."
- **CTA:** "Save this and share with a friend who's been fighting with food rules."
- **Hashtag Themes:** nutrition myths, sustainable eating, fitness education, food freedom, wellness coaching
