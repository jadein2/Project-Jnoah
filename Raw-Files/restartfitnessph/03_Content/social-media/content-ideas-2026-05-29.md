## Idea #1 — Instagram

- **Format:** Carousel (5 slides)
- **Hook / Headline:** "5 exercises you're probably doing wrong (and how to fix them)"
- **Key Points:**
  - Squat depth and knee tracking — most people cut it short
  - Plank form — sagging hips vs. piked hips, find the middle
  - Deadlift setup — the bar should be over mid-foot, not your toes
  - Lunges — front knee drifting too far forward
  - Push-ups — elbow flare vs. tucked position
- **Caption Starter:** You don't need a new workout program. You need to do the basics better. Here are 5 form fixes that make a real difference.
- **CTA:** Save this so you can check your form next session 💪
- **Hashtag Themes:** fitness tips, workout form, beginner fitness, coaching, exercise technique
