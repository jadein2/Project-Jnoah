## Idea #1 — Instagram

- **Format:** Carousel (5 slides)
- **Hook / Headline:** "Slow down your reps and watch what happens"
- **Key Points:**
  - Slide 1: Hook — "The fastest way to get stronger? Go slower."
  - Slide 2: What tempo training is — control the lowering phase (3–4 seconds down)
  - Slide 3: Why it works — more time under tension = more muscle activation with the same weight
  - Slide 4: Try it today — pick any exercise, count "3-Mississippi" on every rep going down
  - Slide 5: "You don't always need heavier weights. You need better reps."
- **Caption Starter:** Most people rush through reps just to finish the set. But the part you're skipping — the slow, controlled lowering — is where most of the growth happens.
- **CTA:** Save this and try it on your next set. Come back and tell me how it felt 💪
- **Hashtag Themes:** training tips, tempo training, strength building, fitness education, coaching
