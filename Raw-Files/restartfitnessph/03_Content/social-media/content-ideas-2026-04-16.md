## Idea #1 — Both (Instagram & Facebook)

- **Format:** Carousel (5–7 slides)
- **Hook / Headline:** "5 signs your body is begging you to move — not punish yourself"
- **Key Points / Talking Points:**
  - Constant fatigue even after sleep = your body needs movement, not rest
  - Mood dips and irritability often vanish after 20 minutes of exercise
  - Cravings spike when we're sedentary — movement resets hunger hormones
  - Stiffness and low-back tension = your hips and spine need more mobility
  - The goal isn't burning calories — it's building energy
- **Caption Starter:** "Your body isn't broken. It's just sending you signals — and most of us have been taught to ignore them. Here's what to listen for instead."
- **CTA:** "Save this and check in with your body today. Which one hit home for you? Comment below 👇"
- **Hashtag Themes:** body awareness, fitness mindset, movement habits, wellness education, coaching

## Idea #5 — Facebook (long-form works well here)

- **Format:** Feed Post — Educational myth-bust with personal coaching perspective
- **Hook / Headline:** "MYTH: You have to work out every day to see results. Here's the truth."
- **Key Points / Talking Points:**
  - Rest days are where the actual progress happens — muscles grow during recovery
  - Overtraining leads to fatigue, injury, and quitting — not a six-pack
  - Consistency over 6 months beats intensity over 2 weeks every time
  - A smart 3–4 day/week plan outperforms a burned-out 7-day grind
  - Your goal: sustainable movement, not punishment
- **Caption Starter:** "I see so many people quit fitness because they went too hard, too fast — and their body gave out. You don't need to earn rest. Rest IS part of the work."
- **CTA:** "Save this and share it with someone who thinks more is always better. 🙌"
- **Hashtag Themes:** fitness myths, rest and recovery, sustainable fitness, coaching advice, wellness
