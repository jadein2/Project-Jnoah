**Idea #5 — Both (Instagram & Facebook)**
- **Format:** Carousel — Myth-Busting Edition (5 slides)
- **Hook / Headline:** "5 fitness myths that are keeping you stuck (backed by science)"
- **Key Points / Talking Points:**
  - Myth 1: "Cardio is the best way to lose fat." → Truth: strength training is your metabolic best friend
  - Myth 2: "You need to be sore to have a good workout." → Truth: soreness ≠ effectiveness
  - Myth 3: "Eating after 8pm makes you gain weight." → Truth: total calories > meal timing

  - Myth 5: "You have to go all-in or it won't work." → Truth: small, sustainable changes win every time
- **Caption Starter:** "If you've believed any of these, it's not your fault — the fitness industry loves to overcomplicate things. Let's clear the air."
- **CTA:** Save this and share it with anyone who's been held back by fitness misinformation.
- **Hashtag Themes:** fitness myths, science-backed, nutrition facts, strength training, education
