## Idea #1 — Instagram

- **Format:** Carousel (5 slides)
- **Hook / Headline:** "The 3-goal rule that keeps my clients from burning out"
- **Key Points:**
  - Slide 1: Hook — "Stop setting 10 goals. Set 3."
  - Slide 2: Pick ONE fitness goal (e.g., train 3x/week)
  - Slide 3: Pick ONE nutrition goal (e.g., drink 2L of water daily)
  - Slide 4: Pick ONE life goal (e.g., sleep by 10pm on weeknights)
  - Slide 5: "Master 3 before adding more. Progress isn't about doing everything — it's about doing the right things long enough."
- **Caption Starter:** Most people don't fail because they're lazy. They fail because they try to change everything at once. Here's the framework I give every new client on day one.
- **CTA:** Save this and write down your 3 goals in the comments — I'll give you feedback 📝
- **Hashtag Themes:** goal setting, coaching framework, beginner fitness, personal growth, accountability

## Idea #2 — Both (Instagram + Facebook)

- **Format:** Feed post with graphic (educational)
- **Hook / Headline:** "Your workout is only 20% of the equation"
- **Key Points:**
  - Sleep is where your muscles actually repair — aim for 7+ hours
  - Stress management matters — cortisol blocks recovery and fat loss
  - Hydration affects performance more than most supplements ever will
  - Consistency in daily movement (walking, stairs) adds up faster than weekend gym binges
  - The other 23 hours of your day determine your results more than that 1 hour in the gym
- **Caption Starter:** You're working hard in the gym. I see you. But if you're sleeping 5 hours and running on coffee and stress, your body can't keep up. Here's what the other 80% looks like.
- **CTA:** Which of these do you struggle with most? Drop a number 1–4 below 👇
- **Hashtag Themes:** recovery, sleep fitness, holistic health, wellness habits, coaching

## Idea #6 — Instagram

- **Format:** Story series (4 frames — interactive quiz)
- **Hook / Headline:** "Pop quiz: How well do you actually know your nutrition?"
- **Key Points:**
  - Frame 1: "Which has more protein — 1 egg or 100g of rice?" (Quiz sticker — answer: egg, ~6g vs ~2.7g)
  - Frame 2: "True or false: Eating after 8pm makes you gain weight." (Quiz sticker — answer: false, total daily intake matters, not timing)
  - Frame 3: "What burns more calories at rest — muscle or fat?" (Quiz sticker — answer: muscle)

- **Caption Starter:** (Story text overlay) "Let's test what you actually know vs. what the internet told you. No judgment — just facts."
- **CTA:** DM me "NUTRITION" and I'll send you a free cheat sheet on the basics.

## Idea #7 — Both (Instagram + Facebook)

- **Format:** Feed post with graphic (community / engagement)

- **Key Points:**
