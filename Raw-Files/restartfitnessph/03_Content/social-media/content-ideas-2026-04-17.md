## Idea #1 — Instagram & Facebook

- **Format:** Carousel (6 slides)
- **Hook / Headline:** "5 tiny habits that quietly change your body (no gym required) 👇"
- **Key Points / Talking Points:**
  - Slide 1: Drink a full glass of water before your first coffee — hydration resets hunger cues
  - Slide 2: Walk 10 minutes after every meal — blood sugar stability, better digestion
  - Slide 3: Protein with breakfast (aim 25–30g) — cuts cravings for the whole day
  - Slide 4: Stand up and stretch every hour — protects your back, boosts energy
  - Slide 5: Lights out 30 minutes earlier — recovery is where results are made
  - Slide 6: "You don't need a 2-hour workout. You need these 5 tiny choices on repeat."
- **Caption Starter:** "The people with the most consistent results aren't doing anything extreme. They're just stacking small, boring habits that work."
- **CTA:** "Save this and try ONE habit this week. Tell me which one you're starting with in the comments 👇"
- **Hashtag Themes:** healthy habits, sustainable fitness, wellness tips, fitness coaching, daily routines

## Idea #5 — Instagram Carousel

- **Format:** Educational / Myth-Busting Carousel (7 slides)
- **Hook / Headline:** "4 'weight loss' tips that are actually slowing you down 🚩"
- **Key Points / Talking Points:**
  - Slide 1: Hook + promise
  - Slide 2: "Eat as little as possible" — why extreme deficits backfire (metabolism, cravings, binges)
  - Slide 3: "Do cardio every day" — overtraining is real, and strength training changes body composition
  - Slide 4: "Cut out all carbs" — why your workouts, hormones, and brain actually need them
  - Slide 5: "Step on the scale every day" — daily fluctuations are not progress markers
  - Slide 6: What to do instead (eat enough, lift weights, track non-scale wins)
  - Slide 7: "You deserve an approach that works WITH your body, not against it."
- **Caption Starter:** "Most mainstream weight loss advice is built to keep you stuck. Let's talk about what actually works (and what doesn't)."
- **CTA:** "Save this for when you're tempted by another crash diet 🚫 — and share it with someone who needs it."
- **Hashtag Themes:** myth busting, evidence based, sustainable weight loss, fitness facts, nutrition coaching
