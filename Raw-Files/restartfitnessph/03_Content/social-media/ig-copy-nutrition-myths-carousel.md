## CAPTION

The fitness industry loves to complicate food.

Here are 5 nutrition myths you can officially let go of today 👇

**Myth #1 — Carbs make you gain weight.**
Wrong. Excess calories do. Carbs are fuel — your brain and muscles run on them. Cut them out and you'll find yourself tired, foggy, and craving everything by 3pm.

**Myth #2 — You have to eat clean 100% of the time.**
Nope. The most sustainable approach is consistency, not perfection. An 80/90% approach to whole foods gives you real results *and* lets you actually enjoy your life.

**Myth #3 — Fat-free = healthier.**
Manufacturers replace fat with sugar to keep things tasting good. Healthy fats — avocado, nuts, olive oil — are non-negotiable for hormones, satiety, and your brain.

**Myth #4 — Skipping breakfast speeds up weight loss.**
It depends on YOU. Some people thrive fasting. Others crash hard without a morning meal. There is no one-size-fits-all — there's only what works for your body and your life.

**Myth #5 — Supplements replace real food.**
They supplement. That's literally the name. Whole food provides thousands of compounds no capsule can replicate. Build the plate first — fill the gaps second.
