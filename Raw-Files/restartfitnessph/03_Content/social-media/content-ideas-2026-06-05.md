## Idea #7 — Instagram

- **Format:** Carousel (4 slides — coaching value, educational)
- **Hook / Headline:** "Progressive overload, explained in 60 seconds"
- **Key Points:**
  - Slide 1: "If you're doing the same weight, same reps, same routine every week — your body has no reason to change."
  - Slide 2: Progressive overload = gradually increasing the demand on your muscles over time. More weight, more reps, more sets, better form, slower tempo — all count.
  - Slide 3: You don't need to add weight every single session. Even one extra rep is forward progress.
  - Slide 4: "Your body adapts to what you give it. Give it a reason to grow."
- **Caption Starter:** This is the most important principle in training that most people ignore — not because it's complicated, but because it's not exciting. Here's why it matters more than any workout trend.
- **CTA:** Save this and check your training log. Are you progressing or just repeating? If you don't have a log, that's the first problem. DM "RESTART" and let's fix that.
- **Hashtag Themes:** progressive overload, training principle, strength gains, coaching, fitness education
