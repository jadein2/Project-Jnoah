## SLIDE 2 — Myth #1

**Myth:**
🚫 "Carbs make you fat."

**Truth:**
✅ Carbs are your body's *preferred* fuel source.

Weight gain comes from a consistent calorie surplus — not carbs themselves. Whole grains, fruit, and rice aren't your enemies. Cutting them out just makes you tired and cranky.

*Eat smarter, not less.*

---

## SLIDE 3 — Myth #2

**Myth:**
🚫 "You have to eat every 2–3 hours to boost your metabolism."

**Truth:**
✅ Meal timing matters far less than total daily intake.

Whether you eat 3 meals or 6, what actually counts is the quality and quantity of what you're eating overall. Eat in a way that *works for your life* — not someone else's schedule.

---

## SLIDE 4 — Myth #3

**Myth:**
🚫 "Clean eating means zero treats — ever."

**Truth:**
✅ The all-or-nothing mindset is the #1 reason diets fail.

Rigid restriction leads to bingeing. A sustainable approach means 80–90% nourishing foods *and* room for the things you love. Joy is part of a healthy diet too.

*Balance > perfection. Every time.*

---

## SLIDE 5 — Myth #4

**Myth:**
🚫 "You need protein shakes to build muscle."

**Truth:**
✅ Whole food protein works just as well — if not better.

Eggs, chicken, fish, legumes, Greek yogurt. Your body doesn't care where the protein comes from. Supplements are a *convenience*, not a requirement. Real food first, always.

---

## SLIDE 6 — Myth #5 + CTA Slide

**Myth:**
🚫 "Detox teas and cleanses flush out toxins."

**Truth:**
✅ Your liver and kidneys are your built-in detox system — and they're working 24/7.

No tea required. The best thing you can do? Drink water, eat fiber, sleep well, and move your body.
