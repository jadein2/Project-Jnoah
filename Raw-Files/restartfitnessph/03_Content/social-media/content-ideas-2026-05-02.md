### Idea #1 — Both (Instagram & Facebook)

- **Format:** Carousel (5–7 slides)
- **Hook / Headline:** "5 exercises you're probably doing wrong (and how to fix them today)"
- **Key Points / Talking Points:**
  - Squats: knees caving in — cue "spread the floor with your feet"
  - Planks: hips sagging or piking — focus on a straight line from head to heels
  - Push-ups: flaring elbows at 90° — tuck them closer to 45°
  - Lunges: front knee shooting past toes — step wider, sit back
  - Deadlifts: rounding the lower back — hinge at hips, chest proud
- **Caption Starter:** "You're putting in the work — let's make sure it actually counts. Here are 5 common form mistakes I see every single week (and the easy fixes that change everything)."
- **CTA:** "Save this so you can check your form next session 💪"
