## Pro Tips

- **Generate one slide at a time** for best results — ChatGPT handles single complex compositions better than batches.
- **If text rendering is off**, tell ChatGPT: "Please make sure the text is spelled exactly as written, with no extra words or letters."
- **For consistency**, after generating slide 1 successfully, reference it: "Use the same exact style, colors, and layout as the previous image."
- **If you want a photo-based version instead**, add to each prompt: "Include a subtle, desaturated background photo of [gym equipment / healthy food / person training] with a dark overlay at 80% opacity so text remains fully readable."
