## 2. Non-Negotiable Rebrand Decision

URBN Athletics is the new main gym brand.

Urban Strong Fitness becomes the legacy foundation and origin story.

This is a name-change rebrand only. The business is not being rebuilt from scratch.

The rebrand keeps:

- Same coaching standard
- Same community
- Same mission
- Same operating model
- Same people
- Same commitment to structured training

The public explanation is:

> Urban Strong Fitness built the foundation. URBN Athletics carries it forward.

Do not change Restart Fitness PH as part of this rebrand.

It is a coaching environment where members are guided through proper movement, progressive training, better habits, and clearer fitness pathways.

The business should be understood as:

- Strength and conditioning facility

- Premium local coaching environment

## 5. Positioning

## Simple Positioning Statement

URBN Athletics is a coached fitness gym for busy adults who want to move better, get stronger, lose body fat, and build a more consistent fitness lifestyle.

URBN Athletics provides structured coaching for adults who want more than gym access. Through coach-led training, practical nutrition guidance, and accountability, members follow a clear system designed to improve strength, health, movement, and long-term consistency.

Most gyms give people access to equipment.

URBN Athletics gives people coaching, structure, accountability, movement standards, and a clear path to progress.

The value is not equipment access alone.

## 7. Primary Audience

URBN Athletics is primarily for busy adults who want to lose body fat, get stronger, improve health, and become more consistent.

Core audience:

- Adults around ages 28-55
- Working professionals
- Parents
- Busy people with limited time
- Beginners and inconsistent exercisers

## Secondary Audience

- Students
- Former athletes

- People preparing for hybrid fitness events

## Hybrid Track Audience

The Hybrid Track is for people with existing fitness background who are ready for higher training demand.

Use the Physical Fitness Test as the readiness gate. The PFT protects safety, sets expectations, and creates a clear performance pathway.

## 9. Offer Architecture

Public offers should support the coaching-first position.

The current program ladder is:

1. **FIT45** - low-barrier beginner class for daily movement, confidence, and consistency
2. **On-Ramp / Foundation Course** - required bridge into URBN FIT or URBN HYBRID
3. **URBN FIT** - main coached group training class
4. **URBN HYBRID** - endurance and strength training for fitness racing / HYROX-style preparation
5. **Personal Training** - premium one-on-one coaching
6. **Small Group Training** - semi-private coaching for 2-4 people

Primary entry points:

- FIT45 waitlist
- FIT45 Free Trial
- URBN HYBRID Free Trial
- Free Assessment
- Pioneer sign-up before Grand Opening
- PFT as a free testing promo for HYBRID leads

Do not market URBN FIT primarily as hardcore CrossFit to beginners. Present URBN FIT as the main coached group training class for people who want to get stronger, fitter, safer, and more consistent.

FIT45 is separate from On-Ramp. FIT45 is a standalone beginner class. On-Ramp is the Foundation Course required before a client joins URBN FIT or URBN HYBRID.

Free Trial applies only to FIT45 and URBN HYBRID. It does not apply to URBN FIT.

Free Trial rules:

- 2 sessions only
- Must be used within 1 week
- Cannot be transferred
- Cannot be extended

## 11. Voice and Tone

URBN Athletics should sound like a professional coach speaking clearly to a busy adult.

The voice is:

- Professional
- Direct
- Supportive
- Practical
- Confident
- Coach-led
- Beginner-friendly
- Strong but not intimidating
- Premium but accessible

The voice is not:

- Hype-heavy
- Hardcore
- Intimidating
- Cheap promo-focused
- Overly motivational
- Random or chaotic

## Approved Tone Examples

Strong and clear:

> You do not need random workouts. You need a system you can follow.

Beginner-friendly:

> You do not need to be fit to start. You need the right place to begin.

Premium value:

> You are not paying for access. You are paying for coaching, structure, and accountability.

Practical:

> Train with purpose. Eat with awareness. Stay consistent.
