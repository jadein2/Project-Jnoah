# 9. Core Offer Structure

## Main Services

### 1. Group Training

Coach-led training sessions designed to improve strength, conditioning, movement quality, and consistency.

Best for:

- General fitness
- Fat loss
- Strength building
- Community support
- Members who want structure but do not need 1-on-1 coaching

### 2. Personal Training

Premium one-on-one coaching for clients who need a more focused program, closer supervision, and a stronger accountability system.

Best for:

- Beginners who need full guidance
- Clients with specific goals
- Body transformation
- Strength development
- Busy professionals
- Clients needing more attention

### 3. Small Group Training

Semi-private coaching for 2-4 people. Each client can still follow their own program while training in a small coached environment.

Best for:

- Friends or couples
- Clients who want coaching but not full 1-on-1 pricing
- People who prefer smaller groups
- Higher-touch coaching experience

### 4. On-Ramp Program

The On-Ramp program is the beginner onboarding pathway before regular training integration.

Purpose:

- Teach movement fundamentals
- Reduce intimidation
- Improve safety
- Set expectations
- Help beginners understand the coaching system
- Prepare members before joining group training
- Identify correct pathway: CrossFit / Hybrid / other appropriate track

### 5. Physical Fitness Test for Hybrid Track

The PFT is the entry filter for people who want to enter the Hybrid Track.

Purpose:

- Screen readiness
- Attract already-active individuals
- Create a performance-based campaign
- Separate beginners from fitness-background leads
- Protect training quality and safety

### 6. Nutrition Guidance

Practical nutrition support focused on:

- Protein intake
- Better food choices
- Portion control
- Meal consistency
- Calorie awareness
- Habit improvement
- Sustainable eating

### 7. Transformation Programs

Programs such as 6-week reset-style campaigns can be used as frontend offers to help people start with structure.

These programs combine:

- Training
- Nutrition guidance
- Accountability
- Progress tracking
- Habit building

### 8. Special Classes

Special classes can be used to create variety, target specific audience segments, and support retention.

Current listed special class:

- Booty Class

  - Equipment: Stepper

## Current Rule

**Lead with emotion first. Present price last.**

# 17. Monthly Member Assessment

## Assessment Rule

Monthly member assessment must be conducted strictly **once a month**.

## Assessment Owner

Assessment is performed by the assigned coach.

## Scheduling Rule

Assessment must be scheduled **one week in advance**.

## Purpose

- Reduce churn rate
- Track member progress
- Improve accountability
- Identify struggling members early
- Strengthen coach-member relationship
- Create an opportunity to adjust the plan

## Assessment Checklist

During the monthly assessment, assigned coach should review:

- Attendance
- Training consistency
- Body composition or bodyweight if applicable
- Goal progress
- Movement quality
- Strength or conditioning improvements
- Nutrition adherence
- Member concerns
- Next month focus

## Recommended Output

Each assessment should produce:

- Current status
- Main win
- Main struggle
- Coach recommendation
- Next 30-day focus
- Follow-up date
