# URBN Athletics Program Architecture and Offer Ladder

**Purpose:** Source-of-truth program structure for marketing, sales, onboarding, and creative handoff.  
**Last updated:** 2026-05-30  
**Brand rule:** Use URBN Athletics as the public brand. Use Urban Strong Fitness only as origin-story context.

---

## 1. Program Ladder

URBN Athletics should present its offers as a clear ladder:

1. **FIT45** - low-barrier beginner hook program
2. **On-Ramp / Foundation Course** - required bridge into URBN FIT or URBN HYBRID
3. **URBN FIT** - main coached group class
4. **URBN HYBRID** - endurance and strength program for fitness racing / HYROX-style preparation
5. **Personal Training** - premium one-on-one coaching
6. **Small Group Training** - semi-private coaching for 2-4 people

The public-facing logic:

> Start where you are. Build your foundation. Move into the right class.

---

## 2. FIT45

FIT45 is the low-barrier hook program for people who are new to fitness, returning after a long break, or intimidated by regular gym classes.

FIT45 is a 45-minute beginner-friendly fitness class designed for people who want to start moving, build confidence, and create a sustainable exercise habit. Sessions use simple, scalable, low-impact strength and conditioning exercises so beginners can improve fitness gradually without feeling overwhelmed.

Short marketing description:

> FIT45 helps beginners start an active lifestyle through simple, low-impact, coach-led workouts built for daily movement, confidence, and consistency.

Best for:

- People new to fitness
- People returning after a long break
- People who feel intimidated by regular group classes
- People who need low-impact exercise
- People who want sustainable daily movement
- People who need a simple starting point before bigger training goals

Price:

- FIT45 Monthly: PHP 2,500

Final FIT45 days:

- Monday to Friday

Tentative class time options:

- 6:30 AM
- 3:15 PM

Upgrade path:

- FIT45 clients can move up to URBN FIT when they are ready.
- FIT45 clients must complete On-Ramp / Foundation Course first before joining URBN FIT.

---

## 3. On-Ramp / Foundation Course

On-Ramp is the required Foundation Course before a client joins URBN FIT or URBN HYBRID.

FIT45 is a standalone beginner class. On-Ramp is the bridge into higher-skill coached classes.

Rule:

- Clients must finish the required Foundation Course before joining or merging into URBN FIT or URBN HYBRID.

## Path 1: 1-on-1 On-Ramp

Structure:

- 8 total sessions
- First 4 sessions: coached 1-on-1 Foundation Course
- Remaining 4 sessions: transition / merging sessions into group class

Price:

- Specific Coach + Specific Time Slot On-Ramp: PHP 4,500

Best for:

- Clients who need more attention
- Clients who prefer private coaching
- Clients with schedule constraints
- Clients who want a specific coach or specific time slot
- Clients who need extra confidence before joining class

## Path 2: Group On-Ramp

Rule:

- Clients must complete 4 Foundation Course sessions before joining or merging into any class.

Schedule:

- Tuesday 6:00 PM
- Thursday 6:00 PM
- Saturday 10:00 AM

Price:

- Group Class On-Ramp: PHP 3,000

Best for:

- Clients who can follow the set schedule
- Clients ready to learn in a small group setting
- Clients who want a lower-cost bridge into URBN FIT or URBN HYBRID

---

## 4. URBN FIT

URBN FIT is the main coached group training class. It is the evolution of the previous Urban Fit / group class concept under the URBN Athletics brand.

Description:

> URBN FIT is the main coached group training program for members who want structured strength, conditioning, movement quality, and consistency in a class environment. It is built for people who want more than random workouts and need coaching, accountability, and progression.

Entry rule:

- Clients must complete On-Ramp / Foundation Course before joining URBN FIT unless ownership later approves a formal coach assessment exception.

Pricing:

- Regular Monthly: PHP 4,500
- Regular Annual: PHP 35,000
- Walk-In / Per Session: PHP 500
- Student Monthly: PHP 3,000
- Student Daily: PHP 400

Pioneer / comeback offer:

- Pioneer Monthly: PHP 3,000
- Pioneer Annual: PHP 30,000
- Old-member comeback offer: Pioneer Price
- Pioneer sign-up urgency should point toward Grand Opening.

---

## 5. URBN HYBRID

URBN HYBRID is the endurance and strength program for fitness racing, HYROX-style preparation, and higher-demand conditioning.

Description:

> URBN HYBRID combines endurance and strength training for people who want to prepare for fitness racing, HYROX-style events, or a more performance-driven training environment.

Use:

- URBN HYBRID
- Hybrid training
- Fitness racing preparation
- HYROX-style preparation

Avoid implying official HYROX affiliation unless the gym has official rights or partnership.

Price:

- URBN HYBRID Monthly: PHP 3,000

Schedule:

- 6:15 PM

PFT role:

- PFT is a free testing promo to generate leads for URBN HYBRID.
- It should be marketed as: "Think you are ready for HYBRID? Test your fitness first."

## 6. Free Trial Rules

Free Trial applies only to:

- FIT45
- URBN HYBRID

Free Trial does not apply to:

- URBN FIT

Rules:

- 2 sessions only
- Must be used within 1 week
- Cannot be transferred
- Cannot be extended
