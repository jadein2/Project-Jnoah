## 5. Operating Rules

### Response Time
Messages are answered within 24 hours on business days during coaching office hours.

### Weekly Check-Ins
Clients must submit one check-in per week. The assigned check-in day may vary by start date or program schedule.

### Missed Check-Ins
1 missed check-in: reminder. 2 missed: accountability message. 3 missed: review call or compliance warning. Ongoing missed check-ins: program continues but adjustments may be limited.

### Refund Policy
Payments are non-refundable once coaching has started, since program design, onboarding, and coaching resources are already allocated.

### Pause / Freeze Policy
Online programs cannot be paused or frozen once the cycle starts.

### Cancellation Policy
Clients may cancel before the next billing cycle, but no cancellation is allowed inside an active paid program block.

### Currency Display
Philippines pages use PHP primary with USD reference. International pages use USD primary with PHP reference.
