## THE RESTART FITNESS SYSTEM

The operating product behind all offers. Everything Restart Fitness PH sells runs through this system.

### Four Pillars

| Pillar | What It Does | Client Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Training | Structured programming, movement, progression | Client knows what to do and how to progress |
| Nutrition | Practical, real-life nutrition guidance | Client eats with structure, not confusion |
| Habits | Simple repeatable behavior targets | Client builds consistency without willpower |
| Accountability | Check-ins, feedback, standards, adjustments | Client stays on track and corrects early |

### Four-Step Process

| Step | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Clarity | Understand goal, baseline, schedule, limits, starting point |
| Structure | Build training, nutrition, habit, and check-in plan |
| Coaching | Guide execution, correct mistakes, teach the plan |
| Accountability | Review, adjust, reinforce, repeat |

### Weekly Coaching Loop

Every active client moves through this every week:

1. Follow assigned training plan
2. Complete nutrition and habit actions
3. Submit training videos when required
4. Complete weekly check-in
5. Coach reviews progress
6. Coach gives feedback and adjustments
7. Client follows next week's action plan
