### What We Do

Restart Fitness PH is a structured coaching business that helps people achieve lasting health and fitness results through The Restart Fitness System — a repeatable coaching process built on training, nutrition, habits, and accountability.

We do not sell gym access, random workout plans, or hype-based transformation promises. We sell a system. The system is what makes the business defensible, scalable, and different from every other fitness option available to our market.

### The Opportunity

The online fitness coaching market in the Philippines and globally is in active growth. The market no longer lacks workout content — it lacks coaching. Anyone can find a free workout on YouTube or Instagram. What people cannot find easily is a structured, human-led coaching system that builds real consistency, corrects mistakes, tracks progress, and stays with them through the hard weeks.

That gap is the Restart Fitness PH opportunity.

### Why This Works

Restart Fitness PH is built on three competitive advantages:

1. **The system itself** — not a personality brand, but a repeatable coaching process that can be taught to other coaches.
2. **High-care standards** — video feedback, weekly check-ins, real nutrition guidance, and accountability that most online coaches do not provide.
3. **Brand clarity** — a specific, premium voice and visual identity that builds trust in a market full of generic fitness content.

### Summary Goals for 2026

- Lock and launch the full offer ladder: 6FIX, Restart-P, 90-Day Coaching System, Restart-Base, Restart-Perform
- Reach ₱200,000/month in revenue within 6 months
- Build fulfillment SOPs ready for a second coach
- Hire an Assistant Coach within 6–12 months

## 4. MARKET ANALYSIS

### 4.1 Industry Overview

The global fitness app market was estimated at USD 12.12 billion in 2025 and is projected to reach USD 33.58 billion by 2033, growing at 13.40% CAGR from 2026 to 2033 (Grand View Research). Persistence Market Research projects similar growth with Asia-Pacific identified as the fastest-growing region due to rising health awareness, mobile adoption, and digital wellness growth.

For Restart Fitness PH, this is not primarily an "app market" opportunity — it is a coaching market opportunity. People are not just looking for apps. They are looking for coaching, guidance, and accountability that apps alone cannot provide.

### 4.2 Key Market Trends

| Trend | Meaning for Restart Fitness PH |
|---|---|
| Hybrid coaching dominance | Build systems that work online, in-person, and mixed |
| AI-assisted coaching workflows | Use AI for admin, drafts, program templates, content, reports |
| Wearable data integration | Future: add sleep, steps, HR, habit data into check-ins |
| Longevity and long-term health | Build Restart-Base around strength, health, and function |
| Performance personalization | Build Restart-Perform for athletes and serious clients |
| Habit-based coaching | Keep 6FIX and 90-Day Coaching System centered on behavior, not workouts |
| Corporate wellness | Future B2B offer for teams and companies |
| OFW / overseas Filipino market | Strong next-phase opportunity for remote Filipino coaching |

According to ABC Trainerize's 2026 industry report, approximately 48% of trainers now report hybrid as their primary delivery model, and 64% use automation or AI to handle backend work while protecting the human premium of coaching. This directly validates Restart Fitness PH's direction.

### 4.3 Philippines Market Context

The Philippines has a growing middle class, increasing smartphone and internet penetration, and rising lifestyle disease awareness. Filipinos are actively seeking fitness guidance but remain underserved by structured, premium online coaching — the market is dominated by low-quality gym memberships, influencer-style programs, and unstructured personal training.

This creates a clear opening for a structured, professional, system-based coaching offer.

### 4.4 Target Market Profile

**Primary: Philippines Professionals and Adults (25–45)**

- Have tried fitness before but struggled with consistency
- Want structured guidance, not random workouts
- Willing to invest in quality coaching that delivers results
- Have access to a gym, home gym, or open space
- Motivated by long-term health, not just appearance
- Value a coach who explains the process and holds them accountable

**Secondary: International / OFW**

- Overseas Filipino Workers with income and flexibility to invest in coaching
- Want Filipino-led coaching delivered in English
- Have access to commercial gyms overseas
- Miss the human connection of local coaching

**Not our market:**
- Price hunters looking for the cheapest option
- People who want shortcuts or fast fixes
- People who refuse coaching standards or accountability
- People who want random workouts with no system

### 4.5 Competitive Landscape

| Competitor Type | What They Offer | Why Clients Choose Restart Fitness PH Instead |
|---|---|---|
| Cheap gyms (₱800–₱1,500/mo) | Facility access | No guidance, no structure, no accountability |

**Restart Fitness PH's competitive advantage:** We are the coaching layer. We are not competing with apps, gym access, or free content. We are for people who have already realized that those things do not produce lasting results.

## 5. PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

### The Restart Fitness System — Core Product

All offers are expressions of The Restart Fitness System. The four pillars and the four-step process are built into every program at the appropriate depth for each offer level.

### 5.1 6FIX — 6-Week Foundation Program

**Role:** Entry point. Low barrier. Foundation-builder.  
**Best for:** Beginners, returning clients, people overwhelmed by fitness.  
**Duration:** 6 weeks  
**Price:** ₱4,500 / $80 reference  
**What it does:** Introduces the client to all four pillars of The Restart Fitness System at a beginner-accessible level. Builds the first layer of structure in training, practical nutrition, habits, and accountability.  
**Inclusions:** Weekly check-ins, key video feedback, practical nutrition guidance, habit targets, in-app messaging, accountability.  
**Funnel role:** Graduates move into Restart-P, 90-Day Coaching System, Restart-Base, or Restart-Perform.  
**Positioning line:** Start with structure before chasing intensity.

**Versions:**
- Program-only: App-based 6-week plan, weekly check-in, tasks, training structure
- Online coaching: Higher support with check-ins, messaging, and more feedback

### 5.2 Restart-P — Program-Only Access (Flagship Scalable Offer)

**Role:** Flagship recurring offer. Most scalable.  
**Best for:** People who can train independently but need structure, coaching support, video feedback, nutrition guidance, and accountability.  
**Duration:** Monthly subscription  
**Price:** P12 ₱2,500/mo | P16 ₱3,000/mo | P20 ₱3,500/mo  
**USD reference:** $45 / $55 / $65  
**What it does:** Delivers personalized programming with full coaching feedback — not just a workout plan. The coaching loop (onboarding, check-in, video review, nutrition guidance, adjustment) is what separates this from every cheap program on the market.  
**Inclusions:** Onboarding call, weekly check-in, nutrition guidance, video feedback every session, in-app messaging, program adjustments.  
**Video rule:** Required every session.  
**Positioning line:** Train independently. Stay coached.

### 5.3 90-Day Coaching System — 90-Day Full Transformation Package (Premium Core Offer)

**Role:** Main transformation offer. Highest value per client.  
**Best for:** Clients who need the full system: training, practical nutrition, habit coaching, accountability, group support, and progress review.  
**Duration:** 90 days  
**Price:** ₱18,000 upfront / ₱6,500/month × 3  
**USD reference:** $320  
**What it does:** The most complete coaching experience. Delivers all four pillars over 90 days with full check-ins, video feedback, group coaching calls, phase workshops, and a final progress review.  
**Inclusions:** Full onboarding assessment, training program, nutrition structure, habit targets, weekly check-ins (written + voice/video support), video feedback, group coaching calls, phase workshops, monthly progress review, in-app messaging, adjustments every cycle.  
**Positioning line:** A complete 90-day coaching system.

### 5.4 Restart-Base — Strength and Longevity Program

**Role:** Long-term health and strength offer.  
**Best for:** People who want to build strength, move better, improve capacity, and train for long-term health. Not chasing transformation claims.  
**Duration:** Monthly subscription; 12 weeks recommended  
**Price:** ₱4,200/month / $75/month  
**What it does:** Strength training designed for real life, with movement quality, mobility, recovery, and sustainable progression built in.  
**Inclusions:** Strength program, movement quality focus, mobility and recovery guidance, weekly check-ins, nutrition basics, habit targets, key lift video feedback, progress tracking.  
**Positioning line:** Build strength for the life you actually live.

### 5.5 Restart-Perform — Performance and Athlete Program (Premium Specialized)

**Role:** Specialized performance offer.  
**Best for:** Athletes, CrossFit-style clients, hybrid athletes, Olympic weightlifting clients, and serious trainees with measurable performance goals.  
**Duration:** Monthly or event-based cycle  
**Price:** ₱5,500/month / $95/month  
**What it does:** Structured performance coaching with required video feedback, phase-based programming, strength and conditioning progression, and event/competition preparation.  
**Inclusions:** Performance goal review, movement and capacity assessment, training program by phase, strength and conditioning progression, required video feedback every session, weekly check-ins, performance metrics, recovery notes, event/competition prep if applicable.  
**Positioning line:** Performance training with structure and feedback.

## 7. MARKETING AND SALES STRATEGY

### 7.1 Core Philosophy

**Sell the practice, not just the outcome.**

Restart Fitness PH should not only post transformation promises. It should show the process behind transformation — the check-ins, the adjustments, the habit targets, the training consistency. This builds trust, not just desire.

### 7.2 Content Pillars

| Pillar | Purpose | Example Topics |
|---|---|---|
| Proof of Practice | Show real client work and process | Check-in screenshots, habit completions, training logs |
| System Education | Explain The Restart Fitness System | Four pillars, coaching loop, what accountability really means |
| Habits + Behavior Coaching | Teach consistency and behavior | How to build a training habit, why motivation fails, habit stacking |
| Training Clarity + Progression | Show smart, progressive training | How to warm up, what progressive overload looks like |
| Nutrition Clarity + Execution | Make nutrition practical | Real meal examples, portion basics, eating at work |
| Accountability Culture | Show the standards | Client check-ins, coach feedback culture, what we expect |
| FAQs + Objections | Remove buying resistance | "Is this just a workout plan?", "What if I miss a session?" |

### 7.3 Minimum Weekly Content Schedule

| Content Type | Frequency |
|---|---|
| Education posts (carousels or single) | 2x per week |
| Proof posts (client results, process) | 1x per week |
| Habit or behavior post | 1x per week |
| CTA or offer post | 1x per week |
| Stories (daily activity, tips, polls) | Daily |
| Reels (short-form video) | 2–3x per week (target) |

### 7.4 Best-Performing Content Angles

| Angle | Message Direction |
|---|---|
| Stop program hopping | You do not need another random plan. You need a system you can follow long enough to measure. |
| Accountability over motivation | Motivation starts the work. Accountability keeps the work honest. |
| Practical nutrition | You do not need a perfect diet. You need repeatable meals, enough protein, and a plan you can adjust. |
| Coaching vs. access | Access gives you a place to train. Coaching gives you direction. |
| Results that stick | The goal is not just progress. The goal is progress you can keep. |
| Autonomy | Good coaching should eventually make you more capable, not more dependent. |

### 7.5 Sales Funnel

| Stage | What Happens | System |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness | Client sees post, reel, story, or testimonial | Content engine |
| Interest | Client saves post, follows, watches stories, DMs | Social CTA: DM "RESTART" |
| Qualification | Client answers 5 DM questions | DM script |
| Trust | Client views proof, service page, system explanation | Website + pinned posts + highlights |
| Conversion | Client books discovery call | Booking link in bio / direct DM |
| Enrollment | Client pays and completes onboarding | Payment + intake workflow |
| Coaching | Client follows program, submits check-ins and videos | Trainerize + coaching loop |
| Retention | Client gets results, receives monthly review | Monthly review + renewal script |
| Referral | Client shares results and refers others | Testimonial + referral ask |

### 7.6 DM Qualification Questions

1. What is your main goal right now?
2. Are you training at home, in a gym, or not training yet?
3. What is your biggest struggle: training, nutrition, consistency, or accountability?
4. How many days per week can you realistically train?
5. Are you looking for program-only access or full coaching support?

### 7.7 Discovery Call Structure

1. Build rapport (2–3 min)
2. Understand their situation and current fitness history (5–7 min)
3. Identify the main obstacle and pain point (3–5 min)
4. Explain The Restart Fitness System and how it solves their specific problem (5 min)
5. Match them to the right offer (3 min)
6. Present price and inclusions (2 min)
7. Handle objections (3–5 min)
8. Close: payment link or next step (2 min)

### 7.8 Content-to-Funnel Rule

Every piece of content should lead to one of these actions:
- DM "RESTART"
- Book a Discovery Call
- Save this post and use it this week
- Reply with your goal
- Visit the service page
