## 03 — HOW WE SOLVE IT

### The Restart Fitness System

A repeatable coaching process built on four pillars — and a weekly loop that keeps every client on track through clarity, structure, coaching, and accountability.

### Four Pillars

| Pillar | What It Does | Client Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| **Training** | Structured programming, movement quality, and progression that makes sense for real life. | Client knows what to do and how to progress. |
| **Nutrition** | Practical, real-food guidance. No extreme diets. No calorie obsession. | Client eats with structure, not confusion. |
| **Habits** | Simple, repeatable behavior targets that build consistency without willpower. | Client builds consistency that lasts. |
| **Accountability** | Weekly check-ins, video feedback, standards, and real adjustments from a coach. | Client stays on track and corrects early. |

### The Four-Step Process

| Step | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **01 — Clarity** | Understand goal, baseline, schedule, and starting point. |
| **02 — Structure** | Build training, nutrition, habit, and check-in plan. |
| **03 — Coaching** | Guide execution, correct mistakes, teach the plan. |
| **04 — Accountability** | Review, adjust, reinforce, and repeat. |

### The Weekly Coaching Loop

Every active client follows this sequence every week — no exceptions, no guessing.

1. Follow the assigned training plan
2. Complete nutrition and habit actions
3. Submit training videos when required
4. Complete the weekly check-in
5. Coach reviews progress
6. Coach gives feedback and adjustments
7. Client follows next week's action plan

**The Standard:** High-care. High-standard. Responsive and structured. Real feedback, not automated replies. Adjustments based on what actually happened that week. Clients are not left to guess. They are coached through it.
