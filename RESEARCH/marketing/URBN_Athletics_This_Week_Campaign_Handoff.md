## 3. Strategic Shift

### Previous Message

> Join our Free Trial.

### New Message

> Book a Free Consultation so we can help you start with the right program.

### Why This Matters

URBN Athletics is not positioned as a basic access gym or random class provider.

The brand is built around:

- Coaching
- Structure
- Movement quality
- Accountability
- Program recommendation
- Better starting point for each client

The Free Consultation supports this positioning better because it helps filter and guide leads before they enter a program.

---

## 4. Campaign Objective

The objective of this campaign is to announce what members and prospects can expect this week while shifting the public CTA toward a consultation-first experience.

### Primary Goal

Drive DM inquiries for Free Consultation bookings.

### Secondary Goals

- Announce FIT45 morning schedule expansion.
- Explain the program paths: FIT45, URBN Hybrid, URBN FIT.
- Communicate that current URBN FIT members can also join FIT45 and do the WOD.
- Promote the Soft Opening offer without putting prices on public assets.
- Reinforce URBN Athletics as a coached, structured training environment.

# 14. DM Inquiry Flow

Use this when someone sends a message after seeing the post.

## DM Script — First Reply

```text
Hi! Thanks for messaging URBN Athletics.

Before we recommend a program, may we ask what your main goal is right now?

Is it fat loss, strength, conditioning, health, or getting consistent again?
```

## Follow-Up Questions

Ask these before giving price:

1. What is your main goal right now?
2. Are you a beginner, returnee, or currently training?
3. What schedule works best for you?
4. Are you looking for fat loss, strength, conditioning, or general structure?
5. Do you prefer morning, afternoon, or evening training?
6. Would you like to book your Free Consultation this week?

## DM Script — Consultation Invite

```text
Based on your goal, the best first step is to book a Free Consultation.

A coach will help you choose the right program based on your current level, schedule, and goal.

Would you like to reserve a consultation slot this week?
```

## DM Script — If They Ask Price Immediately

```text
We can send the pricing details, but we recommend starting with a Free Consultation first so we can guide you to the right program.

That way, you don’t pay for a plan that doesn’t match your goal or schedule.

For this week, if you start with any plan, your first week is free.
```

## DM Script — Closing

```text
Your next step is to book your Free Consultation.

Please send:
Full name:
Contact number:
Preferred schedule:
Main goal:

We’ll help you choose the right starting program.
```

- All discount details

# 17. Design Direction

## Visual Mood

- Community announcement
- Clean and readable
- Not too sales-heavy
- Professional but approachable
- Coach-led, not hype-based

Use gym/community photos if available:

- Keep slide text short.
- Use one main idea per slide.

- Use CTA clearly at the end.
